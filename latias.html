What is Latias?

Latias is a Dragon/Psychic type in Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Latias, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Latias be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Latias?

Best moveset of Latias are Charm which does damage of 20 under Fast Attack and Outrage of 110 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Latias?

Fog weather to boost Dark and Ghost type counters, Cloudy weather to boosts Fairy types counters, Snow weather to boosts Ice types counters.
