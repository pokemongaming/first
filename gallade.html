What is Gallade?

Gallade is a Psychic/Fighting type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Gallade, its competitive strengths, top counters.

Can Mega Gallade be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Gallade?

Best movset of Gallade are Charm which does damage of 20 under Fast Attack and Close Combat of 105 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.
