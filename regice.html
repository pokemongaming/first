What is Regice?

Regice is a Ice type in 5 Star Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Regice, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Regice be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Shiny Regice is available during specific events and raid rotations.

What are best moveset of Regice?

Best movset of Regice are Rock Smash which does damage of 17 under Fast Attack and Focus Blast or Earthquake of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

Is Regice good in PvP?

Regice has niche usage in Ultra League for its bulk and unique charge moves but struggles in Master League due to its low Attack stat.

What is the best weather to raid Regice?

Cloudy weather to boosts Fighting-type counters, Sunny weather to boosts Fire-type counters, Snowy weather to boosts Steel counters and Regice’s Ice moves
