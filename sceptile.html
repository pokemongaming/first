What is Sceptile?

Sceptile is a Grass type in 5 Star Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Sceptile, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Sceptile be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Sceptile has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Sceptile?

Best moveset of Sceptile are Bullet Seed which does damage of 7 under Fast Attack and Earthquake of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Sceptile?

Snow weather to boosts Ice types counters.
