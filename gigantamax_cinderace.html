About Gigantamax Cinderace

Gigantamax Cinderace is a Fire-type raid boss with blistering attack power and unique G-Max moves. It is weak to Water, Rock, and Ground attacks, making it easier to counter if you bring strong bulky Water types or high DPS Rock attackers. This guide explains the best counters, movesets, weather boosts, and catch tips.

Moves & Threats

Cinderace common fast moves are Tackle or Fire Spin. Charged moves include Focus Blast or Flamethrower and Flame Charge.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Boosts Fire damage.
  • Rain: Boosts Water attackers.

    • Water-types resist Fire moves, making them the safest option overall. Rock types also hit hard but need to watch for Fighting coverage.

    Battle Tips

    • Front-load high-DPS Water/Ground/Rock counters to shrink HP quickly.
    • Use a high-TDO tank if the lobby is large and you expect heavy Max Moves.
    • Coordinate Mega leads and avoid wasting revives mid-fight — revive between attempts if needed.
    • Quick-dodge only large Max Moves that will one-shot your counters; otherwise keep DPS uptime.

    Team Size

    15-20 trainers are required.

    Recommended items

    • Max Revives
    • Max Potions
    • Razz / Golden Razz for catch

    When To Avoid

    Avoid Grass and Steel types — Grass burns quickly, and Steel resists Fire poorly. Stick to bulky Water or strong Rock/Ground attackers.

    Raid Strategy & Catch Tips

    Lead with your tankiest Water attackers to survive longer. Shield your best counters if Cinderace runs Low Kick for coverage. After the raid, use Golden Razz and curve throws to maximize catch odds.

    FAQ

    What is Gigantamax Cinderace type and weakness?

    Cinderace is a Fire type. It is weak to Water, Rock, Ground type moves.

    What are good counters for G-Max Fireball?

    Strong ground and water attackers such as Gigantamax Charizard, Gigantamax Lapras, Gigantamax Snorlax, Gigantamax Rillaboom, Dynamax Charizard, Dynamax Toxtricity.

    Can Gigantamax Cinderace be shiny?

    Yes, but in certain events.

    Is Gigantamax Cinderace catchable after the raid?

    Yes — standard raid catch mechanics apply.

    Does weather change move sets?

    Sunny boosts Fire moves, Rain boosts Water counters.
