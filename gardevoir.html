What is Gardevoir?

Gardevoir is a Psychic/Fairy type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Gardevoir, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Gardevoir be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Gardevoir?

Best movset of Gardevoir are Charm which does damage of 20 under Fast Attack and Shadow Ball or Dazzling Gleam of 100 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Gardevoir?

Cloudy weather to boosts Poison and Fairy type counters, Fog weather to boosts Ghost type counters.
