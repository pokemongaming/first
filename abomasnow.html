Moves & Threats

Abomasnow common fast moves are Razor Leaf or Leafage or Powder Snow. Charged moves include Blizzard or Outrage or Energy Ball or Icy Wind and Weather Ball

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,148-1,349 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 1,436-1,687 CP
  • Boosted by: Snow & Sunny

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Buffs your boosts Fire damage.
  • Cloudy: Buffs your Fighting attackers.

    FAQ

    Can Mega Abomasnow be shiny in Pokémon GO?

    Not currently. Shiny Abomasnow has not yet been released in raids or events.

    What are best moveset of Abomasnow?

    Best moveset of Abomasnow are Razor Leaf which does damage of 13 under Fast Attack and Blizzard of 130 under Charged Attack.

    Are Shadow Pokémon better?

    Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

    What is the best weather to raid Mega Abomasnow?

    Sunny or Cloudy weather to boosts Fire and Fighting type counters.
