About Gigantamax Alcremie

Gigantamax Alcremie is a Fairy-type Gigantamax raid boss featured in special Pokémon GO events. Known for its towering cake-like form, it offers unique visuals and limited-time rewards. As a Fairy type, it is weak to Poison and Steel, making strong attackers from those types the best counters.

Moves & Threats

Alcremie common fast moves are Tackle or Charm. Charged moves include Psyshock or Dazzling Gleam and Energy Ball.

Weather Effects

  • Rainy: Boosts Poison moves.
  • Cloudy: Boosts Fairy attackers.
  • Snow: Boosts Steel attackers.

    Raid Strategy & Catch Tips

    Bring top-tier Steel and Poison Pokémon to maximize damage output. Metagross with Meteor Mash is the most reliable counter. Avoid bringing Dragon, Dark, or Fighting types, as they are vulnerable to Fairy attacks. A small group of high-level trainers can clear the raid with optimal counters.

    FAQ

    What is Gigantamax Alcremie type and weakness?

    Alcremie is a Fairy type. It is weak to Poison, Steel.

    What are good counters for G-Max Cannonde?

    Strong poison and steel attackers such as Zacian, Zamazenta, Eternatus, Dynamax Metagross, Dynamax Corviknight.

    Can Gigantamax Alcremie be shiny?

    Yes, but in certain events.

    Is Gigantamax Alcremie catchable after the raid?

    Yes — standard raid catch mechanics apply.

    Does weather change move sets?

    Rainy weather boosts Poison moves, Cloudy weather boosts Fairy attackers, Snow weather boosts Steel attackers.

    Does Alcremie keep G-Max ?

    No — it’s exclusive to Gigantamax raids.

    Is Gigantamax Alcremie good in battles?

    Gigantamax Alcremie is mostly a collector’s raid boss. While Fairy typing is useful, it doesn’t outperform top Fairy attackers.
