❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Lopunny be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Lopunny?

Best moveset of Lopunny are Double Kick which does damage of 10 under Fast Attack and Hyper Beam of 150 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Lopunny?

Cloudy weather to boosts Fairy and Fighting type counters, Windy weather to boosts Psychic type counters.
