What is Azelf?

Azelf is a Psychic type in 5 Star Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Azelf, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Azelf be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Shiny Azelf is available during certain raid rotations and events.

What are best moveset of Azelf?

Best movset of Azelf are Confusion which does damage of 19 under Fast Attack and Fire Blast of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

Is Azelf good in PvP?

Azelf has high Attack but lacks bulk and coverage, making it rarely used in PvP. It is better suited as a raid attacker.

What is the best weather to raid Azelf?

Windy weather to buffs Azelf’s Psychic moves, Foggy weather to boosts Ghost and Dark counters
