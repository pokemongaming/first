Gigantamax Machamp Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Machamp is a Fighting-type raid boss with strong physical attacks. With the right counters, this raid can be cleared quickly and safely.

Best Counters

  • Psychic Types – Mewtwo, Espeon, Alakazam
  • Fairy Types – Togekiss, Gardevoir, Sylveon
  • Flying Types – Staraptor, Honchkrow, Rayquaza

Recommended Moves

  • Mewtwo: Confusion + Psystrike
  • Togekiss: Charm + Dazzling Gleam
  • Rayquaza: Air Slash + Hurricane

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Cloudy weather (Fighting)
Best for you: Windy (boosts Psychic/Flying).

Team Suggestions

  • Psychic types are the strongest and safest option.
  • Bring at least 4–6 high-level Psychic/Fairy Pokémon.

Rewards

XL Candy, bonus items, Charged TMs, and a Machamp encounter.
