Introduction

Entei is one of the iconic Fire-type Legendary Pokémon in Pokémon GO, known for its powerful Fire moves and high stamina. Its presence in raids makes it a sought-after target for players seeking Legendary Pokémon with strong stats.

While Entei’s Fire typing makes it strong against Grass, Bug, Ice, and Steel Pokémon, it has clear weaknesses to Water, Rock, and Ground attacks. By selecting the right counters, trainers can defeat Entei efficiently, even in smaller groups with high-level Pokémon.

This guide will provide detailed information about Entei’s moves, raid strategies, catch CP, shiny form, and the best Pokémon to bring for maximum efficiency in raids.

🧬 About Entei in Pokémon GO

Entei is a pure Fire-type Legendary Pokémon from the Johto region. It is known for its high Attack stat and is a very good Fire-type attacker when powered up.

Entei is useful for:

Weaknesses & resistances

CategoryDetails
❌ Weak To: Water • Rock • Ground

Notes Entei is weak to Water, Rock, and Ground-type moves, making these the best choices to deal significant damage quickly. Its Fire typing resists Grass, Bug, Ice, and Fairy moves, so using the wrong counters can lengthen the fight unnecessarily. Trainers should pick durable attackers to withstand Entei’s powerful Fire moves.

Difficulty

Entei hits hard but has lower HP than tanky Legendaries, meaning the challenge is maximizing damage before it can defeat your team. Small groups need precise counters and shields, while larger groups can brute-force it—but efficiency still comes from choosing the right type advantage.

🌀 Entei Moveset

Entei common fast moves are Fire Spin or Fire Fang. Charged moves include Scorching Sands or Iron Head or Flamethrower or Overheat or Flame Charge and Fire Blast

Most dangerous moveset: Fire Spin + Overheat

🎯 Catch CP

  • Perfect (L20): 1,900-1,984 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,375-2,480 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Entei gets a weather boost in:

  • Sunny weather (boosts Fire)

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Rainy → Water-types
  • Partly Cloudy → Rock-types
  • Sunny → Ground-types

Shiny Entei in Pokémon GO

Yes! Trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Entei after completing an Entei raid. While Mega or raid forms themselves cannot be caught, the standard Entei will appear after the battle.

Shiny Entei features a slightly darker mane and body tone, making it visually distinct and highly sought after by collectors in Pokémon GO.

FAQ

Can Entei be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Shiny Entei is available in Pokémon GO. Its shiny form has a brownish-red body with darker fur.

What is the best moveset of Entei?

The best moveset of Entei are Fire Spin or Fire Fang which does damage of 13 under Fast Attack and Overheat of 160 under Charged Attack.

How many trainers are needed to defeat Entei?

With strong counters, 2–4 experienced trainers can defeat Entei. Newer players may prefer 4–6 trainers for a safer raid.

Is Entei a difficult raid boss?

Entei has strong attack power but average defenses. Using Water or Rock-type counters makes this raid relatively manageable.
