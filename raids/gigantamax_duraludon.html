Gigantamax Duraludon Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Duraludon is a Steel/Dragon-type raid boss with strong defensive stats. Use Ground, Fighting, or strong Dragon attackers for maximum effectiveness.

Best Counters

  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Groudon, Excadrill
  • Fighting Types – Lucario, Conkeldurr, Machamp
  • Dragon Types – Rayquaza, Palkia, Zekrom

Recommended Moves

  • Garchomp: Mud Shot + Earth Power
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere
  • Rayquaza: Dragon Tail + Outrage

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Snow (Steel), Windy (Dragon)
Best for you: Clear weather for Ground types.

Team Suggestions

  • Ground attackers offer consistent fast clears.
  • Fighting types add bulk and reliability.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, and Duraludon capture chances.
