Registeel Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Registeel is a Steel-type Legendary Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. With its high defense and unique Steel typing, it can be tough to defeat without the right counters. Fortunately, Fire-, Fighting-, and Ground-type Pokémon are very effective against it.

Introduction

Registeel is one of the Legendary Steel-type Pokémon in Pokémon GO raids. Known for its incredibly high defense and relatively slow attack speed, it can be a challenging opponent. Many trainers struggle if they use Pokémon that don’t exploit its weaknesses.

This guide will explain which types of counters and movesets are most effective, how CP and IV influence the battle, and the best strategies for small or large groups. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced trainer, this guide will help you defeat Registeel quickly and efficiently.

Overview

Registeel is a pure Steel-type raid boss known for exceptional bulk and reliable Steel coverage. Its role as a raid boss is simple: outlast opponents with high defenses while dealing steady Steel damage. Registeel is not explosively offensive — the win condition is sustained DPS that exploits its limited weaknesses.

Quick takeaway: Fire, Fighting and Ground types are your best choices. Avoid using Steel, Ice or Dragon types as primaries; they deal minimal damage and waste raid time.

Moves & Threats

Registeel common fast moves are Lock On or Rock Smash or Metal Claw. Charged moves include Hyper Beam or Focus Blast and Flash Cannon. Elite moves include Zap Cannon.

Focus Blast is the most dangerous charged move due to its high damage output, especially against non-Fighting counters. Flash Cannon is slower but still threatening. Timing dodges can help reduce damage, especially in smaller raid groups.

Most dangerous combos: Rock Smash (fast) + Focus Blast — this pairing hits hard and charges quickly. Dodging charged moves where possible improves survivability.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,326-1,398 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 1,658-1.748 CP
  • Boosted by: Snowy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Best Team Composition

A strong Registeel raid team should include:

  • 3–4 Fire-type attackers for maximum DPS
  • 1–2 Fighting or Ground-type attackers for coverage
  • 1 Mega Pokémon for extra damage boost

Using Mega Charizard Y or Chandelure will speed up the raid significantly.

Weather Boost

Registeel is boosted in Sunny and Windy weather, increasing its attack power.

Your counters are boosted in:

  • Sunny weather (Fire)
  • Windy weather (Fighting)
  • Rainy weather (Ground)

Is Registeel Worth Raiding?

Registeel is a strong Steel-type for PvP and can be used for defensive and offensive purposes. It is also a key Pokémon for completing the Legendary Pokémon collection.

Shiny Registeel in Pokémon GO

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Registeel after defeating it in a Legendary Raid. Shiny Registeel has a slightly darker metallic appearance compared to the regular version, making it highly sought after by collectors.

While Shiny Registeel cannot appear in the raid itself, the Pokémon you catch after completing the raid has a chance of being shiny. Regular participation in Legendary Raids increases the odds of obtaining a shiny Registeel.
