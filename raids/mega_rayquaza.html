Introduction

Mega Rayquaza is one of the most iconic and challenging Mega Raids in Pokémon GO. As a Dragon and Flying-type Pokémon, it can hit hard with moves like Dragon Ascent and Outrage. Its incredible stats make it a formidable opponent in raids.

However, Mega Rayquaza also has notable weaknesses to Ice, Dragon, Fairy, and Rock-type moves. Using high-level counters that exploit these weaknesses allows trainers to defeat it efficiently and collect valuable rewards.

Whether you are a beginner preparing your first Mega Raid or an experienced trainer optimizing your counters, this guide will give you everything you need to win against Mega Rayquaza.

Mega Rayquaza is a Dragon / Flying-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes the strongest Dragon-type attacker in Pokémon GO and also boosts Dragon-type, Flying-type, and Psychic-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Rayquaza is extremely valuable because:

🌀 Mega Rayquaza Moveset

Rayquaza common fast moves are Air Slash or Dragon Tail. Charged moves include Aerial Ace or Dragon Ascent or Ancient Power and Outrage. Elite moves include Breaking Swipe or Hurricane.

Threat: Mega Rayquaza’s Outrage can devastate unprepared teams, and Draco Meteor hits Dragon-types severely.

Tip: Bring resistant Pokémon that can survive Dragon moves while dealing super-effective damage.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Rayquaza and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 3,153-3,264 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,942-4,080 CP
  • Boosted by: Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Rayquaza

  1. Ice-type attackers are by far the best due to ×4 weakness.
  2. Dodge Dragon Ascent and Outrage whenever possible.
  3. Relobby quickly to keep pressure on the boss.
  4. In Snowy weather, this raid becomes much easier.

Can Mega Rayquaza Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! After completing a Mega Rayquaza raid, players can encounter a Shiny Rayquaza. Shiny Rayquaza has a darker green and black coloration compared to the regular version.

While Mega Rayquaza itself cannot be caught, the regular Rayquaza encountered after the raid has a chance of being shiny. The shiny rate in Mega Raids is slightly higher than standard encounters, giving trainers a good opportunity to collect this rare form.

📌 Is Mega Rayquaza Worth Raiding?

Mega Rayquaza is absolutely worth raiding. It is currently one of the strongest Pokémon in the entire game and the best Dragon-type attacker in Pokémon GO.
