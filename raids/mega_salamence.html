Mega Salamence Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Mega Salamence is a Dragon and Flying-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Its high attack stats and versatile moves make it a challenging raid boss. Players should focus on its weaknesses, particularly Dragon, Ice, and Fairy-type counters, to defeat it efficiently.

This Mega Salamence raid guide covers everything you need to know: best counters, weaknesses, movesets, CP ranges, weather boosts, shiny availability, and expert raid strategies for both beginners and experienced trainers.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Salamence, Mega Salamence, is a formidable Dragon/Flying-type Pokémon in Pokémon GO raids. With its combination of high attack power and fast charged moves, it poses a serious threat to unprepared trainers.

Its Dragon and Flying typing gives it resistances against Grass, Bug, and Fighting-type moves, but it also has clear weaknesses to Dragon, Ice, and Fairy-type moves. Fire and Steel-type Pokémon can also help, depending on your team composition.

This guide will provide detailed information on Mega Salamence’s weaknesses, the best Pokémon counters, recommended movesets, CP ranges, weather effects, shiny encounter chances, and strategies for solo, duo, or group raids. Both new and experienced trainers will find this guide useful to maximize their rewards.

🧬 About Mega Salamence in Pokémon GO

Mega Salamence is a Dragon / Flying-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes one of the best Dragon-type attackers in Pokémon GO and boosts Dragon-type, Flying-type, and Psychic-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Salamence is useful because:

  • It is one of the top Dragon-type Mega attackers
  • It provides strong raid bonuses to Dragon, Flying, and Psychic types
  • It is excellent against Dragon-type raid bosses

🌀 Mega Salamence Moveset

Salamence common fast moves are Fire Fang or Dragon Tail or Bite. Charged moves include Fire Blast or Crunch or Hydro Pump or Fly or Brutal Swing or Draco Meteor. Elite moves include Outrage.

Threat: Outrage and Draco Meteor hit extremely hard, especially for unshielded Pokémon.

Tip: Avoid Grass and Fighting types—they resist little here.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Salamence and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,773-2,875 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,467-3,594 CP
  • Boosted by: Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Salamence

  1. Use Ice-type attackers to exploit the double weakness.
  2. Mega Evolve an Ice-type to boost the whole team.
  3. Dodge Draco Meteor and Outrage if possible.
  4. 3–4 strong trainers can defeat Mega Salamence easily.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 4–5 very strong trainers → Possible
  • 6–8 trainers → Comfortable clear
  • Lower-level groups → 9+ recommended

Shiny Mega Salamence

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Salamence after defeating Mega Salamence in a raid. While Mega Salamence itself cannot be caught, the regular Salamence that appears post-raid has a chance to be shiny.

The shiny version of Salamence has a noticeably darker blue body compared to the regular version, making it a highly sought-after Pokémon for collectors. The shiny rate for Mega Raids is generally higher than standard wild encounters, so participating in multiple raids increases your chances of obtaining one.

📌 Is Mega Salamence Worth Raiding?

Mega Salamence is absolutely worth raiding if you want a top-tier Dragon-type Mega Pokémon. It is extremely useful in Dragon raids and provides excellent raid bonuses.

Can Mega Salamence be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Salamence has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Salamence’s exclusive move?

Mega Salamence can use moves like Dragon Claw, Outrage, and Fly, dealing massive Dragon and Flying-type damage to opponents in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Salamence difficult to defeat?

With very high attack and speed, Mega Salamence can be challenging. Using multiple type-advantage Pokémon is recommended.

What makes Mega Salamence unique?

Its sleek Mega form, Dragon/Flying typing, and incredible offensive stats make Mega Salamence visually striking and strategically dominant in Max Raid Battles.
