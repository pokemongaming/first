Mega Heracross Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

← Back to All Raid Guides

Mega Heracross is a powerful Bug and Fighting-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. This Pokémon is known for its extremely high Attack stat and strong Bug-type moves, making it a challenging opponent in raids.

However, Mega Heracross has a major weakness that trainers can exploit. Because of its typing, it is double weak to Flying-type attacks, meaning strong Flying Pokémon can defeat it quickly. In this raid guide, you will learn the best counters, weaknesses, movesets, catch CP ranges, weather boosts, and battle strategies needed to defeat Mega Heracross efficiently.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Heracross, Mega Heracross is one of the strongest Bug-type Pokémon available in Pokémon GO. With a massive Attack stat and powerful Bug and Fighting-type moves, it can deal significant damage during raid battles.

Despite its strength, Mega Heracross has a clear weakness that trainers can take advantage of. Because it is both Bug and Fighting type, Flying-type attacks deal quadruple damage. This makes powerful Flying Pokémon some of the best choices for defeating this raid boss.

This guide covers everything you need to know about Mega Heracross raids, including weaknesses, best counters, optimal battle strategies, catch CP ranges, and shiny availability. Whether you are attempting the raid with a small group or a larger team of trainers, this guide will help you defeat Mega Heracross more efficiently.

🧬 About Mega Heracross in Pokémon GO

Mega Heracross is a Bug / Fighting-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes a powerful physical attacker and provides damage bonuses to Bug-type and Fighting-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Heracross is useful because:

  • It has one of the highest attack stats among Bug-type Pokémon
  • It is very effective against Normal, Dark, and Grass-type raid bosses

🌀 Mega Heracross Moveset

Heracross common fast moves are Counter or Struggle Bug. Charged moves include Close Combat or Upperhand or Earthquake or Rock Blast or Megahorn.

Pro tip: Close Combat is extremely powerful; bring Pokémon that resist Fighting moves. Avoid using fragile Bug types—they’ll faint quickly.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Heracross and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,650-2,751 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,312-3,439 CP
  • Boosted by: Rain & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Heracross gets a weather boost in:

  • Cloudy → boosts Fighting
  • Windy → boosts Bug

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Cloudy → boosts Psychic
  • Sunny → boosts Fire

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Heracross

  1. Flying-type attacks deal double super-effective damage.
  2. Dodge Rock Blast if possible — it hits Flying types hard.
  3. Mega Heracross has very low defense — use high-DPS teams.
  4. 2 strong trainers can defeat it comfortably.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 strong trainers → Possible
  • 3–4 trainers → Comfortable clear
  • Low-level players → 5+ recommended

Can Mega Heracross Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Heracross after defeating Mega Heracross in a raid. Although Mega Heracross itself cannot be caught, players will receive an encounter with regular Heracross after the raid, which has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Heracross has a noticeable pink color compared to the normal blue version, making it a popular shiny Pokémon for collectors in Pokémon GO.

The shiny rate for Mega Raids is generally higher than wild encounters, so completing multiple Mega Heracross raids increases your chances of finding a shiny version.

📌 Is Mega Heracross Worth Raiding?

Yes, Mega Heracross is one of the best Bug / Fighting-type Mega Pokémon in the game. It is excellent for raids against Normal, Dark, and Grass-type bosses.

FAQ

Can Mega Heracross be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Heracross has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Heracross’s exclusive move?

Mega Heracross can use moves like Megahorn and Close Combat, dealing massive Bug and Fighting-type damage to opponents in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Heracross difficult to defeat?

It has extremely high attack and good speed but moderate defenses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient and safe battles.

What makes Mega Heracross unique?

Its horned Mega form, dual Bug/Fighting typing, and incredible attack stats make Mega Heracross visually striking and strategically dangerous in Max Raid Battles.

Does Mega Heracross keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.
