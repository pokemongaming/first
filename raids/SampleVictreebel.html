Mega Victreebel Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weaknesses & Strategy

Mega Victreebel is a Grass and Poison-type raid boss that may look simple at first, but it can deal surprisingly fast damage if you come unprepared. Its attack-focused moveset allows it to pressure teams quickly, especially those relying on slower or defensive Pokémon.

The good news is that Mega Victreebel has one of the most exploitable weaknesses in the game — it takes double super-effective damage from Fire-type attacks. This makes it significantly easier compared to other Mega raids, provided you build your team correctly.

In this guide, we’ll break down the best counters, explain why they work, compare different strategies, and help you build the most efficient raid team whether you're playing solo or with a group.

Weaknesses and Resistances

Understanding Mega Victreebel’s typing is the key to defeating it efficiently. As a Grass and Poison-type Pokémon, it has multiple weaknesses but one stands out above the rest.

  • Double Weakness: Fire
  • Other Weaknesses: Flying, Psychic, Ice
  • Resistances: Water, Electric, Fairy, Fighting

Among these, Fire-type Pokémon should always be your priority. Not only do they deal the highest damage, but they also allow you to finish the raid faster and more safely.

Best Fire-Type Counters (Top Tier Picks)

Fire-type attackers dominate this raid due to the double weakness. If your goal is to clear the raid quickly with minimal resource usage, building a Fire-heavy team is the best approach.

Shadow Heatran

Shadow Heatran is one of the most reliable counters available because it offers a rare combination of durability and strong damage output. While many Fire-type attackers focus purely on DPS and faint quickly, Heatran can stay in battle longer thanks to its defensive typing.

It also resists Poison-type attacks, which gives it a clear advantage against Mega Victreebel’s moveset. This makes it especially useful in smaller raid groups where survivability becomes more important than raw damage.

Although it may not always top the damage charts, its consistency makes it one of the safest and most efficient choices overall.

Shadow Moltres

Shadow Moltres is one of the highest damage-dealing Fire-type attackers in the game. With access to powerful moves like Fire Spin and Overheat, it can melt Mega Victreebel extremely quickly.

However, this power comes at the cost of survivability. It tends to faint faster than bulkier Pokémon, which means it performs best in coordinated groups where battles end quickly.

If your goal is speed and maximum DPS, Shadow Moltres is one of the best options available.

Reshiram

Reshiram is a top-tier Legendary Pokémon that delivers both high damage and solid survivability. Unlike Shadow Pokémon, it provides a more balanced performance, making it ideal for most players.

Its Fire-type moves fully exploit Mega Victreebel’s double weakness, allowing it to consistently deal strong damage throughout the battle.

For players who prefer reliability over risk, Reshiram is one of the best overall picks.

Mega Charizard Y

Mega Charizard Y is not just a powerful attacker but also a team support Pokémon. Its Mega Evolution boosts Fire-type attacks for all players in the raid, significantly increasing overall damage output.

This makes it extremely valuable in group battles, where the boost can help shorten the raid time for everyone involved.

If you are coordinating with other players, having at least one Mega Charizard Y in the team can make a noticeable difference.

Flying and Psychic Alternatives

While Fire-types are clearly the best choice, Flying and Psychic-type Pokémon can still perform well if you lack strong Fire attackers.

Mega Rayquaza

Mega Rayquaza is one of the strongest Pokémon in the game and provides massive Flying-type damage. It also boosts other Flying-type attackers, making it useful in coordinated teams.

Mewtwo

Mewtwo remains one of the most powerful Psychic-type attackers due to its incredible stats and strong moves. While it doesn’t hit the double weakness, its raw power allows it to remain competitive.

This makes it a great fallback option for players who don’t have enough Fire-type Pokémon.

Mega Raid Mechanics

Mega Raids work differently from standard raids. Instead of catching the Mega form directly, players earn Mega Energy after defeating the boss. This energy is then used to Mega Evolve Victreebel. The faster the raid is completed, the more Mega Energy you receive. This makes using strong counters and coordinated teams extremely important.

Mega Victreebel vs Other Grass Raid Bosses

Compared to other Grass-type raid bosses like Mega Abomasnow, Mega Victreebel is more attack-focused but less bulky. This makes it easier to defeat but more punishing if you fail to dodge charged moves.

Why Raid Mega Victreebel?

Raiding Mega Victreebel allows players to collect Mega Energy, which is essential for Mega Evolution. Mega Evolutions provide powerful attack boosts to your team in raids and are useful for both PvE and events. Additionally, players have the chance to catch a high-IV or shiny Victreebel, making this raid valuable for collectors and competitive players alike.

Best Team Composition

A strong team should focus on Fire and Flying-type Pokémon, as they deal the most damage to Mega Victreebel. Mega Evolutions such as Mega Blaziken can boost the entire team's Fire-type attacks. Avoid using Grass, Water, Electric, or Fairy-type Pokémon, as they deal reduced damage and may faint quickly against the boss.

Counter Strategy Breakdown

Fire-type Pokémon are the most effective due to double weakness. Flying-types provide both offense and survivability, while Psychic-types offer strong burst damage. Using Shadow Pokémon increases damage output significantly, making raids faster and more efficient, especially in smaller groups.

Real Raid Experience

In real battles, Mega Victreebel often defeats players through consistent damage rather than sudden attacks. Fragile Pokémon tend to faint quickly, reducing overall performance.

Balanced attackers like Charizard or Mewtwo perform better because they survive longer and deal steady damage throughout the raid.

My Personal Raid Tip

I completed this raid with 4 trainers. We finished with around 80 seconds remaining. Initially, we failed once due to using wrong counters like Water-types. After switching to Fire-types, the raid became significantly easier. From experience, the biggest advantage in this raid comes from using a Mega Evolution early in the battle. Starting with a Mega Fire-type like Mega Blaziken or Mega Charizard Y boosts not only your damage but also your teammates’ attacks, making the raid significantly faster. Another key tip is to avoid over-relying on glass cannon Pokémon unless you are confident in dodging. Survivability matters more than raw DPS in smaller groups, as fainting frequently can reduce your overall damage output.

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using wrong types
    Many players bring Water, Electric, or Grass Pokémon, which deal low damage and struggle to survive.
  • Ignoring weather boost
    Sunny and Rainy weather both buff the boss, making the raid harder than expected.
  • Not using Mega Evolution
    Missing out on Mega Boost reduces total team DPS significantly.
  • No dodging
    Taking full damage from Sludge Bomb can wipe out your team quickly.
  • Underestimating group size
    Trying to duo or trio without optimized counters often leads to failure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Victreebel be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, you can encounter a shiny Victreebel after the raid, which can later Mega Evolve if a Mega form becomes available.

Is Mega Victreebel difficult to defeat?

It deals consistent damage but is relatively easy to defeat due to its multiple weaknesses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient and safe battles.

Can it be duoed?

Yes, high-level Fire-type teams can defeat it with two trainers.

Best Mega boost support?

Mega Fire-type Pokémon boost team-wide Fire damage.

Does Mega Victreebel keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

What makes Mega Victreebel unique?

Its fast and aggressive Mega form, Grass/Poison typing, and strong offensive stats make Mega Victreebel visually striking and strategically valuable in raid battles.

What is Mega Victreebel’s exclusive move?

Mega Victreebel can use moves like Leaf Blade and Sludge Bomb, dealing massive Grass and Poison-type damage to opponents in Mega Raid Battles.
