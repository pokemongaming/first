Mega Slowbro Raid Guide — Counters, Weakness & Strategy

Your complete resource for defeating TANKY SHELLDER in Pokémon GO raids.

Water Psychic

Mega Slowbro is a Water and Psychic-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Unlike many high-speed Mega Pokémon, Mega Slowbro focuses on bulk and defensive power.

It can absorb large amounts of damage while dealing steady Psychic- and Water-type attacks. Although not the hardest Mega Raid boss, trainers must choose proper counters to avoid long battles and unnecessary relobbies.

🧬 About Mega Slowbro in Pokémon GO

Mega Slowbro is useful because:

  • It boosts Water-type and Psychic-type Pokémon in raids
  • It is very bulky and can survive a long time in battle
  • It is great against Fire, Fighting, and Ground-type raid bosses

⚠️ Mega Slowbro Weaknesses

CategoryDetails

Mega Slowbro Stats & Battle Style

Mega Slowbro is known for its exceptional Defense stat. While it does not deal extreme damage compared to some Mega bosses, its survivability extends the raid timer if teams are unprepared.

This is not a raid you can brute-force casually. High DPS and proper team coordination are important, especially for small groups.

🗡️ Best Counters for Mega Slowbro

The best way to defeat Mega Slowbro is to use strong Electric, Grass, Dark, or Ghost-type Pokémon.

👻 Ghost-type Counters

  • Shadow Claw + Shadow Ball
  • Lick + Shadow Ball
  • Gengar : (Shadow Claw / Shadow Ball)

    • Mega Gengar deals massive Ghost-type damage but is fragile. Best used in coordinated teams or with confident dodging.

  • Darkrai : (Snarl / Shadow Ball)

    • Darkrai performs well due to high DPS against Psychic typing while avoiding Water weaknesses.

Ghost-types are strong offensively but should be used carefully if Mega Slowbro carries Dark coverage.

🌑 Dark-type Counters

  • Snarl + Dark Pulse
  • Bite + Crunch
  • Hydreigon: (Bite / Brutal Swing)

    • Hydreigon is a strong Dark-type option with solid survivability and consistent output.

Dark-types hit the Psychic typing effectively and resist Psychic-type moves.

⚡ Electric-type Counters

  • Thunder Shock + Wild Charge
  • Spark + Thunderbolt
  • Mega Electric Pokémon for team-wide boost
  • Manectric: (Thunder Fang / Wild Charge)

    • Mega Manectric delivers very high Electric-type DPS and boosts other Electric attackers in your team. It is one of the fastest clear options in coordinated raids.

  • Zekrom: (Charge Beam / Fusion Bolt)

    • Zekrom combines strong Electric damage with good bulk, making it reliable for extended fights.

Electric-types deal consistent super-effective damage and avoid being resisted by Mega Slowbro.

🐞 Bug-Type Counters

  • Fury Cutter + X-Scissor
  • Bug Bite + Bug Buzz

Bug Pokémon deal solid damage but may have lower bulk.

🌿 Strong Grass-type Options

  • Mega Sceptile (Fury Cutter + Frenzy Plant)
  • Roserade (Razor Leaf + Grass Knot)

Can You Solo Mega Slowbro?

Soloing Mega Slowbro is extremely difficult and generally not recommended. Due to its bulk, even high-level trainers struggle to defeat it alone within the time limit.

Only top-tier accounts with maxed Electric Megas and ideal weather may attempt a near-solo scenario.

Duo Strategy

Two high-level trainers with optimized Electric teams can defeat Mega Slowbro under neutral or favorable weather conditions.

  • Both trainers use Mega Electric Pokémon
  • Relobby quickly if needed
  • Avoid Grass if Ice Beam is present

Duo is realistic for experienced players.

Trio & Casual Group Strategy

Three to five trainers with decent counters can defeat Mega Slowbro comfortably.

For casual players, prioritize survivability and consistent Electric damage.

Beginner Tips

  • Use recommended team but verify moves
  • Avoid Fire, Water, and Psychic attackers
  • Check weather before raiding
  • Revive quickly to maintain pressure

Revive & Potion Cost

  • Average revives used: 8–14
  • Average potions used: 10–18
  • Dodging significantly reduces resource usage
  • Using Mega Pokémon reduces total relobbies

Common Raid Mistakes

  • Not bringing a Mega Pokémon for team boosts
  • Using weather-boosted Pokémon of the wrong type
  • Relobbying too late and losing damage time
  • Ignoring dodging during hard-hitting moves

🧠 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Slowbro

Focus on Electric or Dark Damage

Electric counters provide consistent performance, while Dark-types offer added safety against Psychic attacks.

Avoid Fire and Water Types

Mega Slowbro resists Fire and Water moves, making them inefficient choices.

Use Mega Boosts Strategically

Activating a Mega Electric or Mega Dark Pokémon increases team-wide damage and shortens battle duration.

FAQ

Can Mega Slowbro be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, if shiny Slowbro is available in Pokémon GO, you may encounter its shiny form after winning the raid.

What is the best moveset of Slowbro?

The best moveset for Slowbro is Confusion as a Fast Attack and Ice Beam or Psychic as Charged Attacks.

Is Mega Slowbro difficult to defeat?

Mega Slowbro has high defense and decent attack. Using proper type advantage and avoiding resisted types makes the raid manageable.

Does Mega Slowbro keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

How many trainers are needed to defeat Mega Slowbro?

2–4 trainers with strong Electric, Grass, or Dark-type counters can defeat Mega Slowbro efficiently.
