Gigantamax Kingler Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Kingler is a pure Water-type raid boss, heavily offensive and vulnerable to Electric and Grass attacks.

Best Counters

  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Electivire, Raikou
  • Grass Types – Zarude, Roserade, Venusaur
  • Dragon Types – Rayquaza, Palkia

Recommended Moves

  • Electivire: Thunder Shock + Wild Charge
  • Zarude: Vine Whip + Power Whip
  • Rayquaza: Dragon Tail + Outrage

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain
Best for you: Partly Cloudy or Sunny.

Team Suggestions

  • Electric attackers easily overpower its defenses.
  • Grass attackers provide consistent damage.

Rewards

Mega Energy, XL Candy, and Kingler catch rewards.
