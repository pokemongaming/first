Gigantamax Charizard Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type raid boss and one of the most iconic Pokémon. With high attack power and strong raid presence, you’ll need optimized counters to defeat it.

Best Counters

  • Rock Types – Rhyperior, Tyranitar, Terrakion
  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Raikou, Electivire
  • Water Types – Kyogre, Swampert, Feraligatr

Recommended Moves

  • Rhyperior: Smack Down + Rock Wrecker
  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Kyogre: Waterfall + Surf

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Sunny and Windy
Best weather to fight: Partly Cloudy (boosts Rock).

Team Suggestions

  • Rock-type teams do massive damage with double effectiveness.
  • Bring at least 4–5 strong Rock attackers for fast clears.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, bonus items, and Charizard catch rewards.
