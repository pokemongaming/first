Mega Lopunny Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Mega Lopunny is a Normal and Fighting-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Thanks to its dual typing, it has a powerful attack stat and can deal strong Fighting-type damage to many common raid counters. However, Mega Lopunny also has several important weaknesses that trainers can take advantage of during raids.

In this raid guide, you will learn everything you need to defeat Mega Lopunny efficiently, including its weaknesses, best counters, movesets, catch CP ranges, shiny availability, and raid strategies for both beginner and experienced trainers.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Lopunny, Mega Lopunny is a powerful Mega Raid Boss that combines Normal and Fighting typing in Pokémon GO. This unique combination gives it access to strong Fighting-type attacks that can quickly defeat unprepared raid teams.

Despite its offensive power, Mega Lopunny also has clear weaknesses. Pokémon that use Flying, Psychic, or Fairy-type moves can deal significant damage and help trainers win the raid more easily.

Preparing the right team of counters is the key to defeating Mega Lopunny efficiently. This guide will help you understand its weaknesses, choose the best counters, learn effective raid strategies, and maximize your rewards after the battle.

Mega Lopunny is useful because:

🌀 Mega Lopunny Moveset

Lopunny common fast moves are Pound or Low Kick or Double Kick. Charged moves include Hyper Beam or Focus Blast and Fire Punch or Triple Axel.

Threats: Mega Lopunny can chain fast attacks with multi-bar charge moves, draining shields quickly.

Tip: Use resistant Flying or Psychic types to absorb attacks and retaliate efficiently.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Lopunny and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,051-2,140 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,564-2,675 CP
  • Boosted by: Partly Cloudy & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Lopunny gets a weather boost in:

  • Partly Cloudy → boosts Normal
  • Cloudy → boosts Fighting

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Windy → boosts Flying
  • Windy → boosts Psychic
  • Cloudy → boosts Fairy

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Lopunny

  1. Flying, Fairy, and Psychic types are the best counters.
  2. Dodge Focus Blast whenever possible.
  3. Mega Lopunny has low bulk and goes down fast.
  4. 2 strong trainers can defeat it comfortably.

Can Mega Lopunny Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Lopunny after defeating Mega Lopunny in a raid battle. Although the Mega Evolution itself cannot be caught, players will encounter the normal Lopunny after the raid, which may appear in its shiny form.

Shiny Lopunny has noticeable visual differences compared to the regular version, featuring lighter color tones that make it stand out. Because shiny Pokémon are rare, many trainers participate in multiple raids to increase their chances of finding one.

Completing Mega Lopunny raids also rewards Mega Energy, allowing trainers to Mega Evolve their own Lopunny for future battles and raids.

📌 Is Mega Lopunny Worth Raiding?

Mega Lopunny is a useful Fighting-type Mega Pokémon and is definitely worth raiding if you want a strong Mega Pokémon for raid battles.
