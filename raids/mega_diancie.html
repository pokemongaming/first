Mega Diancie Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Mega Diancie is a powerful Rock and Fairy-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Thanks to its strong offensive stats and unique typing, it can be a challenging opponent in raids. However, trainers can defeat Mega Diancie efficiently by using strong Steel, Ground, Water, or Grass-type Pokémon.

This Mega Diancie raid guide covers everything trainers need to know, including the best counters, weaknesses, movesets, catch CP ranges, shiny availability, and expert strategies to win the raid battle.

Introduction

Mega Diancie is the Mega Evolution of Diancie, a Mythical Pokémon known for its sparkling diamond-like body and powerful Fairy and Rock-type abilities. In Pokémon GO, Mega Diancie appears as a Mega Raid Boss, giving trainers the opportunity to collect Mega Energy and eventually Mega Evolve their own Diancie.

Because of its Rock and Fairy typing, Mega Diancie has several weaknesses that skilled trainers can exploit. Steel-type Pokémon are particularly effective, as Steel attacks deal super effective damage against both of Mega Diancie’s types.

By building a team with strong counters and understanding Mega Diancie’s moveset and weaknesses, trainers can defeat this raid boss efficiently and earn valuable rewards, including the chance to catch a high-IV Diancie after the raid battle.

🧬 About Mega Diancie in Pokémon GO

Mega Diancie is a Rock / Fairy-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes one of the highest DPS Rock-type Pokémon in Pokémon GO and provides damage bonuses to Rock-type and Fairy-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Diancie is useful because:

  • It is one of the strongest Rock-type attackers in the game
  • It boosts Rock-type and Fairy-type attackers
  • It is excellent against Flying, Fire, Bug, and Dragon-type raid bosses

🌀 Mega Diancie Moveset

Diancie common fast moves are Rock Throw or Tackle. Charged moves include Rock Slide or Moonblast (Massive Fairy-type damage against Dark or Dragon counters) or Power Gem (High DPS Rock attacks capable of breaking shields).

Most Dangerous Move: Diamond Storm and Moonblast hit extremely hard.

Pro tip: Avoid low-CP Grass and Fire-types—they don’t survive long against its balanced Rock/Fairy moves.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Diancie and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,384-2,483 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,980-3,104 CP
  • Boosted by: Partly Cloudy & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Diancie gets a weather boost in:

  • Partly Cloudy → boosts Rock
  • Cloudy → boosts Fairy

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Snow → boosts Steel
  • Rainy → boosts Water
  • Sunny → boosts Grass

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Diancie

  1. Steel-types are the best and safest counters.
  2. Mega Diancie is very fragile and will go down quickly.
  3. Dodge Moonblast to keep Steel attackers alive longer.
  4. 2 strong trainers can defeat it easily.

Pro tip: Mega Diancie is fragile compared to other Megas but can punish careless timing and mismatched counters.

Can Mega Diancie Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Diancie after defeating Mega Diancie in a raid battle. While Mega Diancie itself cannot be caught, players will encounter the base form, Diancie, after the raid and it may appear in its shiny form.

Shiny Diancie has a slightly darker pink coloration compared to the normal version, making it a rare and valuable addition to any Pokémon GO collection.

Shiny Pokémon from Mega Raids generally have higher shiny rates compared to wild encounters, so completing multiple Mega Diancie raids increases the chances of obtaining this rare variant.

📌 Is Mega Diancie Worth Raiding?

Yes, especially because Mega Diancie is currently one of the best Rock-type Mega Pokémon in the entire game and is extremely useful for many raid bosses.

FAQ

Can Mega Diancie be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Diancie’s exclusive move?

Mega Diancie can use moves like Moonblast, dealing massive Rock and Fairy-type damage in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Diancie difficult to defeat?

It has high special attack and decent bulk. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures the battle is manageable and efficient.

What makes Mega Diancie unique?

Its crystalline Mega form, combination of Rock/Fairy typing, and strong moves make Mega Diancie visually stunning and strategically formidable in Max Raid Battles.

Does Mega Diancie keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

🏁 Conclusion

Mega Diancie hits extremely hard but has very low defense. Steel-type teams will destroy it very quickly.
