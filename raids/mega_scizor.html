Introduction

Mega Scizor is the Mega Evolution of Scizor and a challenging raid opponent in Pokémon GO. With its Bug/Steel typing, it resists many common attacks, making careful planning essential for raid success.

The key to defeating Mega Scizor quickly is exploiting its Fire-type weakness. Using strong Fire-type counters can dramatically reduce the number of trainers needed and shorten battle times.

This guide provides all the information trainers need—from the best counters and movesets to CP ranges and shiny availability—so that both beginners and experienced players can take down Mega Scizor efficiently.

🧬 About Mega Scizor in Pokémon GO

Mega Scizor is a Bug / Steel-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes a powerful Bug-type attacker and provides damage bonuses to Bug-type and Steel-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Scizor is useful because:

  • It boosts Bug-type and Steel-type Pokémon in raids
  • It is excellent against Psychic, Grass, and Fairy-type raid bosses
  • It has very high defense and good attack

🌀 Mega Scizor Moveset

Scizor common fast moves are Fury Cutter or Bullet Punch. Charged moves include X-Scissor or Iron Head or Trailblaze or Night Slash.

Threat: Mega Scizor’s Bullet Punch + Iron Head combo can quickly break shields.

Tip: Avoid using Grass, Bug, or Ice Pokémon—they take reduced damage and may slow your raid.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Scizor and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,243-2,335 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,804-2,919 CP
  • Boosted by: Rainy & Snowy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Scizor

  1. Only use Fire-type attackers to exploit the double weakness.
  2. Mega Evolve a Fire-type Pokémon to boost the entire raid lobby.
  3. Dodge Iron Head if possible.
  4. 2–3 strong trainers can defeat Mega Scizor easily.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2–3 very strong trainers → Possible
  • 4–5 trainers → Comfortable clear
  • Lower-level groups → 6+ recommended

Can Mega Scizor Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! After defeating Mega Scizor in a raid, players have a chance to encounter a Shiny Scizor. While Mega Scizor itself cannot be caught, the regular Scizor that appears post-raid may be shiny.

The Shiny Scizor has a darker red coloration compared to the regular Scizor. Because Mega Raids often have higher shiny rates than wild encounters, participating in multiple raids increases the chance to obtain it.

📌 Is Mega Scizor Worth Raiding?

Mega Scizor is worth raiding mainly for its Mega bonus to Bug and Steel-type Pokémon. It is also a solid Bug-type Mega attacker.

What is Mega Scizor’s exclusive move?

Mega Scizor can use moves like Bullet Punch and X-Scissor, dealing massive Steel and Bug-type damage to opponents in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Scizor difficult to defeat?
