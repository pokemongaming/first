Moves & Threats

Lugia common fast moves are Extrasensory or Dragon Tail. Charged moves include Sky Attack or Fly or Hydro Pump and Future Sight. Elite moves include Aeroblast.

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Rainy weather : boosts Electric type moves and Lugia Hydro Pump is also boosted, so be aware. Partly Cloudy weather : boosts Rock attackers. Foggy weather : boosts Dark attackers.

Battle Tips

  • Electric-types like Zapdos or Magnezone deal excellent damage to Flying-type Lugia.
  • Dark-types like Tyranitar or Hydreigon help against Psychic moves.
  • Ice-types like Mamoswine or Kyurem can help cover Dragon counters in multi-type raid strategies.
  • Weather boosts: Rainy weather boosts Electric-type moves, Snow boosts Ice-type moves.
  • Focus on high DPS counters and dodging charged moves to preserve team health.
