🌿 Mega Victreebel Raid Guide

🧬 Raid Overview

Mega Victreebel is a Grass and Poison-type Mega Raid boss known for its aggressive damage output and dangerous charged moves. It is weak to Fire, Flying, Ice, and Psychic attacks, making these types essential for fast raid clears.

This raid is moderately difficult and typically requires 3–5 trainers depending on team strength. High-level players with optimized counters can attempt smaller teams, but coordination and Mega boosts are important.

Mega Victreebel takes super effective damage from:

🔥 Fire
🕊️ Flying
❄️ Ice
🧠 Psychic

Avoid Water and Electric types as they deal reduced damage.

🧪 Moveset Breakdown

  • Fast Moves: Razor Leaf, Acid, Magical Leaf
  • Charged Moves: Sludge Bomb, Leaf Blade, Solar Beam ⚠️

⚠️ Solar Beam is extremely dangerous and can knock out most Pokémon in one hit.

🧠 Raid Strategy (Beginner → Pro)

For beginners, the key to defeating Mega Victreebel is simply using strong Fire-type Pokémon. Fire attacks deal massive damage due to its double weakness, making even average teams effective. Focus on staying alive and rejoining quickly if your team faints.

Intermediate players should begin optimizing their teams by selecting high DPS attackers like Charizard, Blaziken, or Psychic-types like Mewtwo. Dodging charged moves becomes important here, especially against Solar Beam, which can wipe out your team instantly if ignored.

Advanced players should focus on maximizing damage output through Mega Evolution boosts, weather bonuses, and proper team rotation. Using a Mega Charizard Y not only increases your damage but also boosts other Fire-type attackers in your group. Coordinated teams can complete the raid faster and earn more rewards.

In weather conditions like Sunny or Cloudy weather, Victreebel becomes stronger due to boosted moves. In such cases, using high-level counters and dodging effectively becomes critical to avoid unnecessary fainting.

Relobby strategy is also important. Instead of wasting time reviving during the battle, quickly rejoin with a second team of attackers. This ensures constant damage output and improves your chances of winning in smaller groups.

❌ Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Using Water-type Pokémon
  • ❌ Ignoring Solar Beam
  • ❌ Not using Mega Pokémon
  • ❌ Bringing low DPS defenders
  • ❌ Not dodging charged moves
