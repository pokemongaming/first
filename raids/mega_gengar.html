Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Gengar, Mega Gengar, is a Ghost and Poison-type Pokémon that appears in Mega Raids in Pokémon GO. Known for its incredibly high attack stat, Mega Gengar can be a dangerous opponent if trainers are not prepared with the right counters.

Fortunately, Mega Gengar has several exploitable weaknesses, particularly to Psychic, Ghost, Dark, and Ground-type attacks. Trainers using powerful Pokémon like Mewtwo, Hydreigon, or Tyranitar can defeat Mega Gengar relatively quickly.

Defeating Mega Gengar rewards trainers with Mega Energy and the chance to catch Gengar. With the right team composition and strategy, even small groups of trainers can successfully complete this raid.

🌀 Mega Gengar Moveset

Gengar common fast moves are Hex or Shadow Claw (Quick fast attacks that build energy fast) or Sucker Punch. Charged moves include Sludge Bomb (Heavy charged moves capable of taking out unprepared counters) or Drain Punch or Focus Blast or Shadow Ball (Heavy charged moves capable of taking out unprepared counters). Elite moves include Lick (Quick fast attacks that build energy fast) or Sludge Wave or Shadow Punch or Psychic or Dark Pulse (Heavy charged moves capable of taking out unprepared counters).

Pro tip: Shield Shadow Ball or Sludge Bomb with Steel or Ground Pokémon for survivability.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Gengar

  1. Dodge Shadow Ball if possible — it hits extremely hard.
  2. Dark-types are safest and most consistent.
  3. Ground-types help avoid Ghost damage.
  4. 2–3 strong trainers can defeat Mega Gengar easily.

Can Mega Gengar Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Gengar after defeating Mega Gengar in a raid battle. Although the Mega form itself cannot be caught, the Gengar encounter after the raid has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Gengar has a slightly lighter body color compared to the regular version. However, its Mega Evolution form becomes much more noticeable, turning almost completely white, which makes it one of the most visually unique Mega Evolutions in Pokémon GO.

The shiny rate for Mega Raids is higher than normal wild encounters, so participating in multiple raids increases your chances of finding a shiny Gengar.

📌 Is Mega Gengar Worth Raiding?

Yes, Mega Gengar is one of the strongest Ghost-type attackers in Pokémon GO. It is a glass cannon but provides incredible damage output and excellent raid bonuses.

FAQ

Can Mega Gengar be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Gengar has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Gengar’s exclusive move?

Mega Gengar can use moves like Shadow Ball and Sludge Bomb, dealing massive Ghost and Poison-type damage in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Gengar difficult to defeat?

It has extremely high attack and speed but low defenses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures it is defeated efficiently before it can overwhelm your team.

What makes Mega Gengar unique?

Its ghostly Mega form, incredible speed, and high offensive stats make Mega Gengar visually striking and strategically dangerous in Max Raid Battles.

Does Mega Gengar keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.
