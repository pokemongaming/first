Introduction

Mega Tyranitar is the Mega Evolution of Tyranitar and one of the more dangerous Mega Raid bosses in Pokémon GO. Its Rock/Dark typing gives it resistance to many common attack types, but also several key weaknesses, especially Fighting-type Pokémon.

Despite its power, with the right counters and team composition, Mega Tyranitar can be defeated efficiently. This guide will cover everything from its stats, moves, weaknesses, recommended counters, catch CP, and raid strategies, helping both beginners and experienced players prepare for the battle.

🧬 About Mega Tyranitar in Pokémon GO

Mega Tyranitar is a Rock / Dark-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it provides damage bonuses to Rock-type and Dark-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Tyranitar is extremely valuable because:

  • It is one of the strongest Rock-type and Dark-type attackers in the game
  • It boosts Rock and Dark-type Pokémon for the entire raid team
  • It is amazing against Flying, Fire, Ice, Ghost, and Psychic-type raid bosses

🌀 Mega Tyranitar Moveset

Tyranitar common fast moves are Iron Tail or Dragon Breath or Bite. Charged moves include Stone Edge or Fire Blast or Crunch or Brutal Swing. Elite moves include Smack Down.

Threat: Stone Edge and Crunch deal enormous damage.

Dangerous Moves: Stone Edge and Fire Blast hit extremely hard and can wipe teams quickly.

Tip: Fighting-types like Machamp or Conkeldurr can take it down quickly with resisted moves.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Tyranitar and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,951-3,055 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,689-3,820 CP
  • Boosted by: Partly Cloudy & Fog

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Tyranitar gets a weather boost in:

  • Partly Cloudy → boosts Rock
  • Fog → boosts Dark

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Cloudy → boosts Fighting
  • Rain → boosts Water

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Tyranitar

  1. Always use Fighting-type attackers to exploit the double weakness.
  2. Mega Evolve Lucario if possible to boost Fighting-type damage for the whole lobby.
  3. Dodge Stone Edge and Fire Blast when possible.
  4. 3–4 strong trainers can defeat Mega Tyranitar comfortably.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2–3 very strong trainers with Fighting teams → Possible
  • 4–5 trainers → Easy and safe clear
  • Lower-level groups → 6+ recommended

Can Mega Tyranitar Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, after defeating Mega Tyranitar in a raid, trainers have the chance to encounter a Shiny Tyranitar. While Mega Tyranitar itself cannot be caught, the regular Tyranitar that appears after the raid may be shiny.

The shiny Tyranitar has a slightly lighter green hue than the normal version. Shiny encounters are rare, so repeated raids increase the chance of obtaining one.

📌 Is Mega Tyranitar Worth Raiding?

Mega Tyranitar is absolutely worth raiding. It is one of the best Mega Pokémon in Pokémon GO and is useful in a huge number of raids thanks to its Rock and Dark-type power.
