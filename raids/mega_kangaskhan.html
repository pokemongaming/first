Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Kangaskhan, Mega Kangaskhan is a powerful Normal-type Pokémon that appears in Mega Raids in Pokémon GO. Known for battling alongside its baby Pokémon, Mega Kangaskhan has strong attack power and can be a challenging opponent for unprepared trainers.

Unlike many raid bosses that have multiple weaknesses, Mega Kangaskhan has only one type weakness: Fighting-type attacks. Because of this, trainers should focus on bringing powerful Fighting-type Pokémon such as Machamp, Lucario, and Conkeldurr to defeat the raid boss efficiently.

In this guide, you will learn about Mega Kangaskhan’s weaknesses, the best raid counters, catch CP values, and strategies that will help you defeat this Mega Raid boss and earn valuable Mega Energy in Pokémon GO.

🌀 Mega Kangaskhan Moveset

Kangaskhan common fast moves are Low Kick or Mud Slap. Charged moves include Power-Up Punch or Earthquake or Outrage and Crunch. Elite moves include Stomp or Brick Break.

Pro tip: Its Baby attack can deal unexpected damage if you aren’t ready for Close Combat. Use high-damage Fighting-types that can survive Body Slam to reduce raid time.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Kangaskhan

  1. Use Fighting-type Pokémon only — it is Mega Kangaskhan’s only weakness.
  2. Dodge Earthquake whenever possible.
  3. High-DPS Fighting types can finish this raid very fast.
  4. 2 strong trainers can defeat Mega Kangaskhan easily.

When to avoid

Avoid using Fairy or Psychic types, as they take heavy damage from Kangaskhan’s Steel and Psychic moves. Fragile Bug or Ice types can also faint too quickly.

Can Mega Kangaskhan Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Kangaskhan after defeating Mega Kangaskhan in a raid battle. While Mega Kangaskhan itself cannot be caught, the Pokémon encountered after the raid is Kangaskhan, which has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Kangaskhan features a noticeable color change, with its body appearing in a slightly greener shade compared to the normal brown coloration.

Mega Raids generally have a higher shiny rate than standard wild encounters, so participating in multiple Mega Kangaskhan raids increases the chances of obtaining a shiny version.

📌 Is Mega Kangaskhan Worth Raiding?

Mega Kangaskhan is not the most important Mega Pokémon, but it is still worth raiding for Mega Pokédex entry and for players who like collecting Mega Pokémon.
