Gigantamax Rillaboom Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Rillaboom is a pure Grass-type raid boss with strong physical attacks. Fire, Flying, Bug, Ice, and Poison attackers are all effective.

Best Counters

  • Fire Types – Charizard, Blaziken, Moltres
  • Flying Types – Rayquaza, Staraptor, Yveltal
  • Ice Types – Weavile, Glaceon, Mamoswine

Recommended Moves

  • Charizard: Fire Spin + Blast Burn
  • Rayquaza: Air Slash + Hurricane
  • Mamoswine: Powder Snow + Avalanche

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Sunny
Best for you: Windy (Flying), Snow (Ice), or Overcast (Poison).

Team Suggestions

  • Fire attackers deal the fastest damage.
  • Flying attackers help avoid heavy neutral hits.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, and Rillaboom catch rewards.
