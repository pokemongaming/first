Gigantamax Rillaboom — Raid Guide

Quick facts

Species: Rillaboom (Gigantamax)
Type: Grass
Primary weaknesses: Bug, Fire, Flying, Ice, Poison

Introduction

Gigantamax Rillaboom brings a new twist to the classic Rillaboom raid: larger presence, signature G-Max Drum Solo mechanics that apply lingering residual damage, and charged moves that deal heavy-area effects. This guide explains everything trainers need to beat Gigantamax Rillaboom reliably in Pokémon GO raids — from weaknesses and recommended Gigantamax counters to Dynamax picks, weather interactions, team-size guidance, and catch/reward tips.

Moves & Threats

Rillaboom common fast moves are Razor Leaf or Scratch. Charged moves include Earth Power or Grass Knot and Energy Ball.G-Max Drum Solo not only delivers heavy damage but also leaves a damaging area for a short time — timing your swaps and using at least one bulky support reduces wipe risk.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,817-1,899 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,272-2,374 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Sunny weather boosts Grass and Fire damage. Snow weather boosts Ice attackers.

Battle flow tip: Bring strong Fire and Flying attackers, especially if Sunny or Windy weather is active, for the fastest clears. Use top Fire and Flying attackers for maximum efficiency. Ice Pokémon can also help, particularly in Snowy weather conditions.

Battle Tips

  • Bring a mix of high DPS (Fire/Bug/Flying/Ice) and at least one tank for large lobbies.
  • Lead with your strongest DPS to shrink the boss fast — G-Max bosses have huge HP pools, so early damage matters.
  • Quick-dodge only very heavy G-Max hits; too much dodging reduces total DPS in long fights.
  • Star and favorite raid teams before joining to minimize relobby time.

Team Size

Recommended: 15–20 trainers for comfortable clears with mixed player levels. With many Gigantamax / Dynamax counters, 10–14 can work. Under 8 trainers should be attempted only if all participants are high-level and coordinated with ideal counters and Dynamax/G-Max boosts.

Why Raid Gigantamax Rillaboom?

Reasons include Pokédex entry, strong Grass attacker, shiny chance, and XL Candy farming.

Rewards & Catch Tips

  • Rewards typically include Rare Candy, Dynamax resources, and a chance to catch Rillaboom (shiny availability depends on event).
  • Use Golden Razz for high-IV or shiny attempts, Pinap when farming candy en masse.
  • Weather-boosted catches (Sunny, etc.) yield higher CP; plan catch attempts accordingly.

Raid Strategy & Catch Tips

Keep shields for heavy Grass charged moves like Solar Beam. Rotate attackers if your Fire or Flying types faint quickly. Golden Razz Berries give the highest catch success, while Silver Pinaps help farm Rillaboom Candy.

FAQ

What is Gigantamax Rillaboom type and weakness?

Rillaboom is a Grass type. It is weak to Fire, Flying, Ice, Psychic type moves.

What are good counters for G-Max Drum Solo?

Strong Fire, Flying, Ice, Poison, Bug attackers such as Gigantamax Toxtricity, Gigantamax Venusaur, Gigantamax Charizard, Gigantamax Lapras, Dynamax Passimian, Dynamax Machamp.

Can Gigantamax Rillaboom be shiny?

Yes, but in certain events.

Is Gigantamax Rillaboom catchable after the raid?

Yes — standard raid catch mechanics apply.

Does weather change move sets?

Sunny boosts both Rillaboom’s Grass attacks and your Fire counters, making battles riskier but faster.

What does G-Max Drum Solo do?

G-Max Drum Solo is a charged attack that deals immediate damage and creates a short lingering damage field affecting trainers’ Pokémon. It favors teams with at least one bulky support or planned swap windows.

Conclusion

Gigantamax Rillaboom is a mechanically interesting raid boss: its Grass is predictable, but the G-Max residual damage forces teams to plan for survivability, not just pure DPS. Prioritize Fire and Flying Gigantamax picks, add Dynamax support for sustained windows, and include at least one high-TDO tank if your lobby is smaller. With the counters and tactics above, you’ll clear Venusaur efficiently and maximize your rewards.
