Mega Victreebel is a powerful Grass and Poison-type raid boss in Pokémon GO. It is known for its high attack stat and dangerous fast moves like Razor Leaf. Trainers should prepare strong Fire, Flying, Ice, or Psychic-type counters to defeat it efficiently. This guide covers the best counters, movesets, CP ranges, shiny comparison, and battle strategy.


Boss Moves

Possible attacks

Mega Victreebel has access to 2 Fast Attacks (Razor Leaf, Acid), which cover Grass and Poison-type attacks. When using Razor Leaf, Acid you will benefit from STAB (Same-Type-Attack-Bonus), which increases the move damage by ×1.2. It has access to 5 Charge Attacks (Sludge Bomb, Solar Beam, Leaf Blade, Acid Spray, and Leaf Tornado), which cover Poison and Grass-type attacks. When using Sludge Bomb, Solar Beam, Leaf Blade, Acid Spray, and Leaf Tornado you will benefit from STAB (Same-Type-Attack-Bonus), which increases the move damage by ×1.2. It has access to 1 Elite Fast Attacks (Magical Leaf), which cover Poison and Grass-type attacks. When using Magical Leaf you will benefit from STAB (Same-Type-Attack-Bonus), which increases the move damage by ×1.2.

Fire-type Pokémon such as Mega Blaziken and Reshiram are among the best choices against Mega Victreebel due to its double weakness to Fire-type moves. These Pokémon deliver high DPS and can quickly reduce the boss’s HP.

Shadow Pokémon, including Shadow Moltres and Shadow Entei, provide an additional attack boost, making them excellent options for faster raid completion, especially in smaller groups.

⚠️ Important: Both Sunny and Cloudy weather boost Mega Victreebel’s moves, making the raid significantly harder. Avoid raiding in these conditions unless you have strong counters.

To gain an advantage, players can use weather-boosted Fire-type Pokémon during Sunny conditions. However, in Rainy weather, it is recommended to rely more on Flying or Psychic-type attackers to avoid increased damage from Poison moves.

If possible, raiding during neutral weather conditions provides the safest and most balanced experience.

Raid Strategy Guide

For all skill levels

🟢 Beginner Trainers:


  • • Use Fire Pokémon for fastest results (Flying works as backup)
  • • Join raids with 8+ players for safety
  • • Avoid resisted types
  • • Use revives efficiently

🟡 Intermediate Trainers:


  • • With a decent lineup of Fire, Flying, or Psychic-type counters, 6-8 trainers can comfortably defeat Mega Victreebel.
  • • Using Mega Evolutions can further boost team damage.

🔴 Advanced Trainers:


  • • High-level players with optimized teams and Mega boosts can complete this raid with as few as 4-6 trainers.
  • • Coordination and proper dodging can significantly improve success rates.

💡 Pro Tip: Avoid Sunny and Rainy weather, as both boost Mega Victreebel’s attacks and make the raid harder.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Victreebel be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, you can encounter a shiny Victreebel after the raid, which can later Mega Evolve if a Mega form becomes available.

Is Mega Victreebel difficult to defeat?

It deals consistent damage but is relatively easy to defeat due to its multiple weaknesses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient and safe battles.

Can it be duoed?

Yes, high-level Fire-type teams can defeat it with two trainers.

Best Mega boost support?

Mega Fire-type Pokémon boost team-wide Fire damage.

Does Mega Victreebel keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

What makes Mega Victreebel unique?

Its fast and aggressive Mega form, Grass/Poison typing, and strong offensive stats make Mega Victreebel visually striking and strategically valuable in raid battles.

What is Mega Victreebel’s exclusive move?

Mega Victreebel can use moves like Leaf Blade and Sludge Bomb, dealing massive Grass and Poison-type damage to opponents in Mega Raid Battles.
