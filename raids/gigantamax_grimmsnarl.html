Gigantamax Grimmsnarl Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Grimmsnarl is a Dark/Fairy raid boss with powerful mixed attacks. This guide provides the best counters, moves, and strategies to defeat it.

Best Counters

  • Steel Types – Metagross, Zacian, Dialga
  • Poison Types – Roserade, Nihilego, Gengar
  • Fairy Types – Togekiss, Gardevoir

Recommended Moves

  • Metagross: Bullet Punch + Meteor Mash
  • Nihilego: Poison Jab + Sludge Bomb

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Fog (Dark), Cloudy (Fairy)
Best battle weather: Snow (boosts your Steel types).

Team Suggestions

  • Steel is the safest option due to double effectiveness.
  • A solid team of Metagross or Dialga clears the raid quickly.

Rewards

XL Candy, bonus balls, TMs, and chance to catch Grimmsnarl.
