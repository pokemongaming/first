Overview

Centiskorch in Gigantamax form is a Fire/Bug raid boss that amplifies damage and HP. Expect high-power Fire STABs (e.g., Fire Blast / Overheat analogues), large-area flamethrowers, and possible Bug-type charged moves (Bug Buzz) or coverage like Earthquake or Iron Head in special spawns. Because of its Fire typing, Centiskorch is especially vulnerable to Water, Rock, and Ground depending on moves. Prioritize counters that can land repeatedly into long HP bars and survive charged coverage.

Pro tip: If you have access to top Rock attackers (Rampardos, Tyranitar) and Ground (Groudon, Rhyperior), those will greatly reduce required players â€” but Water is the safest, most accessible core for most rosters.
