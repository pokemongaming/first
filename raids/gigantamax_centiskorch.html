Gigantamax Centiskorch Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Centiskorch is a Fire/Bug-type raid boss. It has decent bulk and a mix of fire and bug attacks. Use strong Rock, Ground, or Water attackers to counter.

Best Counters

  • Rock Types – Tyranitar, Rhyperior, Terrakion
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Rhyperior, Excadrill
  • Water Types – Kyogre, Swampert

Recommended Moves

  • Rhyperior: Smack Down + Rock Wrecker
  • Garchomp: Mud Shot + Earth Power
  • Kyogre: Waterfall + Surf

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Sunny (Fire) and Windy (Bug)
Best for you: Rain or Partly Cloudy.

Team Suggestions

  • Rock attackers are ideal for fast takes.
  • Ground types help with bulk; Water gives damage safety.

Rewards

Mega Energy, XL Candy, Charged TMs, and Centiskorch capture chances.
