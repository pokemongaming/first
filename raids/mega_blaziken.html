🧬 About Mega Blaziken in Pokémon GO

Mega Blaziken is the Mega Evolution of Blaziken. It remains a Fire/Fighting-type Pokémon but gains a massive increase in attack power, making it one of the top damage-dealing Mega Pokémon in the game.

Using Mega Blaziken in raids gives bonus damage to:

  • Fire-type Pokémon
  • Fighting-type Pokémon

This makes Mega Blaziken extremely useful against Ice, Steel, Bug, Dark, and Grass-type raid bosses.

Weaknesses & resistances

CategoryDetails

What Makes Mega Blaziken Tough?

Mega Blaziken deals considerable damage with its powerful combo of Fire and Fighting moves, such as Blaze Kick and Brave Bird, and has enough bulk to outlast unprepared teams. Blindly using auto-recommended teams often leads to long battles or defeat because many Pokémon resist its typing.

Best Moveset

  • Fast: Counter, Ember, Fire Spin
  • Charged: Focus Blast, Aura Sphere, Brave Bird, Overheat, Blaze Kick
  • Elite: Stone Edge, Blast Burn

Pro tip: Brave Bird is especially dangerous and can wipe out Fighting and Grass attackers instantly, while Focus Blast can punish Rock and Dark types hard. Because of its massive attack stat, even neutral damage hurts.

Why These Types Work

Because Mega Blaziken combines Fire and Fighting types, its defenses are poor against aerial maneuvers (Flying), heavy hitters like Ground and Water, and fast Psychic attacks that exploit its Fighting moves’ weaknesses. Each of these types hits it for increased damage, reducing the total number of trainers or time needed to defeat it.

Recommended Teams

Easy Group Team (3–6 Trainers)

  • Mega Rayquaza (Flying attacks) — excellent DPS and boosts flying coverage
  • Primal Kyogre (Water moves) — powerful sustained Water damage
  • Groudon (Ground attacks) — solid against both Fire and Fighting
  • Shadow Mewtwo (Psychic)
  • Rayquaza or Shadow Salamence (Flying)

This balanced team focuses on hitting weaknesses while surviving Mega Blaziken’s charged moves.

Advanced Team (Fewer Trainers)

  • Mega Rayquaza — top flying attacker
  • Shadow Mewtwo — high Psychic damage
  • Primal Kyogre or Water Types
  • Garchomp or other Ground attackers
  • Rayquaza or Shadow Salamence

With these choices, experienced trainers can often clear the raid in minimal time.

Raid Strategy

  • Start with your strongest Flying or Water attacker — these give the fastest DPS advantage.
  • Prioritize Psychic attackers if Mega Blaziken uses Fighting moves you need to punish at range.
  • Dodge charged moves like Brave Bird or Focus Blast when possible — this preserves team longevity.
  • Rejoin the battle quickly if your team faints to avoid wasting time and increase total damage done.
  • If the weather boosts one of your effective types, bring those Pokémon first.

FAQ

Can Mega Blaziken be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Blaziken has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Blaziken’s exclusive move?

Mega Blaziken can use moves like Blaze Kick, dealing massive Fire and Fighting-type damage to opponents during Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Blaziken difficult to defeat?

It has very high attack and speed but moderate defenses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures the battle is manageable and efficient.

What makes Mega Blaziken unique?

Its fiery Mega form, powerful dual typing, and incredible speed make Mega Blaziken visually impressive and strategically strong in Max Raid Battles.

Does Mega Blaziken keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.
