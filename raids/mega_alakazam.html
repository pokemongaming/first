Moves & Threats

Alakazam common fast moves are Psycho Cut or Confusion. Charged moves include Focus Blast or Shadow Ball or Fire Punch and Future Sight. Elite moves include Counter or Psychic or Dazzling Gleam .

Pro tip: While it has very low defense, its huge attack stat means that Shadow Ball and Focus Blast can instantly knock out careless counters.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,475-2,577 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,094-3,221 CP
  • Boosted by: Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Foggy weather: boosts Dark and Ghost type attackers. Cloudy weather : boosts Bug type attackers.
