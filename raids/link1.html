📊 Mega Victreebel Stats & Overview

Mega Victreebel is a powerful Grass and Poison-type Mega Pokémon with strong offensive stats and solid raid performance. It reaches a maximum CP of 3963, supported by high attack power and decent bulk, making it a dangerous raid boss and a strong Grass/Poison attacker.

  • Max CP: 3963
  • Attack: 265
  • Defense: 181
  • Stamina: 190
  • Weather Boost: Sunny, Cloudy

It performs especially well as a Grass and Poison attacker, ranking among the stronger options in both categories.

⚔️ Weaknesses & Resistances

Mega Victreebel takes increased damage from:

🔥 Fire
🕊️ Flying
❄️ Ice
🧠 Psychic

It resists multiple common types:

  • ✔️ Grass (strong resistance)
  • ✔️ Water, Electric, Fairy, Fighting

⚠️ Using resisted types like Water or Electric significantly reduces your damage output.

🧪 Best Moveset & DPS

The strongest moveset for Mega Victreebel focuses on maximizing damage output using fast Grass-type attacks combined with powerful charged moves.

  • 🌿 Best Fast Move: Magical Leaf
  • ☠️ Best Charged Move: Sludge Bomb

This combination delivers high DPS and strong total damage output, making it the most efficient setup for raids and PvE battles.

Alternative combinations like Leaf Blade or Acid-based setups are still viable but slightly less effective overall.

🎯 CP Ranges & IV Info

  • Raid CP: 1918 – 2003
  • Weather Boosted: 2397 – 2503
  • Research CP: 1438 – 1502

Raid encounters guarantee a minimum IV floor, meaning every caught Pokémon will have solid baseline stats.

A perfect IV Mega Victreebel reaches its maximum CP when fully powered up, making it ideal for long-term investment.

📈 Performance & Meta Role

Mega Victreebel performs as a strong Grass and Poison-type attacker, offering high damage output with efficient moves. It is especially effective when boosting other Pokémon of the same type during raids.

  • 🌿 Strong Grass attacker (top-tier category)
  • ☠️ Strong Poison attacker
  • ⚔️ Useful for raid damage boosting

While not the absolute strongest Mega in the game, it provides excellent value in coordinated raid teams.

⚡ Mega Boost Mechanics

When Mega Evolved, Mega Victreebel boosts the damage of all other Pokémon in the raid. Pokémon using Grass or Poison-type attacks receive an even stronger bonus, making team coordination extremely important.

  • ✔️ +10% damage boost to all teammates
  • ✔️ +30% boost for same-type attacks

Only one Mega boost applies at a time, so using the strongest Mega Pokémon in your team is key.
