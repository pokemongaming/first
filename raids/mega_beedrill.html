Moves & Threats

Beedrill common fast moves are Poison Jab or Poison Sting or Infestation. Charged moves include Aerial Ace or Sludge Bomb or X-Scissor and Fell Stinger. Elite moves include Bug Bite or Drill Run.

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Cloudy weather: boosts Poison-type moves like Sludge Bomb.Rainy weather : boosts Bug-type moves like X-Scissor. Windy weather : boosts Flying/Psychic counters. Sunny weather : boosts Fire-type counters.

Battle Tips

  • Prioritize multi bar water type charge moves.
  • If weather is sunny, faint rate change - bring more water type or ground type attackers
  • Bring a squad of high water type attackers.
  • Use dodge sparingly - focus on sustained DPS and shields if in a smaller group.
