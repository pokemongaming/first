Introduction

Gigantamax Alcremie is a special Gigantamax Pokémon appearing in raids with a unique Fairy typing. Its attacks can deal significant damage to Pokémon weak to Fairy moves, so choosing counters carefully is key. With its high stamina and attack stats, organizing your raid team properly is crucial.

This guide will cover everything from Alcremie’s weaknesses, the best Pokémon to counter it, its moveset, CP ranges, weather effects, and effective raid strategies. Whether you are a new trainer or a veteran, this guide ensures you can take down Gigantamax Alcremie efficiently.

Overview

Alcremie’s Gigantamax form magnifies its Fairy typing and gives it a bulkier HP pool and potentially stronger charged moves (Dazzling Gleam, Draining Kiss, and event coverage like Psyshock or Shadow Ball). In raid form, it behaves like a resilient Fairy boss that rewards correct super-effective counters (Poison, Steel, Ghost in some cases) and punishes teams that bring common physical attackers without coverage. Because of the enlarged HP, energy efficiency and repeatable charged-move value become more important than single-hit burst.

Pro tip: If the charged-move preview shows heavy Fairy moves (Dazzling Gleam / Moonblast), prioritize Poison + Steel counters; if Psychic coverage appears, Ghost becomes a high priority.

Max Battle Usage Strategy

  • Prioritize Steel-type Max Moves over Poison when possible.
  • Use bulky defenders early to charge the Max Meter safely.
  • Coordinate Max Phases in smaller groups to avoid wasted windows.
  • Avoid using Dragon, Dark, or Fire attackers — they are not optimal.

Summary: Dynamax Metagross and Excadrill are the strongest Steel-type alternatives when Gigantamax options are limited.

Moves & Threats

Alcremie common fast moves are Tackle or Charm. Charged moves include Psyshock or Dazzling Gleam and Energy Ball. G-Max Finale not only delivers heavy damage but also leaves a damaging area for a short time — timing your swaps and using at least one bulky support reduces wipe risk.

Threat: Dazzling Gleam deals high area-of-effect damage

Tip: Bring Poison-types like Roserade or Gengar to quickly counter its Fairy attacks.

Is Gigantamax Alcremie Worth Raiding?

Gigantamax Alcremie is a strong Fairy-type option and a valuable addition for trainers collecting Gigantamax Pokémon. It is particularly useful in future Dragon-type and Dark-type encounters.

Can Gigantamax Alcremie Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, after defeating a Gigantamax Alcremie raid, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Alcremie. While the Gigantamax form itself cannot be caught, the regular Alcremie you encounter post-raid may appear in shiny form.

Shiny Alcremie has a slightly darker or different color palette depending on its form (vanilla, strawberry, rainbow, etc.), making it a prized addition for collectors and competitive players alike.

The shiny rate in Gigantamax raids is slightly higher than standard wild encounters, so participating in multiple raids improves your chance of obtaining it.
