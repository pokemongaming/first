Introduction

Gigantamax Alcremie is a Fairy-type Gigantamax raid boss featured in special Pokémon GO events. Known for its towering cake-like form, it offers unique visuals and limited-time rewards. As a Fairy type, it is weak to Poison and Steel, making strong attackers from those types the best counters.This guide explains everything trainers need to beat Gigantamax Alcremie reliably in Pokémon GO raids — from weaknesses and recommended Gigantamax counters to Dynamax picks, weather interactions, team-size guidance, and catch/reward tips.

Moves & Threats

Alcremie common fast moves are Tackle or Charm. Charged moves include Psyshock or Dazzling Gleam and Energy Ball. G-Max not only delivers heavy damage but also leaves a damaging area for a short time — timing your swaps and using at least one bulky support reduces wipe risk.

Weather Effects

Rainy weather : boosts Poison moves. Cloudy weather : boosts Fairy attackers. Snow weather : boosts Steel attackers.

Team Size

Recommended: 15–20 trainers for comfortable clears with mixed player levels. With many Gigantamax / Dynamax counters, 10–14 can work. Under 8 trainers should be attempted only if all participants are high-level and coordinated with ideal counters and Mega/Dynamax/G-Max boosts.

Why Raid Gigantamax Alcremie?

Reasons include Pokédex entry, tallest Fairy attacker, shiny chance, and XL Candy farming.

Raid Strategy & Catch Tips

Bring top-tier Steel and Poison Pokémon to maximize damage output. Metagross with Meteor Mash is the most reliable counter. Avoid bringing Dragon, Dark, or Fighting types, as they are vulnerable to Fairy attacks. A small group of high-level trainers can clear the raid with optimal counters.

FAQ

What is Gigantamax Alcremie type and weakness?

Alcremie is a Fairy type. It is weak to Poison, Steel.

What are good counters for G-Max Cannonde?

Strong poison and steel attackers such as Zacian, Zamazenta, Eternatus, Dynamax Metagross, Dynamax Corviknight.

Can Gigantamax Alcremie be shiny?

Yes, but in certain events.

Is Gigantamax Alcremie catchable after the raid?

Yes — standard raid catch mechanics apply.

Does weather change move sets?

Rainy weather boosts Poison moves, Cloudy weather boosts Fairy attackers, Snow weather boosts Steel attackers.

Does Alcremie keep G-Max ?

No — it’s exclusive to Gigantamax raids.

Is Gigantamax Alcremie good in battles?

Gigantamax Alcremie is mostly a collector’s raid boss. While Fairy typing is useful, it doesn’t outperform top Fairy attackers.

Conclusion

Gigantamax Alcremie is a mechanically interesting raid boss: its Grass/Poison combo is predictable, but the G-Max residual damage forces teams to plan for survivability, not just pure DPS. Prioritize Fire and Flying Gigantamax picks, add Dynamax support for sustained windows, and include at least one high-TDO tank if your lobby is smaller. With the counters and tactics above, you’ll clear Alcremie efficiently and maximize your rewards.
