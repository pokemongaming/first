Mega Garchomp Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

← Back to All Raid Guides

Mega Garchomp is a powerful Dragon and Ground-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. It is one of the strongest Mega Evolutions in the game and can deal heavy damage with powerful Dragon and Ground-type attacks. Because of its typing, Mega Garchomp has a major weakness to Ice-type attacks, which deal quadruple damage.

This raid guide explains everything trainers need to defeat Mega Garchomp efficiently, including its weaknesses, best counters, catch CP ranges, shiny availability, and raid strategies for both small and large raid groups.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Garchomp, Mega Garchomp is a formidable Mega Raid boss in Pokémon GO. Known for its incredible Attack stat and powerful moves, it can be a challenging opponent for trainers who are not properly prepared.

Mega Garchomp’s Dragon and Ground typing gives it several resistances, but it also creates one very important weakness. Ice-type moves deal extremely high damage against it, making strong Ice attackers the best choice for this raid battle.

In this guide, we will cover Mega Garchomp’s weaknesses, the best Pokémon counters, recommended raid strategies, catch CP values, and shiny availability. Whether you are a beginner trainer or a high-level raider, this guide will help you defeat Mega Garchomp more easily and maximize your raid rewards.

🧬 About Mega Garchomp in Pokémon GO

Mega Garchomp is a Dragon / Ground-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes one of the strongest Dragon-type and Ground-type attackers in the game and provides damage bonuses to Dragon-type and Ground-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Garchomp is useful because:

  • It boosts Dragon-type and Ground-type attackers
  • It has extremely high attack and good bulk
  • It is excellent against Electric, Fire, Steel, and Rock-type raid bosses

🌀 Mega Garchomp Moveset

Garchomp common fast moves are Mud Shot or Dragon Tail (High-speed Dragon-type fast and charged moves). Charged moves include Earthquake (Devastating Ground-type charged move) or Sand Tomb or Fire Blast or Breaking Swipe or Outrage (Optional high damage, can surprise unprepared teams). Elite moves include Earth Power.

Most Dangerous Moves: Outrage and Earth Power deal massive damage.

Pro tip: Mega Garchomp hits hard—bring Pokémon that resist either Dragon or Ground moves to survive longer.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Garchomp and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,993-3,099 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,742-3,874 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Garchomp

  1. Ice-type attackers are the absolute best due to double weakness.
  2. Avoid Electric types (Ground immunity).
  3. Dodge Outrage to reduce fainting.
  4. This is a bulky Mega — bring revives.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 3 strong trainers → Possible
  • 4–6 trainers → Comfortable clear
  • Low-level players → 7+ recommended

Can Mega Garchomp Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Garchomp after defeating Mega Garchomp in a raid battle. Although Mega Garchomp itself cannot be caught, the Pokémon encountered after the raid will be a regular Garchomp, which has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Garchomp has a slightly different color pattern compared to the normal version, with darker tones that make it highly sought after by collectors.

Mega Raid battles generally have better shiny odds than wild encounters, so completing multiple raids increases your chances of finding a shiny Garchomp.

📌 Is Mega Garchomp Worth Raiding?

Yes, especially because Mega Garchomp is one of the best Dragon and Ground-type Mega Pokémon in the game and is extremely useful for many raid bosses.

FAQ

Can Mega Garchomp be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Garchomp has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Garchomp’s exclusive move?

Mega Garchomp can use moves like Dragon Claw and Earthquake, dealing massive Dragon and Ground-type damage in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Garchomp difficult to defeat?

It has high attack and good speed but moderate defenses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures the battle is efficient and safe.

What makes Mega Garchomp unique?

Its draconic Mega form, combination of Dragon/Ground typing, and high offensive stats make Mega Garchomp visually impressive and strategically dangerous in Max Raid Battles.

Does Mega Garchomp keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.
