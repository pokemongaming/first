Incarnate Landorus is a powerful Ground and Flying-type Legendary Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Trainers face strong Ground and Flying moves, so knowing its weaknesses and best counters is essential.

Introduction

Incarnate Landorus is one of the most formidable Legendary Pokémon in Pokémon GO raids. Its Ground/Flying typing gives it immunity to Electric moves and strong resistance to Fighting attacks, but it has notable vulnerabilities to Ice, Water, and Grass moves.

Trainers need to plan carefully to defeat Incarnate Landorus efficiently. This guide will help you identify the best counters, movesets, weather conditions, and strategies for maximizing your chances of success, whether you are tackling it solo or with a group.

By the end of this guide, you’ll be fully prepared to beat Incarnate Landorus and claim the raid rewards, including a chance at the shiny variant.

Difficulty

Small groups must rely on high-level Ice-type attackers and careful shield management. Large teams can overpower it with coordinated DPS and multi-bar charged moves, but dodging key attacks remains crucial to minimize fainting.

Moves & Threats

Incarnate Landorus common fast moves are Rock Throw or Mud Shot. Charged moves include Rock Slide or Focus Blast or Outrage and Earth Power.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,965-2,050 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,456-2,563 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Sunny weather : boosts Ground type attackers. Rainy weather : boosts Water attackers. Snowy weather : boosts Ice attackers.

Battle Strategy

  1. Landorus is 4× weak to Ice — always use Ice attackers.
  2. Watch out for Stone Edge; it hits Ice Pokémon hard.
  3. Dodge if possible to extend your team’s survival.
  4. In small groups, relobby quickly to save time.

Can Incarnate Landorus Be Shiny?

Yes! After defeating Incarnate Landorus in a raid, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Landorus (Incarnate Form). The shiny version has slightly darker orange coloring and is highly sought after by collectors.

While the shiny rate is not guaranteed, participating in multiple raids increases your odds of catching one.

Why Raid Incarnate Landorus?

Its appearance allows trainers to hone counter strategies, practice dodge timing, and strengthen Ice-type teams. Landorus also adds tactical value in PvE and Gym battles, making it a highly rewarding Legendary to capture.

Conclusion

Incarnate Landorus is an easy raid if you use Ice-type Pokémon. Thanks to its double weakness, even small groups can defeat it quickly.

Summary

Defeating Landorus Incarnate efficiently relies on Ice-type attackers, precise charged move timing, and team coordination. Even small groups can succeed with high-level Mamoswine, Weavile, or Kyurem, while larger teams can overwhelm it with well-planned rotations and shield usage. It’s a raid that rewards strategy and preparation more than raw numbers alone.
