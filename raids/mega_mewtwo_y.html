What is Mega Abomasnow?

Mega Abomasnow is the Mega Evolution of Abomasnow, an Ice/Grass-type Pokémon originally from the Sinnoh region.

When it Mega Evolves:

  • Its stats increase significantly (especially Attack and Defense)
  • It becomes much stronger than its normal form
  • It provides aMega Boost to other Ice- and Grass-type Pokémon in raids

Role as a Raid Boss

In Mega Raids:

  • You fight Mega Abomasnow as a Tier 4/5-level difficulty boss
  • It has very high CP (often around 45,000+)
  • Requires 3–6 trainers depending on level and counters

Key Traits:

  • Type: Ice / Grass
  • Weather Boost: Snowy (Ice), Sunny (Grass)
  • High bulk + strong Ice-type moves

Why Players Do This Raid

Players battle Mega Abomasnow to:

  • Earn Mega Energy (to Mega Evolve their own Abomasnow)
  • Catch a strong Abomasnow with good IVs
  • Use it for:
    • Ice-type raid boosts (great for Dragon raids)
    • PvP (especially in certain leagues)

Simple Summary

Mega Abomasnow is:

  • A dual Ice/Grass Mega Raid Boss
  • Easy to beat if you use Fire-types
  • Valuable for its Mega Boost and utility

Why Mega Abomasnow Matters

Mega Abomasnow in Pokémon GO is not just another raid boss—it has real strategic value for both raids and long-term gameplay. First, defeating it rewards Mega Energy, which allows you to Mega Evolve your own Abomasnow. This is important because Mega Evolutions provide team-wide damage boosts, especially for Ice- and Grass-type Pokémon, making future raids faster and easier. Second, Mega Abomasnow is particularly useful in the current raid meta because Ice-type attackers are crucial for taking down powerful Dragon-type bosses like Rayquaza or Salamence. Having a Mega Abomasnow active increases your team’s overall efficiency in such battles. Lastly, it’s a solid addition for players who want:

  • Better raid performance
  • Useful type coverage
  • A strong Mega option for weather-based strategies

In short, this raid helps you build a stronger account, not just win one battle.

Difficulty Level of Mega Abomasnow

Mega Abomasnow is generally considered a moderate-difficulty Mega Raid Boss. Its dual Ice/Grass typing gives it many weaknesses, and most importantly, a double weakness to Fire-type attacks (4× damage). This dramatically reduces its difficulty compared to bulkier or more defensive raid bosses.

Difficulty Breakdown:

  • With strong Fire counters: Easy to Medium
  • With average teams: Medium
  • With poor counters: Hard

Because of this:

  • 2–3 high-level players with Fire-types can defeat it comfortably
  • 4–6 players make the raid very safe and fast

However, it can still be dangerous if:

  • It uses strong charged moves like Blizzard
  • Players don’t exploit its Fire weakness
  • Weather boosts increase its damage output

Simple Verdict

  • Importance: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (Very useful Mega for raids)
  • Difficulty: ⭐⭐☆☆☆ (Easy if prepared, medium otherwise)
