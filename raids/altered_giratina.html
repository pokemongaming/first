Moves & Threats

Altered Giratina common fast moves are Shadow Claw (fast energy gain and dangerous DPS) or Dragon Breath (very high damage). Charged moves include Dragon Claw (very frequent, hard to dodge) or Ancient Power (weaker but can still chip your team) or Shadow Sneak (fast and spammy). Elite moves include Shadow Force.
