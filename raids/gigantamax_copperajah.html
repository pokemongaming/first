Gigantamax Copperajah Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Copperajah is a pure Steel-type raid boss with high attack power. This guide provides the best counters, weather conditions, and team strategies to defeat it efficiently.

Best Counters

  • Fire Types – Reshiram, Blaziken, Chandelure
  • Fighting Types – Lucario, Machamp, Conkeldurr
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Rhyperior, Excadrill

Recommended Moves

  • Reshiram: Fire Fang + Fusion Flare
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere
  • Garchomp: Mud Shot + Earth Power

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Snow (Steel attacks)
Best for you: Sunny (boosts Fire) or Cloudy (boosts Fighting).

Team Suggestions

  • Fire Pokémon deal maximum damage and survive well.
  • Ground Pokémon are effective but avoid heavy Steel attacks.

Rewards

XL Candy, bonus items, Charged TMs, and a Copperajah catch chance.
