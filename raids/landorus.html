Landorus Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness & Strategy

Therian Landorus is a Legendary Ground and Flying-type Pokémon in Pokémon GO. It is a challenging raid boss with high attack stats, making it essential to bring the right counters. This guide covers the best counters, weaknesses, movesets, CP ranges, weather boosts, and raid strategy.

Raid Boss: Therian Landorus

Type: Ground / Flying

Raid Tier: Legendary Raid

Major Weakness: Ice-type attacks

Introduction

Therian Landorus is one of the most formidable Legendary Pokémon in Pokémon GO. Its dual Ground/Flying typing gives it unique resistances, but also a significant weakness to Ice-type moves. With high attack and stamina, it can pose a serious challenge for unprepared trainers.

This raid guide will help both beginners and experienced trainers defeat Therian Landorus efficiently. You will find detailed information on its weaknesses, the best counters, optimal raid strategies, and CP ranges to maximize your rewards.

Difficulty

Small teams require fast-charging Ice-type moves and careful timing of shields, while larger groups can rely on sheer DPS. Its combination of high Attack and moderate HP makes it a challenging, but rewarding, raid boss.

Moves & Threats

Landorus common fast moves are Extrasensory or Mud Shot. Charged moves include Superpower or Earthquake or Bulldoze and Stone Edge. Elite moves include Sandsear Storm.

Earthquake and Earth Power deal massive damage, especially to Rock and Steel-type Pokémon, so be ready to dodge when necessary.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,151-2,241 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,688-2,801 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Best Team Composition

For best results, your team should include:

  • 4–5 Ice-type attackers
  • 1 Water-type backup Pokémon

Because Landorus has a double weakness to Ice, Ice-type Pokémon are by far the best choice for this raid.

Weather Boost

Landorus is boosted in Sunny and Windy weather, making it stronger and harder to defeat.

Your counters are boosted in:

  • Snowy weather (Ice)
  • Rainy weather (Water)

How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 trainers: Possible with top Ice counters and Mega boost
  • 3 trainers: Easy with good Ice teams

Catch Tips

  • Use Golden Razz Berries
  • Go for curve Excellent throws
  • Wait until Landorus finishes its attack animation
  • Aim slightly above center due to its flying position

Battle Tips

  • Ice-type Pokémon are the absolute best counters
  • Dodge Earthquake if your team is small
  • Use Mega Abomasnow or Mega Glalie for Ice boost
  • Avoid Electric, Poison, and Rock-type attackers

Is Landorus Worth Raiding?

Landorus is one of the best Ground-type attackers in Pokémon GO, especially in its Therian form. It is absolutely worth raiding for both PvE and collection purposes.

Can Therian Landorus Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Therian Landorus after completing the raid. The shiny version has a slightly darker, more striking color palette compared to the normal form.

Shiny Legendary Pokémon are rare, so participating in multiple raids will increase your chances of obtaining one.

FAQ

Can Landorus be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Orange becomes light tan, and tail glows more gold.

What is Landorus’s exclusive move?

Landorus can use Earth Power and Rock Slide to deal massive damage in Max Raid Battles.

Is Landorus difficult to defeat?

It has high attack and decent defenses. Using Ice or Water-type counters ensures efficient battles.

What makes Landorus unique?

Its control over land and wind, combined with Ground/Flying typing and massive attacks, makes Landorus visually impressive and strategically challenging.

What makes Landorus strong?

Its high attack, strong resistances, and Meteor Mash make it a top-tier raid and PvP option.

Conclusion

Landorus is a powerful raid boss, but its double weakness to Ice makes it much easier to defeat with the right team. Bring strong Ice-type Pokémon, use a Mega evolution for extra damage, and you will defeat Landorus efficiently and secure a high-CP catch.

Quick picks

Summary

To efficiently defeat Therian Landorus, focus on Ice-type counters with high DPS while supplementing with Rock-type Pokémon for Flying coverage. Small raids require coordination and shielding, while large groups can overwhelm it with a mix of strong attackers. Always consider weather boosts and move types to optimize your strategy.
