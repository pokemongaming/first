Gigantamax Venusaur Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Venusaur is a Grass/Poison-type raid boss. Strong Psychic, Fire, Flying, and Ice attackers are effective in this matchup.

Best Counters

  • Fire Types – Charizard, Arcanine, Heatran
  • Psychic Types – Mewtwo, Espeon, Alakazam
  • Flying Types – Rayquaza, Staraptor, Tornadus

Recommended Moves

  • Mewtwo: Confusion + Psystrike
  • Charizard: Fire Spin + Blast Burn
  • Rayquaza: Air Slash + Hurricane

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Sunny (Grass), Cloudy (Poison)
Best for you: Windy (Psychic), Snow (Ice), or Sunny (Fire).

Team Suggestions

  • Fire and Psychic attacks deal the fastest clears.
  • Flying attackers help avoid heavy Grass moves.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, and Venusaur capture rewards.
