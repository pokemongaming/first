Mega Banette Raid Guide Overview

Mega Banette is a powerful Ghost-type Mega Evolution in Pokémon GO, known for its extremely high attack stat and strong offensive presence in raid battles. As a Mega Pokémon, it not only deals significant damage but also boosts Ghost-type attacks of other players in the raid.

Despite its high damage output, Mega Banette has relatively low defense, making it a glass cannon that requires smart play and proper team composition. Trainers who understand its strengths and weaknesses can take full advantage of its raid performance and Mega boost capabilities.

Banette FAQs

Here are the most common questions about Banette, including strategies, Mega Evolution, PvP tips, and shiny hunting. This section helps trainers quickly find answers and make informed decisions.

1. What is the best fast move for Banette?

Snarl is the best fast move, generating energy quickly and synergizing with charged moves like Dark Pulse for maximum raid and PvP efficiency.

2. Which charged move should I prioritize?

Dark Pulse is the most consistent choice. For coverage against Psychic-types, Shadow Ball can be added as a secondary charged move.

3. How do I Mega Evolve Banette?

Obtain Mega Energy from raids or special events and use it on Banette. Mega Evolution lasts the duration of the raid or battle, boosting attack, defense, and CP.

4. What is the shiny Banette spawn rate?

Shiny Banette is very rare, usually appearing in Mega Raids or special events with a base rate of ~1 in 20 encounters. Using multiple Premier Balls and observing animations carefully increases success chances.

5. Who are Banette’s best counters?

Strong Ghost- or Dark-type Pokémon like Gengar, Chandelure, and Tyranitar make excellent counters in raids. In PvP, Fairy-types like Togekiss or Gardevoir are highly effective.

6. Is Banette good in PvP leagues?

Banette performs best in the Great League due to its fast energy gain and strong Dark-type attacks. Avoid battles with strong Fairy-types and manage shields carefully to maximize effectiveness.
