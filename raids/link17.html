Groudon Overview

Groudon is a **Legendary Ground-type Pokémon** from the Hoenn region, known as the **Continent Pokémon**. It has immense power over land and is one of the strongest Ground-type attackers in Pokémon GO, especially in raids and Master League PvP.

With its **high Attack stat, strong movesets, and access to powerful Ground-type moves**, Groudon is a top-tier choice for defeating Electric-, Fire-, Steel-, and Rock-type raid bosses. Its Primal form further boosts its dominance in high-level gameplay.

Explore this guide: Stats & Base Attributes, Best Movesets, Counters, and more.

Groudon Type & Weaknesses

Groudon is a **pure Ground-type Pokémon**, which gives it strong advantages against several types but also leaves it vulnerable to key weaknesses. Understanding these interactions is essential for **building counters and planning raid battles**.

Type Effectiveness

Type Effect on Groudon
Water Super Effective ⚡ – Major weakness, especially in raids
Grass Super Effective ⚡ – Strong counter type
Ice Super Effective ⚡ – Effective but less common
Electric No Effect ❌ – Completely immune due to Ground typing
Rock Resistant – takes reduced damage
Poison Resistant – reduced damage

Weakness Strategy Tips

  • ✔ Use **Water-type Pokémon** as the primary counter in raids.
  • ✔ Grass-types are excellent secondary options with strong DPS.
  • ✔ Avoid Electric-type attackers—they deal no damage to Groudon.
  • ✔ Watch out for **Fire-type moves** in Groudon’s moveset, which can counter Grass Pokémon.
  • ✔ In PvP, switch out quickly if facing strong Water-type opponents.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: Can Groudon spawn in the wild?
    A: No, Groudon only appears in **Legendary Raid Battles** or special events.
  • Q: What are the best counters for Groudon raids?
    A: **Kyogre, Mamoswine, Gyarados, Swampert, and Roserade** are top counters.
  • Q: Is Primal Groudon stronger than regular Groudon?
    A: Yes, Primal Groudon has higher **Attack and Defense** and adds **Fire typing** for extra coverage.
  • Q: Which moveset is best for raids?
    A: **Mud Shot + Earthquake** is the most reliable, with **Solar Beam or Fire Punch** for coverage.
  • Q: How many Premier Balls do I get?
    A: The number depends on your **damage contribution, gym control, and participation** in the raid.
  • Q: Can I catch Groudon with a low-level Pokémon?
    A: It’s highly recommended to **use high-CP counters**; low-level Pokémon won’t deal enough damage to defeat it efficiently.
