Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Manectric, Mega Manectric is a powerful Electric-type Pokémon that appears in Mega Raids in Pokémon GO. With its fast Electric-type attacks and strong offensive stats, it can deal significant damage to many raid counters.

However, Mega Manectric has a major weakness that trainers can easily exploit. As a pure Electric-type Pokémon, it is weak to Ground-type moves, which deal super-effective damage and can defeat it quickly when used by strong attackers.

In this raid guide, you will learn about Mega Manectric’s weaknesses, the best Pokémon counters, catch CP values, shiny availability, and effective raid strategies that will help you defeat this Mega Raid boss with ease.

🌀 Mega Manectric Moveset

Manectric common fast moves are Charge Beam or Thunder Fang or Snarl. Charged moves include Flame Burst or Overheat or Thunder or Wild Charge and Psychic Fangs.

Threat: Mega Manectric hits hard and fast, chaining Volt Switch into Wild Charge can quickly knock out fragile Pokémon.

Tip: Use high-defense Ground-types to absorb hits and retaliate with super-effective moves.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Manectric and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 1,959-2,046 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,449-2,557 CP
  • Boosted by: Rainy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Manectric gets a weather boost in:

  • Rainy → boosts Electric

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Sunny → boosts Ground

Mega Manectric’s Meteor Mash hits very hard. Prioritize bulky Fire- or Ground-types to absorb damage while dishing out super-effective hits.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Manectric

  1. Use Ground types only — Mega Manectric has just one weakness.
  2. Dodge Overheat if possible — it hits extremely hard.
  3. Mega Manectric is very fragile and goes down quickly.
  4. 2 strong trainers can defeat it easily.

Can Mega Manectric Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Manectric after defeating Mega Manectric in a Mega Raid battle.

While the Mega Evolution itself cannot be caught, players will encounter Manectric after completing the raid, and it has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Manectric has a darker black and yellow color scheme compared to the regular version, making it a highly sought-after Pokémon for collectors and shiny hunters.

Participating in multiple Mega Manectric raids increases the chance of encountering its shiny form.

📌 Is Mega Manectric Worth Raiding?

Mega Manectric is absolutely worth raiding if you want one of the best Electric-type Mega Pokémon for raid battles.
