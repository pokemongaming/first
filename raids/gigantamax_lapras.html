Gigantamax Lapras Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Lapras is an Ice/Water raid boss with very high bulk, making it a tanky opponent. This guide covers the best counters, moves, weather boosts, and optimal teams for defeating it quickly.

Best Counters

  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Raikou, Magnezone
  • Fighting Types – Lucario, Conkeldurr, Machamp
  • Grass Types – Zarude, Roserade, Venusaur

Recommended Moves

  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere
  • Zarude: Vine Whip + Power Whip

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain (Water moves), Snow (Ice moves)
Best for you: Cloudy (boosts Fighting) or Sunny (boosts Grass).

Team Suggestions

  • Electric types provide the fastest clear time.
  • A team of 5–6 strong Electric/Fighting Pokémon is ideal.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, bonus balls, and a chance to catch Lapras.
