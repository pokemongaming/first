Gigantamax Meowth Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Meowth is a Normal-type raid boss with moderate stats. Fighting attackers dominate this battle.

Best Counters

  • Fighting Types – Machamp, Lucario, Conkeldurr
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Groudon
  • Dark Types – Hydreigon, Tyranitar (backup)

Recommended Moves

  • Machamp: Counter + Dynamic Punch
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere
  • Conkeldurr: Counter + Dynamic Punch

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Partly Cloudy (Normal)
Best for you: Cloudy (boosts Fighting).

Team Suggestions

  • A full Fighting team clears it very easily.
  • Ground types can be used as secondary support.

Rewards

XL Candy, bonus raid items, and Meowth catch chance.
