Moves & Threats

Ampharos common fast moves are Charge Beam or Volt Switch. Charged moves include Focus Blast or Power Gem or Trailblaze and Zap Cannon or Thunder or Thunder Punch and Brutal Swing

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Cloudy weather to boost Fairy types counters

Sunny weather to boost Ground types counters

Snow weather to boost Ice type counters

Windy weather to boost Dragon type counters

Battle Tips

  • Prioritize multi bar water type charge moves.
  • If weather is sunny, faint rate change - bring more water type or ground type attackers
  • Bring a squad of high water type attackers.
  • Use dodge sparingly - focus on sustained DPS and shields if in a smaller group.
