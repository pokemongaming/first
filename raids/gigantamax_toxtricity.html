Gigantamax Toxtricity Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Toxtricity is an Electric/Poison raid boss with strong special attacks. This guide lists counters, weather benefits, and team strategies.

Best Counters

  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Rhyperior, Excadrill
  • Psychic Types – Mewtwo, Alakazam, Espeon
  • Rock Types – Groudon, Terrakion

Recommended Moves

  • Garchomp: Mud Shot + Earth Power
  • Excadrill: Mud-Slap + Drill Run
  • Mewtwo: Confusion + Psystrike

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain (Electric) and Cloudy (Poison)
Best to fight in: Sunny or Clear (boosts Ground).

Team Suggestions

  • Ground types completely wall Toxtricity’s Electric attacks.
  • A full Ground-type team clears the raid fast.

Rewards

XL Candy, bonus Poké Balls, Charged/Elite TMs, and a chance to catch Toxtricity.
