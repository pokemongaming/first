Mega Dragonite Raid Guide

Mega Dragonite is a powerful Dragon/Flying boss with extremely high damage output. Its double weakness to Ice makes Ice-type Pokémon essential.

Weaknesses

Ice (Double Damage), Dragon, Fairy, Rock

FAQ

Can Mega Dragonite be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Dragonite has a distinctive green color compared to its normal orange appearance.

Is Mega Dragonite hard to defeat?

It can be challenging without Ice-type counters, but manageable with proper team setup.

Can it be duoed?

Yes, strong Ice teams at high trainer levels can complete the raid with two players.

Best Mega support?

A Mega Ice-type Pokémon boosts team-wide Ice damage significantly.

What is Mega Dragonite’s exclusive move?

Mega Dragonite can use moves like Dragon Claw and Hurricane, dealing massive damage while taking advantage of its dual typing.

What makes Mega Dragonite unique?

Its dragonic Mega form, balanced stats, and combination of Dragon/Flying typing make Mega Dragonite visually impressive and strategically versatile in Mega Raid Battles.
