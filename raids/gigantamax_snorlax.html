Gigantamax Snorlax Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Snorlax is a Normal-type raid boss with extremely high HP. Fighting types are the most effective counters.

Best Counters

  • Fighting Types – Machamp, Lucario, Conkeldurr
  • Steel Types – Metagross (backup)
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Groudon (backup)

Recommended Moves

  • Machamp: Counter + Dynamic Punch
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere
  • Conkeldurr: Counter + Dynamic Punch

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Partly Cloudy (Normal)
Best for you: Cloudy (boosts Fighting).

Team Suggestions

  • Full Fighting-type team clears Gigantamax Snorlax effectively.
  • Bring bulky Pokémon to survive heavy charged attacks.

Rewards

XL Candy, bonus raid items, and Snorlax capture chance.
