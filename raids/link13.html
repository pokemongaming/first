Tapu Koko Overview

Tapu Koko is an Electric/Fairy-type Legendary Pokémon, known as the guardian of Melemele Island. It is highly versatile in Pokémon GO raids and PvP battles due to its strong Electric STAB moves and Fairy-type coverage, making it a valuable asset for both offensive and supportive roles.

Type & Role in Battles

Tapu Koko's dual Electric/Fairy typing makes it effective against Dragon-types and Water-types in raids. Its high-speed Electric moves allow it to charge its attacks quickly, giving an advantage in PvP battles and raid DPS.

  • ✔ Strong against Dragon, Water, and Flying-types
  • ✔ Weak to Ground-types, resistances include Fighting, Fairy, and Electric moves

Tapu Koko Fast Moves Performance

Tapu Koko’s fast moves are essential for generating energy quickly and enabling charged moves. Understanding the strengths and weaknesses of each fast move will help you optimize your raid and PvP strategy.

Thunder Shock

Thunder Shock is Tapu Koko’s primary fast move for raids. It generates energy quickly, allowing frequent use of charged moves like Wild Charge. Its low damage per hit is compensated by rapid energy gain and STAB (Same-Type Attack Bonus).

  • ✔ Generates 8 energy per attack
  • ✔ Works best in sustained DPS scenarios
  • ✔ Ideal for raids against Dragon, Water, and Flying-types
  • ✔ Fast energy allows multiple charged attacks in a short time

Zen Headbutt

Zen Headbutt is slightly slower but provides moderate damage and PvP utility. While it doesn’t generate as much energy as Thunder Shock, it can apply pressure in PvP battles and against opponents where Psychic moves are advantageous.

  • ✔ Generates 7 energy per attack
  • ✔ Slightly higher damage per hit than Thunder Shock
  • ✔ Useful in PvP for fast shield baiting and coverage
  • ✔ Situational in raids; less effective than Thunder Shock

  • ✔ Damage: 80 | Energy: 45
