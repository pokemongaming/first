Mega Mawile Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

← Back to All Raid Guides

Mega Mawile is a Steel and Fairy-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. While it has strong defensive typing with several resistances, it also has two major weaknesses: Fire-type and Ground-type attacks. Trainers using powerful Fire or Ground Pokémon can defeat Mega Mawile efficiently in raids.

This raid guide covers everything you need to know to defeat Mega Mawile, including its weaknesses, best counters, movesets, catch CP ranges, shiny availability, and effective raid strategies for both small groups and larger teams.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Mawile, Mega Mawile is a powerful Steel and Fairy-type Pokémon that appears in Mega Raids in Pokémon GO. Although Mawile is normally a relatively small Pokémon, its Mega Evolution dramatically increases its power and raid difficulty.

Mega Mawile benefits from a defensive typing that resists many common attack types such as Dragon, Dark, and Bug. However, it has two key weaknesses that trainers can exploit: Fire and Ground. Using strong Fire-type attackers like Reshiram or Mega Charizard can make this raid significantly easier.

In this guide, we will explain Mega Mawile’s stats, weaknesses, best raid counters, catch CP ranges, and helpful strategies to help trainers defeat the raid boss and collect valuable Mega Energy.

Mega Mawile is a Steel / Fairy-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes a very strong attacker and provides damage bonuses to Steel-type and Fairy-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Mawile is useful because:

  • It is excellent against Dragon, Dark, and Fairy-type raid bosses
  • It has very high attack for a Mega Pokémon

🌀 Mega Mawile Moveset

Mawile common fast moves are Astonish or Fire Fang or Ice Fang or Bite or Fairy Wind. Charged moves include Vise Grip or Power-Up Punch and Iron Head or Play Rough.

Threat: Mega Mawile can combine Steel and Fairy moves, making it tough against mixed teams.

Tip: Avoid using Dragon or Poison Pokémon as they take reduced damage and risk being KO’d faster.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Mawile and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 1,290-1,360 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 1,613-1,700 CP
  • Boosted by: Snowy & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Mawile

  1. Fire types are the fastest way to clear this raid.
  2. Watch out for Play Rough — it hits very hard.
  3. Dodging saves revives and time.
  4. 2 strong trainers can defeat it easily.

Can Mega Mawile Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Mawile after defeating Mega Mawile in a raid. Although the Mega form itself cannot be caught, the Mawile encountered at the end of the raid can be shiny.

Shiny Mawile has a distinctive golden-yellow body compared to the normal version’s darker color. Because shiny Pokémon are rare, many trainers participate in multiple Mega Mawile raids to increase their chances of encountering one.

Mega Raids generally have higher shiny rates compared to wild encounters, making them a great opportunity for collectors looking to add Shiny Mawile to their Pokémon GO collection.

📌 Is Mega Mawile Worth Raiding?

Mega Mawile is worth raiding mainly for Pokédex completion and for boosting Fairy-type Pokémon in raids, but it is not a top-tier Mega compared to others.

FAQ

Can Mega Mawile be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes. Shiny Mawile has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Mawile’s exclusive move?

Mega Mawile can use moves like Play Rough and Iron Head, dealing massive Fairy and Steel-type damage to opponents in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Mawile difficult to defeat?

It has very high attack and decent bulk but moderate speed. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient and safe battles.

What makes Mega Mawile unique?

Its dual Steel/Fairy typing, massive jaw Mega form, and powerful offensive stats make Mega Mawile visually striking and strategically dangerous in Max Raid Battles.

What makes Mega Mawile strong?

Its high attack, strong resistances, and Meteor Mash make it a top-tier raid and PvP option.

🏁 Conclusion

Mega Mawile is a fragile but dangerous Mega Raid boss. Because of its double weakness to Fire, it can be defeated very quickly with the right team.
