← Back to All Raid Guides

Suicune is a Water-type Legendary Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Known for its high Defense and Stamina, it can be tough to defeat without the right counters. This guide covers everything you need to know about Suicune raids, including weaknesses, best counters, movesets, catch CP, weather effects, and raid strategies.

Introduction

Suicune is one of the Legendary “Beast Trio” Pokémon in Pokémon GO, known for its high Defense and overall durability. As a Water-type, it is weak to Electric- and Grass-type attacks, making Pokémon like Raikou, Zapdos, and Venusaur ideal counters for this raid.

This guide is designed to help both beginners and experienced trainers defeat Suicune efficiently. You’ll learn which Pokémon are best to use, what moves to expect, optimal team setups, catch CP ranges, and tips to maximize your chances for a shiny encounter.

💡 Expert Insight

Suicune is known for its high defensive stats, making it one of the bulkier raid bosses. Although it lacks heavy damage output, its endurance can prolong battles.

Electric and Grass-type attackers are the best choices for maintaining steady damage throughout the fight.

Moves & Threats

Suicune common fast moves are Extrasensory or Water Gun or Ice Fang or Snarl. Charged moves include Water Pulse or Scald or Ice Beam or Hydro Pump or Bubble Beam. Elite moves include Hidden Power.

Danger: Ice Beam can be especially dangerous for many Grass counters, so be ready to dodge charged moves when possible.

Hydro Pump is Suicune’s most dangerous charged move and can heavily damage even bulky counters. Ice Beam is also threatening to Grass-type Pokémon, so be ready to dodge if needed.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,627-1,704 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,033-2,130 CP
  • Boosted by: Rainy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Best Team Composition

For the best results against Suicune, your team should include:

  • 3–4 strong Electric-type attackers
  • 1–2 Grass-type attackers for additional DPS
  • 1 Mega Pokémon to boost team damage

Using Mega Sceptile, Mega Venusaur, or Mega Manectric will significantly increase your team’s overall damage output.

Weather Boost

Suicune is boosted in Rainy weather, which makes it stronger and harder to defeat.

Your counters are boosted in:

  • Rainy weather (Electric)
  • Sunny weather (Grass)

How Many Trainers Are Needed?

Catch Tips

  • Use Golden Razz Berries for best catch chance
  • Go for curve Excellent throws
  • Wait for Suicune’s attack animation to finish before throwing
  • Aim slightly higher because Suicune stands far back

Battle Tips

  • Electric types are the safest and most consistent counters
  • Grass types deal excellent damage but are weak to Ice Beam
  • Dodge Hydro Pump if your team is small
  • Always bring at least one Mega Pokémon for damage boost

Is Suicune Worth Raiding?

Suicune is not a top-tier Water-type attacker for raids, but it is very useful for PvP (especially in Ultra League) and is an important Pokémon for Pokédex and shiny collectors.

Can Suicune Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Suicune can be encountered as a Shiny Pokémon after defeating it in a Legendary Raid. The shiny variant has a slightly darker blue coloration, making it visually distinct and highly sought after by collectors.

Shiny Pokémon are rare, so you may need to complete multiple raids to encounter one. Participating in raids with a group can increase your chances of receiving raid rewards, including a shiny Suicune.

FAQ

Can Suicune be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Shiny Suicune is available in Pokémon GO. It features a beautiful teal-blue color and white mane.

What moves does Suicune use in raids?

Suicune can use moves like Hydro Pump, Bubble Beam, and Ice Beam, dealing powerful Water and Ice-type damage to opponents.

Is Suicune difficult to defeat?

Due to its very high defensive stats, Suicune can take a long time to defeat. Strong Electric or Grass-type attackers help finish the raid faster.

What makes Suicune unique?

Its elegant design, legendary status, and incredible defensive strength make Suicune one of the most iconic Water-type raid bosses in Pokémon GO.

Conclusion

Suicune is a bulky and defensive raid boss, but with strong Electric and Grass-type attackers, it can be defeated without much trouble. Build a team with high DPS counters, use a Mega Pokémon, and you’ll be able to secure this Legendary Beast with ease.
