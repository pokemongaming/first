Introduction

Origin Giratina is the Origin Forme of the Legendary Pokémon Giratina, appearing in Pokémon GO raids as a formidable Ghost/Dragon-type boss. Its high stats and powerful moves make it a challenging opponent for most trainers.

Despite its strength, Origin Giratina has clear type weaknesses that can be exploited. Dragon, Ghost, Ice, Dark, and Fairy-type attacks are particularly effective. Trainers using optimized counters can defeat it quickly and maximize rewards.

This guide provides detailed information about Origin Giratina’s raid mechanics, weaknesses, recommended counters, movesets, catch CP ranges, shiny availability, and strategic tips to help both new and experienced trainers succeed in raids.

Moves & Threats

Origin Giratina common fast moves are Shadow Claw or Dragon Tail. Charged moves include Ominous Wind or Shadow Ball or Dragon Pulse. Elite moves include Shadow Force.

High-Threat Moves: Shadow Force, Shadow Claw, and Dragon Breath deal strong damage. Dodging charged attacks when possible will improve survival.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,018-2,105 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,523-2,631 CP
  • Boosted by: Fog or Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Foggy / Windy weather: boosts Ghost/Dragon type attackers. Rainy weather : boosts Dark type attackers. Cloudy weather : boosts Fairy type attackers. Snowy weather : boosts Ice type attackers.

Origin Giratina can be challenging with a weaker team, but balanced selections of Dragon, Ghost, Ice, and Dark provide strong overall coverage.

Battle Strategy Tips

  1. Start with your highest DPS Pokémon like Mega Rayquaza or Eternatus to quickly reduce HP.
  2. Dodge charged moves like Shadow Ball and Shadow Force when possible to increase your team’s longevity.
  3. Mix Ghost and Dragon counters to exploit multiple weaknesses.
  4. If your Pokémon faints, rejoin quickly — every bit of damage helps under the time limit.

When to avoid

Avoid using fragile Fire or Grass attackers that will faint quickly to Origin Giratina’s strong Fire- and Ground-type moves. Also be cautious with Ice attackers if Origin Giratina carries Rock coverage.

Rewards

  • Chance to catch Origin Giratina (with boosted IVs)
  • Rare Candy & XL Candy

Why Raid Origin Giratina?

Defeating it grants XL Candy, rare candy, and a chance for high-IV or shiny Giratina. Origin form offers a new strategic twist, requiring trainers to adjust their usual raid tactics.

Shiny Origin Giratina

Yes! Trainers can encounter a Shiny Giratina after defeating the Origin Giratina raid boss. While the raid itself cannot be caught, the Giratina encountered afterward has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Origin Giratina has a distinct darker color palette compared to the regular version, making it a highly desirable Pokémon for collectors and competitive players.

The shiny rate in Legendary Raids is higher than wild encounters, so participating in multiple raids increases the chance of obtaining a shiny Origin Giratina.

FAQ

Can Origin Giratina be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Giratina body turns teal with altered accent colors.

What moves does Origin Giratina use in raids?

Origin Giratina can use moves like Shadow Claw, Shadow Force, Dragon Claw, and Draco Meteor, dealing massive Ghost and Dragon-type damage to opponents.

Is Origin Giratina difficult to defeat?

With very high stats and a versatile movepool, Origin Giratina can be challenging. Using multiple type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient raids.

What makes Origin Giratina unique?

Its Ghost/Dragon typing, Origin Forme design, and powerful signature moves make Origin Giratina visually striking and strategically dominant in Max Raid Battles.

Conclusion

Origin Forme Giratina remains one of the most iconic and challenging Legendary bosses in Pokémon GO. By using strong Ghost, Dragon, Dark, Ice, and Fairy counters with optimized teams and dodging key moves, you can defeat it faster and more reliably. With the right strategy and weather boosts, this Legendary raid becomes manageable even for mid-level trainers.

To beat Origin Giratina efficiently, assemble a team of Dragon, Fairy, and Ghost Pokémon. Take advantage of weather boosts, plan for shield management, and focus on high-DPS movesets.
