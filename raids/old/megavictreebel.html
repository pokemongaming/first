Mega Victreebel Raid Guide – Pokémon GO (2026)

Overview

Mega Victreebel is a Grass/Poison-type raid boss in Pokémon GO. It has a combination of fast and charged moves that can be dangerous if not countered properly. This guide provides the best counters, tiered strategies, and tips to defeat it efficiently.

Weaknesses

  • Weak to: Fire, Psychic, Ice, Flying types
  • Resistant to: Grass, Electric, Fighting, Poison

Best Counters

Meta Counters

Pokémon Moves Why It Works
Charizard Fire Spin + Blast Burn Strong Fire-type STAB moves exploit Mega Victreebel's Grass weakness for maximum DPS.
Mewtwo Confusion + Psystrike High Psychic damage bypasses Victreebel's Poison resistance, very effective overall.
Staraptor Wing Attack + Brave Bird Flying moves hit Grass-type hard; fast charged move allows quick energy generation.

Budget / Free Player Counters

Pokémon Moves Why It Works
Flareon Fire Spin + Overheat Accessible Fire-type attacker for beginners, decent DPS against Grass type.
Alakazam Confusion + Psychic High Psychic output with relatively low investment; can solo smaller raids.
Pidgeot Wing Attack + Brave Bird Easy-to-get Flying counter; fast charge and safe against Grass moves.

Strategy Tips

  • Bring multiple Fire and Psychic-type Pokémon to handle Mega Victreebel quickly.
  • Coordinate with friends for larger raids to avoid solo struggles.
  • Use weather-boosted counters (Sunny for Fire, Windy for Flying) to maximize damage.
  • Watch out for Sludge Bomb – it deals heavy damage if unprepared.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Boosts Fire-type counters
  • Windy: Boosts Flying-type counters
  • Partly Cloudy: Boosts Normal and Rock types, less effective here

FAQ

Can Mega Victreebel be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, you can encounter a shiny Victreebel after the raid, which can later Mega Evolve if a Mega form becomes available.

Is Mega Victreebel difficult to defeat?

It deals consistent damage but is relatively easy to defeat due to its multiple weaknesses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient and safe battles.

Can it be duoed?

Yes, high-level Fire-type teams can defeat it with two trainers.

Best Mega boost support?

Mega Fire-type Pokémon boost team-wide Fire damage.

Does Mega Victreebel keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

What makes Mega Victreebel unique?

Its fast and aggressive Mega form, Grass/Poison typing, and strong offensive stats make Mega Victreebel visually striking and strategically valuable in raid battles.

What is Mega Victreebel’s exclusive move?

Mega Victreebel can use moves like Leaf Blade and Sludge Bomb, dealing massive Grass and Poison-type damage to opponents in Mega Raid Battles.
