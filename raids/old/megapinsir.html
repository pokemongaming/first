Mega Pinsir Raid Guide – Pokémon GO (2026)

Overview

Mega Pinsir is a Bug-type raid boss in Pokémon GO. With powerful moves like Close Combat and X-Scissor, it can be challenging without the right counters. This guide will help you choose the best Pokémon, plan your team, and maximize your raid efficiency.

Weaknesses

  • Weak to: Fire, Flying, Rock types
  • Resistant to: Fighting, Grass, Bug

Best Counters

Meta Counters

Pokémon Moves Why It Works
Charizard Fire Spin + Blast Burn Strong Fire-type moves exploit Pinsir's Bug weakness and deal high DPS quickly.
Tyranitar Bite + Crunch / Stone Edge Rock-type moves hit Bug-type hard; high CP ensures survivability against Close Combat.
Staraptor Wing Attack + Brave Bird Flying moves target Pinsir's weakness, with fast charge for efficient raids.

Budget / Free Player Counters

Pokémon Moves Why It Works
Flareon Fire Spin + Overheat Accessible Fire-type counter for beginners; decent damage output against Bug-types.
Pidgeot Wing Attack + Brave Bird Easy-to-get Flying counter; handles Pinser's Bug attacks effectively.
Onix Rock Throw + Stone Edge Low-cost Rock-type counter; can deal significant damage when maxed.

Strategy Tips

  • Bring multiple Fire and Rock-type Pokémon to deal with Pinsir efficiently.
  • Time charge moves to avoid Pinsir's Close Combat when possible.
  • Weather boosts help – Sunny for Fire, Windy for Flying, partly cloudy for Rock.
  • Coordinate with friends for optimal raid completion and to avoid solo struggles.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Boosts Fire-type counters
  • Windy: Boosts Flying-type counters
  • Partly Cloudy: Boosts Rock/Normal types

FAQ

Can Mega Pinsir be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes. Shiny Pinsir has been released in raids or events.

Can Mega Pinsir be soloed?

With maxed-out Rock-type counters and optimal dodging, it may be possible, but small groups are recommended.

What is the biggest threat?

Flying-type charge moves under Windy weather can deal significant damage.

Best Mega for support?

A Rock-type Mega evolution boosts overall team performance significantly.
