Mega Latios is a powerful Dragon and Psychic-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. Because of this, defeating Mega Latios usually requires multiple trainers and well-prepared counters.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Latios, Mega Latios is one of the most challenging Mega Raid bosses in Pokémon GO. With its Dragon and Psychic typing, it has access to extremely powerful Dragon-type attacks that can deal massive damage during raids.

Because of its high attack stat and strong moves, Mega Latios raids are usually difficult to complete with small groups. Trainers will need powerful Dragon-type, Dark-type, Fairy-type, or Ice-type counters to defeat this raid boss efficiently.

This guide explains Mega Latios weaknesses, the best Pokémon to use as counters, optimal raid strategies, catch CP values, and other important information to help players defeat Mega Latios and obtain valuable Mega Energy rewards.

🌀 Mega Latios Moveset

Latios common fast moves are Zen Headbutt (moderate damage) or Dragon Breath (fast energy gain). Charged moves include Solar Beam (rare but dangerous if it appears) or Aura Sphere or Psychic (very strong STAB) and Dragon Claw (spammable high DPS). Elite moves include Luster Purge (slower but powerful).

Pro tip: Mega Latios cycles fast moves rapidly, so shield timing and dodging are key. Use resistant Ice or Dark Pokémon to withstand Psychic attacks while dealing strong DPS.

Most dangerous move combos: Dragon Breath + Dragon Claw or Psychic can deal heavy, fast damage — dodging charged moves when possible increases survivability.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Latios gets a weather boost in:

  • Windy → boosts Dragon and Psychic

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Snow → boosts Ice
  • Windy → boosts Dragon
  • Cloudy → boosts Fairy
  • Fog → boosts Dark and Ghost

Dragon-type counters like Mega Rayquaza and Eternatus provide the **highest damage output**, while Ghost and Dark types add coverage against Psychic moves. Ice and Fairy types help round out your team.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Latios

  1. Start with your highest DPS Dragon counter (e.g., Mega Rayquaza) to quickly reduce health.
  2. Dodge charged moves like Psychic and Solar Beam to preserve your attackers.
  3. Mix in Ghost or Dark counters if Mega Latios uses many Psychic moves.
  4. Rejoin the raid quickly if fainted — every bit of extra damage helps under the time limit.

Can Mega Latios Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Latios after defeating Mega Latios in a raid battle. Although Mega Latios itself cannot be caught, players will encounter the regular Latios after the raid, which may appear in its shiny form.

Shiny Latios has a greenish-teal body color compared to the normal blue version, making it a very popular shiny Pokémon among collectors.

The shiny rate in Legendary and Mega Raids is higher than wild encounters, so completing multiple Mega Latios raids increases the chances of finding a shiny Latios.

📌 Is Mega Latios Worth Raiding?

Mega Latios is worth raiding mainly for its Mega Evolution and Pokédex entry. It is a powerful Dragon-type Mega Pokémon but requires a lot of Mega Energy to maintain.
