Mega Gallade Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

← Back to All Raid Guides

Mega Gallade is a powerful Psychic and Fighting-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. With high attack stats and strong moves like Close Combat and Psychic, Mega Gallade can be a challenging opponent for trainers who are not prepared with the right counters.

Because of its dual typing, Mega Gallade is weak to Fairy, Flying, and Ghost-type attacks. Using strong Pokémon of these types can make the raid much easier and allow smaller groups of trainers to defeat it quickly.

This raid guide covers everything you need to know to defeat Mega Gallade, including its weaknesses, best counters, CP ranges, shiny availability, weather boosts, and raid strategy.

Introduction

Mega Gallade is the Mega Evolution of Gallade and is known for its impressive offensive capabilities in Pokémon GO. With a combination of Psychic and Fighting typing, Mega Gallade has access to a diverse moveset that can threaten many common raid attackers.

Despite its power, Mega Gallade has several exploitable weaknesses. Fairy, Flying, and Ghost-type Pokémon are particularly effective against it, allowing trainers to deal heavy damage during raids.

Participating in Mega Gallade raids not only provides the opportunity to earn valuable rewards but also allows trainers to collect Mega Energy required to Mega Evolve Gallade for use in raids, gyms, and PvP battles.

This guide will help both beginner and experienced trainers prepare the best team to defeat Mega Gallade efficiently and maximize their chances of catching a high-IV Gallade after the raid.

🧬 About Mega Gallade in Pokémon GO

Mega Gallade is a Psychic / Fighting-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes one of the strongest Fighting-type attackers in the game and provides damage bonuses to Psychic-type and Fighting-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Gallade is useful because:

  • It boosts Fighting-type and Psychic-type attackers
  • It has very high attack stat
  • It is excellent against Dark, Steel, Ice, and Normal-type raid bosses

🌀 Mega Gallade Moveset

Gallade common fast moves are Low Kick or Confusion (Fast psychic damage and builds energy quickly)or Psycho Cut (Fast psychic damage and builds energy quickly) or Charm. Charged moves include Close Combat (High Fighting-type damage that pierces defenses) or Leaf Blade (Adds variety and can surprise counters) or Psychic. Elite moves include Synchronoise.

Most Dangerous Moves: Close Combat and Psychic hit extremely hard.

Pro tip: Low-defense Pokémon, especially pure Physic or Fighting types, are vulnerable to Mega Gallade’s charged attacks.

🎯 Rewards & Catch CP

In Mega Raids, you catch a normal Gallade and also earn Mega Energy.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,485-2,583 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 3,106-3,230 CP
  • Boosted by: Windy & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Gallade

  1. Flying, Fairy, and Ghost types are the best counters.
  2. Avoid using Fighting or Psychic Pokémon.
  3. Dodge Close Combat to keep your attackers alive longer.
  4. 2–3 strong trainers can defeat Mega Gallade easily.

Pro tip: Mega Gallade is fast and punishing—focus on shields and timing charged attacks for maximum survival.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 strong trainers → Possible
  • 3–4 trainers → Comfortable clear
  • Low-level players → 5+ recommended

Can Mega Gallade Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Gallade after defeating Mega Gallade in a Mega Raid. While Mega Gallade itself cannot be caught directly, players will encounter Gallade after the raid battle, which has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Gallade features a noticeable color difference compared to the regular version, making it highly desirable for collectors and shiny hunters.

The shiny rate for Mega Raids is generally higher than standard wild encounters, so completing multiple Mega Gallade raids increases your chances of finding a shiny version.

📌 Is Mega Gallade Worth Raiding?

Its Psychic/Fighting typing teaches the importance of double weakness exploitation, which is critical for advanced raids.

FAQ

Can Mega Gallade be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Gallade has been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Gallade’s exclusive move?

Mega Gallade can use moves like Psycho Cut and Close Combat, dealing massive Psychic and Fighting-type damage to opponents in Max Raid Battles.

What makes Mega Gallade unique?

Its elegant Mega form, combination of Psychic/Fighting typing, and fast, powerful moves make Mega Gallade visually striking and strategically dangerous in Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Gallade difficult to defeat?

It has very high attack and decent speed but moderate defenses. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient battles in raids.

Does Mega Gallade keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

🏁 Conclusion

Mega Gallade is dangerous but not bulky. With proper Flying, Fairy, or Ghost-type teams, it will go down quickly.
