🧬 About Mega Sceptile in Pokémon GO

Mega Sceptile is the Mega Evolution of Sceptile. While normal Sceptile is a pure Grass-type Pokémon, Mega Sceptile becomes a Grass/Dragon-type Pokémon after Mega Evolving.

This typing gives Mega Sceptile more resistances but also introduces new weaknesses. It also gains a big boost in attack, making it one of the best Grass-type Mega Pokémon in the game.

Using Mega Sceptile in raids gives bonus damage to:

  • Grass-type Pokémon
  • Dragon-type Pokémon

Why Mega Sceptile Is Dangerous

Mega Sceptile hits very hard with fast Grass and Dragon-type attacks, but its defenses are relatively low. If you bring the wrong Pokémon, it can quickly wipe your team. However, if you exploit its many weaknesses, the fight becomes very easy.

🌀 Mega Sceptile Moveset

Sceptile common fast moves are Fury Cutter or Bullet Seed. Charged moves include Aerial Ace or Earthquake or Leaf Blade or Dragon Claw or Breaking Swipe. Elite moves include Frenzy Plant.

Threat: Frenzy Plant and Draco Meteor deal huge burst damage, especially to unshielded Pokémon.

Tip: Avoid relying on Water-types alone—they take neutral damage from Dragon moves.

🎯 Catch CP (100% IV)

After defeating Mega Sceptile, you will catch a normal Sceptile.

  • Perfect (L20): 2,224-2,317 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,780-2,897 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Sceptile gets a weather boost in:

  • Sunny weather (boosts Grass)
  • Windy weather (boosts Dragon)

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Snowy → Ice-types
  • Cloudy → Fairy-types
  • Windy → Dragon & Flying-types

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Mega Sceptile

  • Start the battle with your strongest Ice-type Pokémon.
  • Dodge charged moves if possible — Mega Sceptile hits hard but faints quickly.
  • Rejoin the battle immediately if your team faints.
  • Keep Ice attackers in the first slots for maximum DPS.
  • If using a Mega, choose one that boosts Ice, Fairy, or Dragon types.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 high-level trainers → Easy duo with Ice-type teams
  • 3–4 trainers → Very comfortable clear
  • Low-level players → Bring at least 5–6 trainers

Can Mega Sceptile Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Trainers can encounter a Shiny Sceptile after defeating Mega Sceptile in a raid. While Mega Sceptile itself cannot be caught, the regular Sceptile that appears post-raid has a chance of being shiny.

The shiny version has a slightly darker green coloration compared to the normal Sceptile. Participation in multiple raids increases the chance of encountering a shiny Pokémon.

Collectors and competitive players alike value shiny Pokémon, so completing Mega Sceptile raids is highly rewarding.

📌 Is Mega Sceptile Worth Raiding?

Mega Sceptile raids are great for farming Mega Energy and testing your Ice/Fire Pokémon counters.

PvP Relevance

While Sceptile isn’t top tier in PvP, it’s a fun spice pick. Mega Sceptile shines more in raids and PvE content.

Tips for Trainers

  • Dodge Hydro Pump and Crunch to extend battle time.
  • Coordinate Mega boosts with your raid group.
  • Raid during weather that benefits your counters.
