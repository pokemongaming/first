Mega Abomasnow Overview

Mega Abomasnow is the Mega Evolution of Abomasnow, an Ice/Grass-type Pokémon. Known as the “Frost Tree Pokémon,” it combines Ice-type attacks with Grass-type versatility, making it a powerful raid boss in Pokémon GO. Mega Abomasnow excels in high-damage output against Water- and Flying-types while maintaining solid resistances to Grass and Electric moves.

Mega Evolution temporarily boosts Abomasnow's attack and defense stats, making it a formidable opponent in raids. Trainers can expect it to be challenging to defeat without proper counters and strategies.

  • ✔ Type: Ice / Grass
  • ✔ Role: Raid Boss
  • ✔ Mega Evolution Boost: Increased Attack & Defense
  • ✔ Weaknesses: Fire, Fighting, Rock, Steel
  • ✔ Resistances: Grass, Water, Electric

💡 Pro Tip: Mega Abomasnow is most effective when combined with Ice-type counters for Water- and Flying-type Pokémon. Focus on exploiting its vulnerabilities for faster raid completions.

What is the best counter for Mega Abomasnow?

Mega Abomasnow itself cannot be caught, but the Abomasnow you encounter after the raid can be shiny. Mega Raid shiny rates are typically higher than standard encounters.
