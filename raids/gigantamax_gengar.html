Gigantamax Gengar Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Gengar is a Ghost/Poison-type raid boss with high attack damage. Dark, Ground, and Psychic attackers work best.

Best Counters

  • Dark Types – Tyranitar, Hydreigon, Darkrai
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Excadrill, Groudon
  • Psychic Types – Mewtwo, Espeon, Alakazam

Recommended Moves

  • Darkrai: Snarl + Shadow Ball
  • Tyranitar: Bite + Crunch
  • Mewtwo: Confusion + Psystrike

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Fog (Ghost), Cloudy (Poison)
Best weather: Windy for Psychic attackers.

Team Suggestions

  • Dark attackers give the safest clears.
  • Psychic attackers deal high burst damage.

Rewards

XL Candy, rare items, and Gengar capture chance.
