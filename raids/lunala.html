Introduction

Lunala, the Moone Pokémon, is a Legendary Psychic/Ghost-type raid boss that poses a real challenge to trainers in Pokémon GO. Its combination of typing and high CP means that battles can be tough for unprepared teams.

However, Lunala has clear weaknesses to Dark and Ghost-type attacks, making Pokémon with these types the most effective counters. This guide will walk you through everything from weakness analysis, best counters, and movesets to raid strategies that make defeating Lunala a straightforward task.

Whether you are a solo trainer aiming to maximize rewards or part of a large raid group, this guide provides all the information you need to efficiently defeat Lunala.

Difficulty

Small groups must coordinate shields and timing, whereas larger groups can overwhelm it with multiple high-DPS counters. Its Ghost typing makes dodging and move selection critical.

Moves & Threats

Lunala common fast moves are Shadow Claw or Air Slash or Confusion. Charged moves include Shadow Ball or Psychic or Future Sight and Moonblast.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,219-2,310 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,774-2,887 CP
  • Boosted by: Foggy & Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Foggy weather : boosts Dark and Ghost type attackers. Windy weather : boosts Lunala’s Psychic moves.

Can Lunala Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, after completing a Lunala raid, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Lunala. While Lunala itself is a raid boss and cannot be caught in its Mega or powered form, the Pokémon obtained after the raid may appear shiny.

Shiny Lunala has a slightly different coloration with lighter wing highlights and a darker body, making it a prized collectible for trainers. The shiny rate in Legendary raids is generally higher than normal wild encounters, so multiple raids increase the chance of getting one.

Use this opportunity to catch a Shiny Lunala and add it to your collection of Legendary Pokémon in Pokémon GO.

Why Raid Lunala?

Its combination of tanky HP and versatile attacks provides a strategic challenge, making team-building and raid coordination crucial. Capturing Lunala also offers powerful counters for future Legendary raids and PvP battles.

FAQ

Can Lunala be shiny in Pokémon GO?

No, Lunala shiny version is not available.

What is Lunala’s exclusive move?

Lunala can use the special move Moongeist Beam, which ignores stat changes and deals massive damage to the target in Max Raid Battles.

Is Lunala difficult to defeat?

It has very high special attack and HP. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures the battle is manageable and efficient.

What makes Lunala unique?

Its cosmic, bat-like design, Psychic/Ghost typing, and devastating Moongeist Beam make Lunala visually striking and strategically challenging in Max Raid Battles.

What makes Lunala strong?

Its high attack, strong resistances, and Meteor Mash make it a top-tier raid and PvP option.
