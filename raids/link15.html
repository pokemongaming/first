Mega Manectric Overview

Mega Manectric is the **Mega Evolution of Manectric**, introduced in special Pokémon GO events. As an **Electric-type Pokémon**, it excels at **high-speed attacks** and can **quickly deal damage** in both raids and PvP battles. Its Mega form boosts its **Attack and Speed stats**, making it a strong choice for trainers seeking fast and efficient Electric damage.

This guide covers **Stats, Movesets, PvP strategies, counters, event availability, and fun trivia** about Mega Manectric to give trainers a complete resource for maximizing its potential.
