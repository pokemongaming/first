Kartana Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Kartana is an Ultra Beast and Grass / Steel-type raid boss in Pokémon GO. Its unique typing gives it very few weaknesses, but careful selection of counters can make the battle much easier.

This guide covers Kartana’s best counters, weaknesses, movesets, CP ranges, raid strategy, shiny availability, and tips for both beginner and expert trainers.

Raid Boss: Kartana

Type: Grass / Steel

Raid Tier: Ultra Beast

Major Weakness: Fire, Fighting, Ground

Introduction

Kartana is an Ultra Beast with an uncommon Grass / Steel typing, making it a formidable opponent in Pokémon GO raids. Its high attack stats can threaten unprepared teams, and its dual typing gives it only a few effective weaknesses.

Despite being tough, Kartana can be defeated efficiently using Fire, Fighting, or Ground-type counters. This guide will help you select the best Pokémon, plan raid strategies, and understand its moves and CP to maximize your success.

Whether you are a solo player with high-level counters or part of a larger raid group, following this guide will help you take down Kartana and earn rewards quickly.

Moves & Threats

Kartana common fast moves are Fury Cutter or Razor Leaf and Air Slash. Charged moves include Aerial Ace or Night Slash or X-Scissor or Leaf Blade and Sacred Sword.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,010-2,101 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,512-2,626 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Snowy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Why Kartana Is Both Dangerous and Fragile

Kartana hits extremely hard with fast Grass-type attacks and charged moves like Leaf Blade, but its terrible bulk means it also faints extremely quickly. If your team uses strong Fire-type attackers, this raid often ends faster than most Tier 5 bosses.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Boosts Fire-type moves (BEST weather)
  • Rainy: Boosts Kartana’s Grass-type moves
  • Snow: Boosts Kartana’s Steel-type moves

In Sunny weather, Fire-type attackers become unbelievably powerful and Kartana will faint extremely fast. In Rainy or Snowy weather, Kartana becomes slightly more dangerous but is still very manageable.

Battle Strategy & Fight Flow

This is a pure DPS race. Start with your strongest Fire-type Pokémon and attack aggressively. Kartana will faint very quickly if you maintain pressure.

Dodging is optional because the fight is usually very short, but if Kartana has Leaf Blade, some Pokémon may faint fast. Just relobby and continue attacking.

Can Kartana Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Kartana after completing the raid battle. Shiny Kartana has a slightly lighter and brighter color palette compared to its normal version, making it highly sought after by collectors.

Shiny encounters in Ultra Beast raids are rare but more frequent than regular wild encounters, so repeated participation in Kartana raids will increase your chances of catching one.

How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 Trainers: Possible with top-tier Fire teams
  • 3 Trainers: Very easy and safe
  • 4+ Trainers: Guaranteed fast win

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using Water or Grass Pokémon (they are double resisted)
  • Not using Fire-type attackers
  • Bringing low-DPS tanky Pokémon
  • Overthinking survivability instead of DPS

FAQ

Is Kartana easier than Celesteela?

Yes. Kartana is extremely fragile, while Celesteela is extremely bulky.

What is Kartana’s exclusive move?

Kartana can use powerful moves like Leaf Blade, dealing massive damage with high critical-hit potential in raids.

What is the best Mega Pokémon to use?

Mega Charizard Y or Mega Blaziken are the best choices because they boost Fire-type damage for the whole team.

Can Kartana be shiny?

Yes, Shiny Kartana is available during special events.

Is Kartana difficult to defeat?

It has extremely high attack and speed but very low HP. Using type-advantage counters allows players to defeat it efficiently before it can strike repeatedly.

What makes Kartana unique?

Its paper-thin, razor-sharp body, extreme speed, and high attack stats make Kartana visually impressive and a formidable challenge in Max Raid Battles.

Conclusion

Kartana is one of the fastest Tier 5 raids if you bring Fire-type attackers. Its double weakness to Fire makes it extremely easy to farm efficiently with even small groups.
