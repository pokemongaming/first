Gigantamax Cinderace Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Cinderace is a pure Fire-type raid boss, known for very high offense and potentially devastating fire-based charged moves.

Best Counters

  • Water Types – Kyogre, Swampert, Blastoise
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Rhyperior, Excadrill
  • Rock Types – Tyranitar, Terrakion

Recommended Moves

  • Kyogre: Waterfall + Surf
  • Garchomp: Mud Shot + Earth Power
  • Tyranitar: Smack Down + Stone Edge

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Sunny weather
Best for you: Rain or Windy.

Team Suggestions

  • A full Water-type team is very strong.
  • Mix in Ground or Rock types for bulk + consistent DPS.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, fire-based items, and a capture opportunity for Cinderace.
