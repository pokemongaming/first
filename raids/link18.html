Kyogre Overview

Kyogre is a **Legendary Water-type Pokémon** and the mascot of **Pokémon Sapphire**. Known as the **Sea Basin Pokémon**, it has the ability to **expand oceans** and unleash devastating Water-type moves. Kyogre is a **tier 5 raid boss** in Pokémon GO, making it highly sought after for raids and PvP battles.

With its high **Attack and Special Attack stats**, Kyogre is an ideal choice for **Water-type offense**, dominating Fire-, Ground-, and Rock-type Pokémon in both PvE and PvP scenarios. Its shiny variant, a striking **purple-blue color**, is highly prized among collectors.

Kyogre Raid Guide – FAQs

Is Kyogre good in Pokémon GO?

Yes, Kyogre is one of the best Water-type attackers in Pokémon GO. It performs exceptionally well in raids and Master League PvP.

What is Kyogre weak against?

Kyogre is weak to Electric- and Grass-type moves. These are the best counters in raids.

How many players are needed to defeat Kyogre?

Typically, 4–6 high-level trainers are enough to defeat Kyogre in Tier 5 raids.

Can Kyogre be shiny?

Yes, Kyogre has a shiny variant with a purple color. The shiny rate is approximately 1 in 20 in raids.

What is the best moveset for Kyogre?

The best moveset for raids is Waterfall + Surf or Hydro Pump, while PvP builds may include Ice Beam or Blizzard.
