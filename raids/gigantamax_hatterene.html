Gigantamax Hatterene Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Gigantamax Hatterene is a powerful Psychic/Fairy-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. It can deal heavy damage with its charged moves and has a few key weaknesses that make it vulnerable. This guide covers the best counters, weaknesses, movesets, CP ranges, weather boosts, shiny availability, and raid strategies for both beginners and advanced trainers.

Introduction

Gigantamax Hatterene is one of the most unique Mega Raid bosses in Pokémon GO due to its dual Psychic and Fairy typing. Its Gigantamax form increases its size, power, and access to devastating moves that can challenge unprepared trainers.

Despite its power, Gigantamax Hatterene has a clear set of weaknesses, including Poison, Ghost, and Steel-type attacks. With the right counters and strategy, trainers can defeat this raid boss efficiently, collect rewards, and potentially encounter a shiny version of Hatterene.

Whether you’re a beginner looking for guidance or an experienced player aiming to maximize raid efficiency, this guide provides all the information needed to tackle Gigantamax Hatterene raids successfully.

Overview

Hatterene’s Gigantamax form amplifies its Psychic/Fairy toolkit and HP pool. Expect charged Psychic and Fairy moves (Psyshock/Psychic, Dazzling Gleam/Moonblast) and possibly coverage such as Shadow Ball or Thunderbolt in special events. Gigantamax HP changes the optimal approach from "burst and swap" to "sustained pressure + efficient shield usage." For raids, your best approach is to maximize super-effective hits (Dark, Ghost) while including solid neutral tanks to soak occasional heavy Psychic nukes.

Moves & Threats

Hatterene common fast moves are Psycho Cut or Confusion or Charm. Charged moves include Psyshock or Dazzling Gleam or Psychic and Power Whip. G-Max Smite not only delivers heavy damage but also leaves a damaging area for a short time — timing your swaps and using at least one bulky support reduces wipe risk.

Pro tip: Dazzling Gleam – high Fairy damage. Psychic – strong STAB coverage

Pro tip: Prioritize dodging Dazzling Gleam to survive longer in raids.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,540-1,616 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 1,925-2,020 CP
  • Boosted by: Windy & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Windy weather : boosts Psychic moves. Cloudy weather : boosts Fairy attackers. Snowy weather : boosts Steel moves. Foggy weather : boosts Ghost attackers.

Battle Tips

  • Lead with Ghost or Poison Pokémon for max damage.
  • Steel-types are good for sustained DPS
  • Watch out for Hatterene’s Dazzling Gleam and Psychic combination.

Size

Recommended: 15–20 trainers for comfortable clears with mixed player levels. With many Gigantamax / Dynamax counters, 10–14 can work. Under 8 trainers should be attempted only if all participants are high-level and coordinated with ideal counters and Dynamax/G-Max boosts.

Rewards

Reasons include Pokédex entry, strong Psychic / Fairy attacker, shiny chance, and XL Candy farming.

Max Battle Strategy

  • Lead with bulky Steel-types to build Max energy safely.
  • Coordinate Max activations during shield windows.
  • Ghost-types provide burst damage but require good timing.
  • Avoid Dragon-type attackers due to Fairy resistance.
  • Steel-type teams provide the safest overall approach.

Can Gigantamax Hatterene Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, after defeating Gigantamax Hatterene, players may encounter a Shiny Hatterene in the wild or as a raid reward. The shiny version has a distinct coloration compared to the regular Hatterene, making it highly sought after by collectors.

Shiny Pokémon are rare, so multiple raid attempts may be necessary to increase the chances of obtaining one. Be sure to participate in every raid you can to maximize your odds.

FAQ

What is Gigantamax Hatterene type and weakness?

Hatterene is a Psychic / Fairy type. It is weak to Poison, Ghost, Steel.

Is Gigantamax Hatterene difficult to defeat?

It has high special attack stats, but using Steel or Ghost-type counters allows you to defeat it efficiently.

What makes Gigantamax Hatterene unique?

Conclusion

Gigantamax Hatterene is a mechanically interesting raid boss: its Psychic/Fairy combo is predictable, but the G-Max residual damage forces teams to plan for survivability, not just pure DPS. Prioritize Poison and Steel Gigantamax picks, add Dynamax support for sustained windows, and include at least one high-TDO tank if your lobby is smaller. With the counters and tactics above, you’ll clear Hatterene efficiently and maximize your rewards.
