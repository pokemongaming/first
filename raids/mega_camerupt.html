Mega Camerupt Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Mega Camerupt is a Fire and Ground-type Mega Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. While it has powerful Fire-type attacks and high damage potential, it also has a major weakness: Water-type attacks deal double damage due to its Fire and Ground typing.

This Mega Camerupt raid guide covers everything trainers need to know to defeat it successfully, including its weaknesses, best counters, movesets, catch CP ranges, weather boosts, shiny availability, and expert raid strategies for both small and large raid groups.

Introduction

The Mega Evolution of Camerupt, Mega Camerupt is a powerful raid boss in Pokémon GO known for its strong Fire and Ground-type attacks. Its volcanic design reflects its battle style, as it can unleash devastating moves such as Earth Power and Overheat against opposing Pokémon.

Despite its offensive power, Mega Camerupt has a major vulnerability. Because it is both a Fire and Ground-type Pokémon, it takes extremely high damage from Water-type attacks. This makes Water Pokémon some of the best counters for defeating this raid boss quickly.

In this raid guide, trainers will learn about Mega Camerupt’s weaknesses, the best Pokémon to use against it, recommended raid strategies, and tips for maximizing rewards after the battle. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced trainer, this guide will help you defeat Mega Camerupt efficiently.

🧬 About Mega Camerupt in Pokémon GO

Mega Camerupt is a Fire / Ground-type Pokémon. When Mega Evolved, it becomes one of the strongest Fire-type attackers in the game and provides damage bonuses to Fire-type and Ground-type Pokémon in raids.

Mega Camerupt is useful because:

  • It boosts Fire-type and Ground-type attackers
  • It has extremely high attack stat
  • It is excellent against Steel, Bug, Ice, and Electric-type raid bosses

This makes Mega Camerupt extremely useful against Ice, Steel, Bug, Dark, and Grass-type raid bosses.

Why Water Types Dominate

Mega Camerupt’s Fire/Ground typing gives it a **quadruple weakness to Water** — meaning Water attacks deal *vastly increased damage*. Water attackers not only hit super-effectively but also resist many of Camerupt’s common moves, making the battle fast and efficient for prepared trainers.

Best Mega Evolutions

Recommended Megas to use against Mega Camerupt:

  • Swampert, Blastoise, Gyarados, Slowbro, Sharpedo – Water boost
  • Garchomp – Ground boost

Best Moveset

  • Fast: Rock Smash, Ember, Incinerate
  • Charged: Earthquake, Earth Power, Overheat, Solar Beam

Pro tip: Its Overheat and Earth Power can one-shot many attackers, and Solar Beam can brutally punish careless Water-types that fail to dodge. Even though it’s slow, every charged move is a potential knockout.

The combination of Incinerate and Earth Power hits many counters neutrally, but Water types resist both and exploit Camerupt’s glaring weaknesses.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Mega Camerupt gets a weather boost in:

  • Sunny / Clear → boosts Fire & Ground

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Rainy → boosts Water

If Mega Camerupt opens with a fast-charging special move (like Eruption), switch to a Water-type to soak the hit and retaliate quickly.

When to avoid

Avoid using fragile Fire or Grass attackers that will faint quickly to Camerupt’s strong Fire- and Ground-type moves. Also be cautious with Ice attackers if Camerupt carries Rock coverage.

Raid Strategy

  • Lead with a high-level Water attacker (Primal Kyogre or Mega Swampert) for maximum initial damage.
  • Focus on defeating shields quickly with Water DPS — Hydro Cannon / Origin Pulse are especially strong.
  • Dodge charged Earth Power to preserve your attackers’ HP.
  • If you lack top Water counters, supplement with Ground attackers to exploit Mega Camerupt’s secondary weakness.
  • Rejoin instantly if your team faints to maximize damage output within raid time.

Can Mega Camerupt Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, trainers have a chance to encounter a Shiny Camerupt after defeating Mega Camerupt in a raid battle. While Mega Camerupt itself cannot be caught directly, players will encounter the regular Camerupt after the raid, which has a chance of being shiny.

Shiny Camerupt has a darker red and black color scheme compared to the normal version, making it a popular collectible among Pokémon GO players.

Mega Raids typically have a higher shiny rate than wild encounters, so completing multiple raids increases your chances of obtaining a shiny Camerupt.

FAQ

Can Mega Camerupt be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Camerupt has not yet been released in raids or events.

What is Mega Camerupt’s exclusive move?

Mega Camerupt can use moves like Eruption and Earth Power, dealing massive Fire and Ground-type damage to opponents during Max Raid Battles.

Is Mega Camerupt difficult to defeat?

It has high special attack and decent bulk but low speed. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient and safe battles in raids.

What makes Mega Camerupt unique?

Its volcanic Mega form, dual Fire/Ground typing, and devastating moves make Mega Camerupt visually striking and strategically strong in Max Raid Battles.

Does Mega Camerupt keep its Mega form after the battle?

Mega Evolution lasts only during battles where Mega is enabled; it does not persist outside combat.

🏁 Conclusion

Mega Camerupt’s massive double weakness to Water makes this Mega Raid one of the more accessible challenges in Pokémon GO. With powerful Water counters — especially Primal Kyogre, Mega Swampert, and Shadow variants — you can clear this boss quickly even with a small group. Plan your team around Rainy weather boosts and Water movesets for the fastest wins.
