Mega Camerupt Raid Guide

Overview

Mega Camerupt is a Fire/Ground-type Pokémon with massive attack power. Prepare teams that exploit its weaknesses to Water and Ground.

Best Counters

  • Water Types – Kyogre, Swampert, Gyarados
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Rhyperior, Excadrill

Recommended Moves

  • Kyogre: Waterfall + Surf
  • Swampert: Mud Shot + Hydro Cannon

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Sunny weather
Best to fight in: Rain (boosts Water attackers).

Team Suggestions

  • Water-type teams melt Mega Camerupt extremely fast.
  • A group of 3–4 trainers is enough with strong Water Pokémon.

Rewards

Mega Energy, Candies, Charged TMs, and Camerupt catch rewards.
