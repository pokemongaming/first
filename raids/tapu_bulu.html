Tapu Bulu Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Tapu Bulu is a Grass and Fairy-type Legendary Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. It has notable resistances but is extremely vulnerable to Fire, Poison, and Steel-type attacks. This guide covers everything you need to know to defeat Tapu Bulu efficiently.

Learn the best counters, movesets, CP ranges, weather boosts, shiny availability, and expert raid strategies to ensure victory whether you are a beginner or an experienced trainer.

Introduction

Tapu Bulu is one of the four guardian deities of the Alola region in Pokémon GO, known for its Grass/Fairy typing. Its moveset includes powerful Grass and Fairy-type attacks, which can challenge unprepared teams.

Despite its strength, Tapu Bulu’s weaknesses to Fire, Poison, and Steel moves make it manageable for players with the right counters. This guide will provide detailed information on the best Pokémon to use, optimal raid strategies, and tips to maximize your rewards.

Moves & Threats

Tapu Bulu common fast moves are Rock Smash or Bullet Seed. Charged moves include Grass Knot or Dazzling Gleam or Solar Beam or Megahorn. Elite moves include Nature's Madness.

Tip: Watch out for Megahorn and Dazzling Gleam — these can deal heavy damage to many common counters.

Solar Beam hits extremely hard and should be dodged whenever possible.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,870-1,953 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,338-2,442 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Best Team Composition

A strong Tapu Bulu raid team should include:

  • 2–3 Fire or Poison-type attackers
  • 2 Steel or Flying-type attackers
  • 1 backup Pokémon with high DPS

Using Mega Charizard Y, Mega Venusaur, Mega Gengar, or Mega Beedrill will greatly boost your team’s overall damage output.

Weather Boost

Tapu Bulu is boosted in Sunny and Cloudy weather, making it stronger and harder to defeat.

Your counters are boosted in:

  • Sunny weather (Fire)
  • Cloudy weather (Poison)
  • Snowy weather (Ice)
  • Windy weather (Flying)

A mix of Pokémon from these categories helps balance damage and durability in longer battles.

Is Tapu Bulu Worth Raiding?

Tapu Bulu is useful for Pokédex completion and PvP formats, but it is not a top-tier Grass-type attacker for raids.

Shiny Tapu Bulu

Yes, trainers can encounter a Shiny Tapu Bulu after defeating it in a raid. The shiny version has a slightly darker color palette compared to the normal form, making it highly sought after by collectors.

Shiny rate for Legendary Raids is higher than regular wild encounters, so completing multiple raids improves your chance of obtaining a shiny Tapu Bulu.

FAQ

Can Tapu Bulu be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, Tapu Bulu's shiny version is available during select events and raid rotations.

What moves does Tapu Bulu use in raids?

Tapu Bulu can use moves like Grass Knot, Dazzling Gleam, and Solar Beam, dealing strong Grass and Fairy-type damage during raids.

Is Tapu Bulu difficult to defeat?

Tapu Bulu has strong attack and decent bulk, making it a moderately challenging raid boss. Proper counters make the battle much easier.

What makes Tapu Bulu unique?

Its Grass/Fairy typing, guardian deity status in the Alola region, and powerful terrain abilities make Tapu Bulu a memorable Legendary Pokémon in raids.
