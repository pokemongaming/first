🦈 Mega Sharpedo Common Raid Mistakes

Mega Sharpedo may look easy because of its low bulk, but many players still fail raids due to poor strategy. Most mistakes come from underestimating its damage output.

❌ 1. Underestimating Its Damage

🔍 The Mistake

Players assume: It’s fragile, so it’s easy.

⚠️ Reality

  • Mega Sharpedo deals very high DPS
  • Moves like Hydro Pump + Waterfall can:
    • One-shot glass counters
    • Quickly wipe teams

✅ Fix

  • Treat it like a glass cannon boss
  • Play aggressively but stay alert

❌ 2. Not Dodging Hydro Pump

🔍 The Mistake

Ignoring charged move animations

⚠️ Why It’s Dangerous

  • Hydro Pump is the deadliest move
  • Can instantly KO:
    • Electric types
    • Fire-type attackers

✅ Fix

  • Watch for animation
  • Swipe dodge just before impact
  • Especially important in duo raids

❌ 3. Using Wrong Counters

🔍 The Mistake

Using:

  • Fire types
  • Psychic types
  • Random high-CP Pokémon

⚠️ Problem

  • Fire gets destroyed by Water
  • Psychic/Ghost get destroyed by Dark

✅ Fix

Use proper types:

  • ⚡ Electric (best)
  • 🌿 Grass (safe)
  • 🧚 Fairy / 🥊 Fighting (backup)

❌ 4. Relying Only on Auto-Recommended Team

🔍 The Mistake

Using the game’s suggested team blindly

⚠️ Problem

  • Often prioritizes bulk over DPS
  • May include:
    • Wrong typings
    • Inefficient attackers

✅ Fix

  • Build custom raid teams
  • Focus on:
    • Type advantage
    • High DPS Pokémon

❌ 5. Not Rejoining Quickly

🔍 The Mistake

After fainting:

  • Waiting too long
  • Not re-entering battle

⚠️ Problem

  • Mega Sharpedo raids are short battles
  • Lost time = lost damage

✅ Fix

  • Tap “Rejoin Battle” immediately
  • Pre-heal teams before raid

❌ 6. Ignoring Mega Evolution Boost

🔍 The Mistake

Not using Mega Pokémon

⚠️ Why It Matters

Mega Pokémon:

  • Boost team damage
  • Increase raid efficiency

✅ Fix

  • Use Mega Electric or Grass Pokémon
  • Coordinate with teammates

❌ 7. Over-Dodging

🔍 The Mistake

Dodging every attack

⚠️ Problem

  • Reduces overall DPS
  • Slows raid completion

✅ Fix

  • Only dodge:
    • Hydro Pump
  • Tank smaller moves:
    • Bite
    • Crunch

❌ 8. Poor Team Composition

🔍 The Mistake

Mixing random types in team

⚠️ Problem

  • No synergy
  • Lower total damage

✅ Fix

  • Use:
    • Full Electric team OR
    • Full Grass team

🔍 The Mistake

Ignoring moveset at start
