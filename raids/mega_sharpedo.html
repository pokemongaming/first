Mega Sharpedo Raid Guide

Overview

Mega Sharpedo is a Water/Dark-type raid boss with extremely high attack but low defense. This makes it dangerous but easy to defeat with the right setup.

Best Counters

  • Fairy Types – Togekiss, Sylveon, Gardevoir
  • Fighting Types – Lucario, Machamp, Conkeldurr
  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Raikou

Recommended Moves

  • Togekiss: Charm + Dazzling Gleam
  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain and Fog
Best weather for you: Cloudy (boosts Fighting/Fairy).

Team Suggestions

  • Focus on Fairy attackers for safest and fastest takedown.
  • A small group can easily defeat it due to low bulk.

Rewards

Mega Energy, XL Candy, bonus items, and Sharpedo catch rewards.
