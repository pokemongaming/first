Introduction

Mesprit, the Emotional Pokémon, is one of the three Lake Guardians in Pokémon GO. As a Psychic-type Legendary Pokémon, it has access to a strong moveset that can challenge many counters.

While Mesprit’s attacks can be powerful, it is weak against Bug, Ghost, and Dark-type moves, making those the best types of Pokémon to use. With proper counters and strategy, even smaller groups can take down this Legendary raid boss effectively.

This guide is designed for both new and experienced trainers and will help you maximize your rewards while making the raid battle as smooth as possible.

Difficulty

Small groups can beat it with carefully chosen Dark- or Ghost-type Pokémon, while larger groups can focus on swarming it with high-speed attackers. Shield management is less critical than with tankier Legendaries, but dodging remains important.

Moves & Threats

Mesprit common fast moves are Confusion or Extrasensory. Charged moves include Blizzard or Swift or Future Sight.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1,669-1,747 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,086-2,184 CP
  • Boosted by: Windy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Foggy weather : boosts top counters like Gengar, Hydreigon, Darkrai. Windy weather : boosts Mesprit’s Psychic moves.

Can Mesprit Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Players can encounter a Shiny Mesprit after defeating it in a raid battle. Shiny Mesprit has a slightly different color palette compared to the normal version, making it highly sought after by collectors.

Shiny rates in Legendary Raids are higher than standard encounters, so repeated participation increases your chances of obtaining one.

Why Raid Mesprit?

Its moderate HP and quick attacks provide a different kind of challenge than tanky Legendaries. Capturing Mesprit unlocks a versatile Psychic Pokémon for PvE raids and PvP battles.

FAQ

Can Mesprit be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes. Shiny Mesprit is available during limited events and raid rotations.

What moves does Mesprit use in raids?

Mesprit can use moves like Psychic, Future Sight, and Shadow Ball, dealing massive Psychic and Ghost-type damage to opponents.

Is Mesprit difficult to defeat?

It has moderate bulk but very high speed and special attack. Proper counters and dodging techniques make battles manageable.

What makes Mesprit unique?

Mesprit represents emotion among the Lake Guardians. Its speed, special attack, and lore make it a highly sought-after Legendary Pokémon in raids.

What makes Mesprit strong?

Its high attack, strong resistances, and Meteor Mash make it a top-tier raid and PvP option.

To defeat Mesprit efficiently, bring high-level Dark- or Ghost-type Pokémon. Small teams should focus on precision and quick shields, while larger groups can rely on raw DPS.
