Nihilego Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Nihilego is a Rock / Poison-type Ultra Beast in Pokémon GO. With its unique typing, it has several vulnerabilities that make it easier to defeat with the right counters. This guide covers everything you need to know, including weaknesses, best counters, movesets, CP ranges, weather boosts, shiny availability, and raid strategy.

Type: Rock / Poison

Raid Tier: Tier 5 / Ultra Beast

Introduction

Nihilego is one of the Ultra Beasts in Pokémon GO, known for its unique combination of Rock and Poison typing. This makes it a tricky raid opponent for many trainers, especially when it uses powerful Rock or Poison moves.

Trainers can defeat Nihilego efficiently by using Pokémon that exploit its weaknesses. This guide will help you understand its movesets, optimal counters, weather boosts, CP ranges, and strategies for both solo and group raids. Whether you are a new trainer or an experienced player, this guide ensures you’re fully prepared.

Moves & Threats

Nihilego common fast moves are Pound or Poison Jab and Acid. Charged moves include Rock Slide or Power Gem or Sludge Bomb and Gunk Shot.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,167-2,256 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,709-2,821 CP
  • Boosted by: Partly Cloudy & Cloudy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Why Nihilego Is Easier Than Most Tier 5 Raids

Although Nihilego can hit hard with moves like Sludge Bomb, Thunderbolt, or Rock Slide, its Defense and HP are very low for a legendary-tier boss. This makes Nihilego a “glass cannon” raid: it deals damage, but it also goes down extremely fast when attacked with proper counters.

With top Ground-type counters, even 2–3 high-level players can defeat Nihilego comfortably.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny / Clear: Boosts Ground-type moves (BEST weather for this raid)
  • Partly Cloudy: Boosts Nihilego’s Rock-type moves
  • Cloudy: Boosts Nihilego’s Poison-type moves

In Sunny weather, Ground attackers become even more powerful and Nihilego will melt extremely fast. However, in Cloudy or Partly Cloudy weather, Nihilego becomes more dangerous and can knock out your team faster.

Battle Strategy & Fight Flow

Start the battle with your strongest Ground-type attackers and maintain constant pressure. Because Nihilego is very fragile, the key is to maximize DPS rather than focus too much on survival.

If Nihilego has Sludge Bomb or Thunderbolt, some Pokémon may faint quickly. Don’t worry — just rejoin and continue attacking. In most cases, the raid will still end very fast.

Rewards

Reasons include Pokédex entry, strong Rock / Poison attacker, shiny chance, and XL Candy farming.

How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 Trainers: Possible with top-tier Ground teams
  • 3 Trainers: Easy and safe
  • 4+ Trainers: Guaranteed win

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using Rock or Fairy Pokémon (they are resisted)
  • Ignoring Ground-type attackers
  • Over-focusing on tanky Pokémon instead of high DPS
  • Not reviving and rejoining quickly

Can Nihilego Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Trainers can encounter a Shiny Nihilego after completing the raid. Shiny Nihilego has a slightly different hue compared to the normal version, making it a rare and collectible variant.

Keep in mind that Nihilego can only be caught after the raid ends. Participating in multiple raids increases your chances of encountering the shiny version.

FAQ

Can Nihilego be shiny?

Yes, Shiny Nihilego is available during certain events.

What moves does Nihilego use in raids?

Nihilego can use moves like Power Gem, Sludge Bomb, and Meteor Beam, dealing massive Rock and Poison-type damage to opponents.

What is the best Mega Pokémon to use?

Mega Garchomp is the best choice because it boosts Ground-type damage for the entire team.

Is Nihilego hard to defeat?

No. Despite being a Tier 5 raid boss, Nihilego is one of the easiest due to its extremely low bulk.

Is Nihilego difficult to defeat?

With very high special attack and speed but low bulk, Nihilego can be challenging. Using type-advantage Pokémon ensures efficient raids.

What makes Nihilego unique?

Its Rock/Poison typing, Ultra Beast status, and incredible offensive stats make Nihilego visually striking and strategically powerful in Max Raid Battles.

Conclusion

Nihilego is a glass-cannon raid boss that can be defeated very quickly with the right Ground-type counters. If you build your team properly and take advantage of weather boosts, this raid is one of the easiest and fastest Tier 5 raids in Pokémon GO.
