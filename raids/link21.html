Mega Sharpedo Overview

Mega Sharpedo is the Mega Evolution of Sharpedo, a Water/Dark-type Pokémon known for its high attack and speed. In raids and PvP battles, it excels as a fast, hard-hitting attacker capable of exploiting vulnerabilities in Psychic- and Grass-type opponents.

With its sharp teeth and streamlined body, Mega Sharpedo gains increased attack and CP, making it a top-tier choice for Water- and Dark-type coverage. This guide covers stats, movesets, raid strategies, PvP tips, counters, and shiny hunting to maximize your gameplay.

Mega Sharpedo Evolution Guide

Mega Sharpedo evolves from Sharpedo using a Mega Stone during battles. Mega Evolution temporarily boosts its stats, granting significantly higher Attack and Defense, which makes it a top-tier choice for raids and PvP encounters. Understanding when and how to Mega Evolve is key to maximizing its battle potential.

How to Mega Evolve

  • ✔ Acquire a Sharpedo Mega Stone from special events or Mega Raids
  • ✔ Select Sharpedo in your battle party

  • ✔ Mega Evolution lasts the entire raid or until your Pokémon faints
