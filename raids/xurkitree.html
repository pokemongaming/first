Introduction

Xurkitree is a powerful Electric-type Ultra Beast with very high offensive stats in Pokémon GO. Its Electric moves can quickly threaten Water and Flying Pokémon, making careful preparation essential. Luckily, it is vulnerable to Ground-type attacks, allowing trainers to defeat it efficiently.

This guide explains Xurkitree’s weaknesses, the best counters, optimal movesets, catch CP ranges, shiny availability, and strategies for solo and group raids. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced trainer, this guide ensures you’ll maximize your success against Xurkitree.

Moves & Threats

Xurkitree common fast moves are Thunder Shock or Spark. Charged moves include Discharge or Thunder or Dazzling Gleam and Power Whip.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,155-2,249 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,695-2,812 CP
  • Boosted by: Rainy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Why Xurkitree Can Be Very Dangerous

Xurkitree has extremely high Attack and can hit unbelievably hard with moves like Thunderbolt, Discharge, or Dazzling Gleam. If your team is not prepared, your Pokémon will faint very quickly. However, its relatively low Defense means that strong Ground attackers can still defeat it quickly.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny / Clear: Boosts Ground-type moves (BEST weather)
  • Rainy: Boosts Xurkitree’s Electric-type moves

In Sunny weather, Ground-type attackers become extremely powerful and the raid becomes much easier. In Rainy weather, Xurkitree becomes significantly more dangerous and can knock out teams very quickly.

Battle Strategy & Fight Flow

Always lead with your strongest Ground-type Pokémon and maintain constant pressure. Because Xurkitree hits extremely hard, dodging charged moves like Thunderbolt can significantly improve your survival.

If your team faints, rejoin quickly and continue attacking. With proper Ground counters, the raid should not last very long.

Rewards

Reasons include Pokédex entry, strong Electric attacker, shiny chance, and XL Candy farming.

How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 3 Trainers: Possible with strong Ground teams
  • 4 Trainers: Comfortable and safe
  • 5+ Trainers: Very easy

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using Electric or Flying Pokémon (they are resisted)
  • Not using Ground-type attackers
  • Ignoring dodging when Xurkitree uses powerful charged moves
  • Using too many glass cannons without backup teams

Can Xurkitree Be Shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes! Trainers can encounter a Shiny Xurkitree after completing an Ultra Beast raid. Shiny Xurkitree has a slightly darker and more striking color scheme compared to its regular form.

Ultra Beast raids have a higher chance of yielding a shiny Pokémon than standard wild encounters. Participating in multiple raids will increase your chances of obtaining this rare variation.

FAQ

Can Xurkitree be shiny?

Yes, Shiny Xurkitree is available during certain events.

What moves does Xurkitree use in raids?

Xurkitree can use moves like Thunder Shock, Discharge, Thunderbolt, and Power Whip, dealing strong Electric and Grass-type damage.

What is the best Mega Pokémon to use?

Mega Garchomp is the best choice because it boosts Ground-type damage for the entire raid group.

Is Xurkitree harder than Nihilego?

Yes. Xurkitree hits much harder, but it is still fairly fragile when attacked by Ground-type Pokémon.

Is Xurkitree difficult to defeat?

Xurkitree has extremely high attack but relatively low defenses. This means it can deal heavy damage but can also be defeated quickly with strong Ground-type counters.

What makes Xurkitree unique?

Xurkitree is an Ultra Beast known for its unusual electric cable-like design and incredibly high special attack, making it one of the strongest Electric-type attackers.
