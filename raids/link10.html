Mega Absol Overview: Raids, PvP, and Stats

Mega Absol is the Mega Evolution of Absol, a Dark-type Pokémon in Pokémon GO. Known for its high attack and decent speed, it is a versatile choice for both raids and PvP battles. Its Mega Evolution amplifies its damage output, making it a top-tier pick against Psychic and Ghost-type opponents.

Released during special Mega Raid events, Mega Absol requires careful preparation, including optimal movesets, counters, and team coordination. Players who master its strengths can efficiently clear high-tier raids and perform well in competitive PvP leagues.

Mega Absol Raid Mechanics & Battle Tips

Mega Absol raids require strategy beyond just selecting strong counters. Understanding **raid mechanics, shield timing, weather effects, and phase management** is essential for a successful battle and faster completion.

Key Raid Mechanics

  • ✔ **Mega Evolution Boost:** Mega Absol gains increased attack while Mega-Evolved, improving DPS significantly during the raid.
  • ✔ **Charge Move Cooldown:** Mega Absol’s charged moves have predictable cooldowns; time your counters to exploit openings.
  • ✔ **Phase Damage Tracking:** Boss HP phases allow players to anticipate moves and energy build-up for better shield usage.
  • ✔ **Weather Boosts:** Cloudy weather boosts Dark-type moves, while Sunny weather improves Fighting-type counters.

Shield & Energy Management

  • ✔ **Fast Move Energy:** Use Snarl or similar fast moves to generate energy quickly for charged attacks.
  • ✔ **Shield Baiting:** Use less critical charged moves to bait opponent shields, preserving stronger moves for crucial moments.
  • ✔ **Charged Move Timing:** Delay non-critical charged moves to avoid wasting energy during short raid phases.

Phase-Specific Battle Tips

  • ✔ **Phase 1 (Full HP):** Use strongest counters and maximize DPS to reduce raid time.
  • ✔ **Phase 2 (Half HP):** Save energy for charged moves; anticipate shield usage from opponents.
  • ✔ **Phase 3 (Low HP):** Focus on finishing with high-damage charged moves; switch to tanky counters if needed.

💡 Pro Tip: Coordinating with other trainers is crucial. Synchronize charged moves to avoid wasted shields and maximize DPS. Weather-boosted moves and Mega Evolution energy management can shorten raids significantly.

Mega Absol Evolution & Mega Evolution Info

Mega Absol is the Mega Evolution of Absol. To evolve, you need **Absol Candy** and a **Mega Stone**. Mega Evolution temporarily boosts attack stats, improving DPS during raids and PvP battles.

  • ✔ **Base Form:** Absol
  • ✔ **Mega Evolution:** Mega Absol (requires Mega Stone)
  • ✔ **Mega Bonus:** +20% attack and slightly improved charged move efficiency
  • ✔ **Duration:** Mega Evolution lasts until the raid ends or Pokémon is withdrawn
