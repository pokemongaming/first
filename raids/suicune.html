Moves & Threats

Suicune common fast moves are Extrasensory or Water Gun or Ice Fang or Snarl. Charged moves include Water Pulse or Scald or Ice Beam or Hydro Pump or Bubble Beam. Elite moves include Hidden Power.

Danger: Ice Beam can be especially dangerous for many Grass counters, so be ready to dodge charged moves when possible.
