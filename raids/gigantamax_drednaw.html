Gigantamax Drednaw Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Drednaw is a Water/Rock-type raid boss with high defense and mixed attacks. Use strong Grass, Electric, or Fighting attackers to counter effectively.

Best Counters

  • Grass Types – Zarude, Venusaur, Meganium
  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Raikou, Magnezone
  • Fighting Types – Lucario, Machamp, Conkeldurr

Recommended Moves

  • Zarude: Vine Whip + Power Whip
  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Lucario: Counter + Aura Sphere

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain (Water) and Partly Cloudy (Grass)
Best to fight in: Windy or Cloudy.

Team Suggestions

  • Grass-types are highly effective if weather allows.
  • Electric brings fast damage; Fighting offers a balanced, reliable team.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged/Elite TMs, bonus items, and Drednaw catch rewards.
