Moves & Threats

Blastoise common fast moves are Water Gun or Rollout or Bite. Charged moves include Ice Beam or Hydro Pump or Flash Cannon and Skull Bash. Elite moves include Hydro Cannon.

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

Rainy weather: boosts Electric type attackers.

Battle Tips

  • Prioritize multi bar water type charge moves.
  • If weather is sunny, faint rate change - bring more water type or ground type attackers
  • Bring a squad of high water type attackers.
  • Use dodge sparingly - focus on sustained DPS and shields if in a smaller group.
