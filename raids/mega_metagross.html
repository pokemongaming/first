Mega Metagross Raid Guide

Overview

Mega Metagross is one of the strongest Steel/Psychic-type mega bosses. This raid requires high-level counters with strong Dark, Fire, and Ground moves.

Best Counters

  • Dark Types – Hydreigon, Darkrai, Yveltal
  • Ground Types – Garchomp, Excadrill, Rhyperior
  • Fire Types – Reshiram, Blaziken, Chandelure

Recommended Moves

  • Hydreigon: Bite + Brutal Swing
  • Garchomp: Mud Shot + Earth Power
  • Reshiram: Fire Fang + Fusion Flare

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Snow, Windy
Best weather to battle: Sunny (boosts your Ground attacks).

Team Suggestions

  • Ground-type teams provide the most stable performance.
  • Attack with Dark types to avoid Psychic damage.

Rewards

Mega Energy, XL Candy, and a chance to catch Metagross.
