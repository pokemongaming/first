Gigantamax Butterfree Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Butterfree is a Bug/Flying-type raid boss. While it doesn’t have very high bulk, its moves can be tricky, and it often uses status-type or control attacks.

Best Counters

  • Rock Types – Tyranitar, Terrakion, Rhyperior
  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Magnezone
  • Ice Types – Glaceon, Weavile

Recommended Moves

  • Tyranitar: Smack Down + Stone Edge
  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Glaceon: Frost Breath + Avalanche

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Windy (Flying)
Best for you: Snow (boosts Ice) or Partly Cloudy (boosts Rock).

Team Suggestions

  • Rock attackers give the safest strategy to counter Flying and Bug moves.
  • A balanced team of Rock and Electric works well.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, bonus raid items, and a chance to catch Butterfree.
