Gigantamax Garbodor Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Garbodor is a Poison-type raid boss known for strong poison attacks and high durability. This guide helps you prepare the best counters, moves, and strategies for defeating it efficiently.

Best Counters

  • Ground Types – Excadrill, Garchomp, Rhyperior
  • Psychic Types – Mewtwo, Espeon, Alakazam
  • Steel Types – Metagross, Dialga

Recommended Moves

  • Mewtwo: Confusion + Psystrike
  • Excadrill: Mud-Slap + Drill Run
  • Metagross: Zen Headbutt + Psychic

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Cloudy weather (Poison)
Best to fight in: Clear or Windy weather.

Team Suggestions

  • Use Ground/Psychic attackers for fastest clear time.
  • Bring 5–6 strong level 35+ Pokémon.

Rewards

Chance to catch Gigantamax Garbodor, XL Candy, bonus items, and TMs.
