Heatran Raid Guide – Best Counters, Weakness, CP & Strategy

Heatran is a Fire and Steel-type Legendary Raid Boss in Pokémon GO. With its unique typing, Heatran is resistant to many types but has weaknesses to Fighting, Ground, and Water moves. This guide provides the best counters, weaknesses, movesets, CP ranges, weather boosts, shiny availability, and raid strategies.

Type: Fire / Steel

Raid Tier: Legendary Raid

Major Weakness: Fighting, Ground, Water

Introduction

Heatran is a Legendary Pokémon with Fire and Steel typing, making it a challenging opponent in raids. Its Steel typing gives it many resistances, but trainers can exploit its vulnerabilities to Fighting, Ground, and Water-type attacks.

Heatran’s strong Fire-type charged moves can quickly defeat unprepared counters, so understanding its moveset, weaknesses, and recommended raid team is crucial. This guide will help both beginners and expert trainers prepare for Heatran raids efficiently.

🧬 About Heatran in Pokémon GO

Heatran is a Fire/Steel-type Legendary Pokémon from the Sinnoh region. It is well known for its massive number of resistances and its strong performance in both raids and PvP.

Heatran is useful for:

  • Raids (as a Fire-type attacker)
  • Master League and Ultra League PvP
  • Legendary collection

Weaknesses & resistances

CategoryDetails
❌ Weak To: Water • Fighting • Ground
✅ Resistant / Immune To: Normal • Flying • Poison • Bug • Steel • Psychic • Ice • Dragon • Fairy • Grass
Notes Grass and Bug attacks are partially effective, but Water-types like Kyogre or Swampert and Ground-types like Rhyperior will deal the most consistent damage. Avoid using Fire, Rock, or Steel Pokémon—they barely scratch Heatran’s tough armor.

🗡️ Best Counters for Heatran

The absolute best way to defeat Heatran is to use strong Ground-type Pokémon because of its double weakness to Ground moves.

🌍 Best Ground-type Counters

  • Mamoswine: (Mud Slap / High Horsepower)
  • Golurk: (Mud Slap / Earth Power)
  • Krookodile: (Mud Slap / Earthquake)

🥊 Fighting-type Counters

  • Heracross: (Counter)
  • Lucario: (Force Palm / Aura Sphere)
  • Blaziken: (Counter / Aura Sphere)

Best Mega Evolutions

Recommended Megas to use against Heatran:

  • Blaziken, Lucario, Heracross – Fighting boost
  • Garchomp, Swampert, Heracross – Ground boost

Difficulty

Small raid groups must rely on max-level Water or Ground Pokémon, while larger teams can afford some flexibility. Timing charged moves and shields is essential to keep DPS Pokémon alive.

🌀 Heatran Moveset

Heatran common fast moves are Bug Bite or Fire Spin. Charged moves include Earth Power or Stone Edge or Iron Head or Flamethrower and Fire Blast. Elite moves include Magma Storm.

Most dangerous moveset: Fire Spin + Fire Blast or Iron Head

🎯 Catch CP (100% IV)

  • Perfect (L20): 2,058-2,145 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2,573-2,681 CP
  • Boosted by: Sunny & Snowy

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

🌦️ Weather Boost

Heatran gets a weather boost in:

  • Sunny weather (boosts Fire)
  • Snowy weather (boosts Steel)

Your counters get boosted in:

  • Sunny → Ground-types
  • Rainy → Water-types
  • Cloudy → Fighting-types

Recommended Teams

🌍 Best Ground-type Counters

  • Primal Groudon
  • Mega Garchomp
  • Landorus (Therian)
  • Garchomp

💡 Best Strategy to Defeat Heatran

  1. Use only Ground-type attackers for fastest win
  2. Dodge Fire Blast and Stone Edge if possible
  3. Mega or Primal Groudon makes this raid extremely easy
  4. Raid with friends to save revives and time

When to avoid

Avoid using fragile Fire or Grass attackers that will faint quickly to Heatran’s strong Fire- and Ground-type moves. Also be cautious with Ice attackers if Heatran carries Rock coverage.

👥 How Many Trainers Are Needed?

  • 2 strong trainers → Very easy with Ground teams
  • 3–4 trainers → Comfortable clear
  • Low-level players → Bring at least 5–6 trainers

Shiny Heatran

Yes, Heatran can appear as a shiny Pokémon in raids. Shiny Heatran has a darker, slightly redder appearance compared to the regular version, making it a highly sought-after addition to any collection.

The chance to encounter a shiny Heatran is limited to raid encounters, so participating in multiple raids increases your odds of obtaining this shiny form.

📌 Is Heatran Worth Raiding?

Yes. Heatran is a very good Fire-type attacker and also very strong in PvP. It is definitely worth raiding, especially if you want a good IV one or its shiny form.

PvP Relevance

While Heatran isn’t top tier in PvP, it’s a fun spice pick. Heatran shines more in raids and PvE content.

FAQ

Can Heatran be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Heatran has been released in raids or events.

What is Heatran’s exclusive move?

Heatran can use powerful attacks such as Magma Storm and Flash Cannon. These moves deal massive damage and can leave opponents burned or weakened.

What makes Heatran unique?

Its combination of Fire and Steel typing, molten metallic body, and high attack stats make Heatran both visually impressive and strategically challenging in Max Raid Battles.

Is Heatran difficult to defeat?

It has high special attack and solid defenses. Using Water, Ground, or Fighting-type counters ensures efficient battles.

🏁 Conclusion

Heatran is one of the easiest Legendary raids if you exploit its double weakness to Ground-type Pokémon.

Summary

Even small groups can succeed with high-level Water or Ground Pokémon and careful shield management. Heatran tests strategic planning more than raw power, making it a satisfying raid for experienced trainers.
