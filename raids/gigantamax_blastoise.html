Gigantamax Blastoise Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Blastoise is a Water-type raid boss with strong defensive stats and powerful water moves. Use high-DPS Electric or Grass attackers to maximize your damage.

Best Counters

  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Raikou, Electivire
  • Grass Types – Zarude, Roserade, Exeggutor
  • Dragon Types – Palkia, Rayquaza

Recommended Moves

  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Zarude: Vine Whip + Power Whip
  • Palkia: Dragon Tail + Hydro Pump

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain (Water), Cloudy (Grass)
Best for you: Windy (boosts Dragon).

Team Suggestions

  • Electric attackers provide the fastest clear time.
  • A mixed team of Electric and Grass is ideal for safety.

Rewards

Mega Energy, XL Candy, Charged TMs, and Blastoise catch chance.
