Mega Aerodactyl Overview

Mega Aerodactyl is a Rock/Flying-type Pokémon known for its incredible speed and strong attack power. As the Mega Evolution of Aerodactyl, it excels in raids, PvP battles, and gym attacks due to its high offensive stats.

Mega Aerodactyl’s unique typing gives it resistance to Normal and Flying moves while making it vulnerable to Rock, Steel, and Electric moves. It is best used as a fast striker in both PvE and PvP.
