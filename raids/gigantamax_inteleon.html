Gigantamax Inteleon Raid Guide

Overview

Gigantamax Inteleon is a pure Water-type raid boss known for its powerful ranged attacks. Use strong Electric and Grass attackers.

Best Counters

  • Electric Types – Zekrom, Magnezone, Raikou
  • Grass Types – Zarude, Venusaur, Roserade
  • Dragon Types – Rayquaza, Zekrom

Recommended Moves

  • Zekrom: Charge Beam + Wild Charge
  • Zarude: Vine Whip + Power Whip
  • Magnezone: Spark + Wild Charge

Weather Effects

Boosted in: Rain
Best for you: Partly Cloudy (Grass) or Windy (Dragon).

Team Suggestions

  • Electric attackers are the fastest option.
  • Grass attackers offer safe bulk and steady DPS.

Rewards

XL Candy, Charged TMs, and Inteleon capture rewards.
