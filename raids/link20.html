Mega Alakazam Overview

Mega Alakazam is the Mega Evolution of Alakazam, a Psychic-type Pokémon renowned for its incredible Speed and Special Attack stats. Its psychic powers are amplified after Mega Evolution, making it one of the strongest glass-cannon attackers in both raids and PvP battles.

Key Highlights

  • ✔ Type: Psychic
  • ✔ Mega Evolution significantly boosts Special Attack and Speed
  • ✔ Excels in raids, PvP battles, and gym offense
  • ✔ Extremely fragile – careful placement is required in battle
  • ✔ High DPS for Psychic-type moves

Mega Alakazam Evolution & Mega Evolution

Alakazam evolves from Kadabra, which evolves from Abra. Mega Evolution significantly enhances Alakazam’s Special Attack and Speed, making it one of the fastest and most powerful glass-cannon attackers in Pokémon GO.

Evolution Line

  • ✔ Abra → Kadabra → Alakazam → Mega Alakazam
  • ✔ Abra evolves into Kadabra at level 16 or via candies
  • ✔ Kadabra evolves into Alakazam using 100 candies or trading
  • ✔ Mega Evolution requires Mega Energy specific to Alakazam

Mega Evolution Details

  • ✔ Mega Alakazam gains a massive boost to Special Attack and Speed
  • ✔ Remains extremely fragile – low HP and Defense remain a weakness
  • ✔ Best used strategically in PvE raids or PvP lead roles
  • ✔ Mega Energy can be farmed through Mega Raids or special events
  • ✔ Mega Evolution lasts for the duration of the battle or until Pokémon faints

Mega Alakazam Fun Facts & Trivia

Mega Alakazam is one of the most iconic Psychic-type Pokémon. Beyond its impressive stats and competitive usage, it has interesting lore, gameplay quirks, and trivia that make it a fan favorite.

Fun Facts

  • ✔ Mega Alakazam’s intelligence is said to be over 5,000 IQ in Pokémon lore, making it one of the smartest Pokémon.
  • ✔ It carries spoons that amplify its psychic powers – losing them weakens its abilities in battles.
  • ✔ Mega Alakazam was first introduced in Generation VI as a Mega Evolution of Alakazam.
  • ✔ Despite being fragile in battles, its speed allows it to strike first, often taking down opponents before they react.
  • ✔ In the anime, Mega Alakazam has been featured as a trusted Pokémon of elite trainers and gym leaders.
  • ✔ Mega Evolution does not change its type, remaining Psychic, but significantly boosts its stats.
  • ✔ Its shiny form has a distinct purple hue, making it highly sought after during special events.

Trivia

  • ✔ Mega Alakazam is the only Mega Evolution of a non-legendary Pokémon with a high-speed, glass-cannon role in both PvE and PvP.
  • ✔ Its design is inspired by the concept of a psychic spoon-bending prodigy.
  • ✔ In competitive play, Mega Alakazam is often banned in certain leagues due to its overwhelming speed and damage.
  • ✔ Trainers often nickname it based on its intelligence, like “Professor” or “Spoon Master.”
  • ✔ Mega Evolution temporarily boosts its stats without permanent change, allowing it to revert to Alakazam after battle.

Mega Alakazam FAQs & Common Player Questions

Trainers often have recurring questions about Mega Alakazam’s gameplay, evolution, and strategic use. This FAQ section answers the most common queries to help players make informed decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q1: How do I Mega Evolve Alakazam?
    A: Collect 200 Mega Energy for Alakazam and use it during a Mega Raid or in battle to temporarily evolve it into Mega Alakazam.
  • Q2: Is Mega Alakazam good for PvP?
    A: Yes, its extremely high speed and damage output make it a strong glass-cannon attacker. Pair it with resistant teammates to cover its weaknesses.
  • Q3: What Pokémon counters Mega Alakazam?
    A: Dark-, Ghost-, and Bug-type Pokémon such as Umbreon, Tyranitar, and Honchkrow are effective counters in PvP and raids.
  • Q4: Can I use Mega Alakazam in raids effectively?
    A: Absolutely. Lead with Mega Alakazam to break shields and deal massive damage, then rotate out if facing super-effective moves.
  • Q5: Where can I get Mega Energy for Alakazam?
    A: Mega Energy is obtained from Mega Raids, special events, and occasionally through timed research tasks.
  • Q6: Should I power up Mega Alakazam fully?
    A: Power it up based on your gameplay goals. Maxing out IVs is recommended for PvP, while event-focused players may invest selectively for raids.
  • Q7: Is Mega Alakazam shiny available?
    A: Yes, Mega Alakazam has a shiny form with a distinctive purple hue, obtainable during special events.
  • Q8: How long does Mega Alakazam stay in Mega Evolution?
    A: Mega Evolution lasts for the duration of the battle or raid, after which it reverts back to Alakazam.
