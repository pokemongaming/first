What is Lucario?

Lucario is a Fighting/Steel type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Lucario, its competitive strengths, top counters.

Not currently. Shiny Lucario has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Lucario?

Best moveset of Lucario are Counter which does damage of 13 under Fast Attack and Close Combat of 105 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.
