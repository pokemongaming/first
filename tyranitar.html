What is Tyranitar?

Tyranitar is a Rock/Dark type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Tyranitar, its competitive strengths, top counters.

🎯 Type Effectiveness Chart

Super Effective Against

Fighting, Ground, Bug, Steel, Water, Grass, Fairy

Resistant To

Normal, Flying, Poison, Ghost, Fire, Psychic, Dark

    Use Cloudy weather to boosts Fighting and Fairy type counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Tyranitar be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Tyranitar?

Best moveset of Tyranitar are Iron Tail which does damage of 14 under Fast Attack and Fire Blast of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Tyranitar?

Rainy weather to boosts Water type counters, Cloudy weather to boosts Fighting and Fairy type counters
