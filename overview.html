Mega Abomasnow

Mega Abomasnow is a Grass & Ice-type Pokémon in Pokémon GO. Maximum CP: 3850. It is weak to Fire, Bug, Fighting, Flying, Poison, Rock, and Steel-type moves. Resistant to Electric, Grass, Ground, and Water.
