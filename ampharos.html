❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Ampharos be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, shiny Mega Ampharos is available.

What are best moveset of Ampharos?

Best movset of Ampharos are Volt Switch which does damage of 13 under Fast Attack and Zap Cannon or Focus Blast of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Ampharos?

Cloudy weather to boost Fairy types counters, Sunny weather to boost Ground types counters, Snow weather to boost Ice type counters, Windy weather to boost Dragon type counters.

How do I get Mega Energy for Ampharos?

You can earn Mega Energy by defeating Mega Ampharos in raids or walking with Ampharos if already Mega Evolved once.
