What is Blaziken?

Blaziken is a Fire/Fighting type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Blaziken, its competitive strengths, top counters.

🎯 Type Effectiveness Chart

Super Effective Against

Flying, Ground, Psychic, Water

Resistant To

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Blaziken be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Blaziken?

Best movset of Blaziken are Counter or Fire Spin which does damage of 13 under Fast Attack and Overheat of 160 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Blaziken?

Partly Cloudy weather to boosts Flying type counters, Rainy weather to boosts Water type counters, Windy weather to boosts Psychic type counters.
