Introduction

Mega Metagross is one of the most intimidating Mega Pokémon in Pokémon GO, featuring a sleek design and fierce Steel / Psychic typing. Known as the “Iron Leg Pokémon,” Metagross already has a reputation as a fast but fragile attacker. With Mega Evolution, its power skyrockets, making it a glass cannon that can shred opponents quickly but also falls just as fast.

This guide will cover everything you need to know about defeating Mega Metagross in raids: weaknesses, best counters, ideal team setups, weather boosts, duo/trio possibilities, and even tips for maximizing rewards.

Moves & Threats

Metagross common fast moves are Fury Cutter or Bullet Punch or Zen Headbutt. Charged moves include Earthquake or Flash Cannon and Psychic. Elite moves include Meteor Mash.

Weather Effects

Sunny weather : boosts Fire type attackers. Windy weather : boosts Ghost and Dark attackers.

Mega Metagross’s Meteor Mash hits very hard. Prioritize bulky Fire- or Ground-types to absorb damage while dishing out super-effective hits.

When to avoid

Avoid using Fairy or Psychic types, as they take heavy damage from Metagross’s Steel and Psychic moves. Fragile Bug or Ice types can also faint too quickly.

Duo & Trio Possibility

A duo is possible with maxed-out counters and coordination. A trio is comfortable for mid-level players. Casual groups should bring 4–5 trainers.

Rewards

  • Mega Energy for Metagross
  • Chance to catch Metagross (with boosted IVs)
  • Rare Candy & XL Candy

Why Raid Mega Metagross?

Reasons include Pokédex entry, strong Steel / Psychic attacker, shiny chance, and XL Candy farming.

PvP Relevance

While Metagross isn’t top tier in PvP, it’s a fun spice pick. Mega Metagross shines more in raids and PvE content.

FAQ

Can Mega Metagross be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes. Shiny Metagross has been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Metagross?

Best moveset of Metagross are Zen Headbutt which does damage of 11 under Fast Attack and Earthquake of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Metagross?

Sunny weather to boosts Fire type counters. Windy weather to boosts Ghost and Dark type counters.

What makes Mega Metagross strong?

Its high attack, strong resistances, and Meteor Mash make it a top-tier raid and PvP option.

Conclusion

Mega Metagross is not the strongest Mega, but it’s easy to raid thanks to multiple weaknesses. It’s worth raiding for its shiny form, Mega Energy, and for filling your Mega Pokédex. With Electric, Fighting, and Grass counters, you’ll defeat it with ease.
