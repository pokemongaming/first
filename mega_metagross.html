About Mega Metagross

Mega Metagross is a Steel/Psychic-type powerhouse with massive attack and defense stats. It resists many common types, making it a tough raid boss. Understanding its weaknesses and countering effectively is key to victory.

Moves & Threats

Metagross common fast moves are Fury Cutter or Bullet Punch or Zen Headbutt. Charged moves include Earthquake or Flash Cannon and Psychic. Elite moves include Meteor Mash.

Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Boosts Fire type attackers.
  • Windy: Boosts Ghost and Dark attackers.

    • Mega Metagross’s Meteor Mash hits very hard. Prioritize bulky Fire- or Ground-types to absorb damage while dishing out super-effective hits.

    Team size & strategy

    2-3 high level trainers with top counters.4-5 casual trainers are required. Fire-types perform best under sunny weather, while Ghost/Dark excel in fog. Always save shields for high-damage charge moves like Meteor Mash or Psychic.

    When to avoid

    Avoid using Fairy or Psychic types, as they take heavy damage from Metagross’s Steel and Psychic moves. Fragile Bug or Ice types can also faint too quickly.

    FAQ

    Can Mega Metagross be shiny in Pokémon GO?

    Yes. Shiny Metagross has been released in raids or events.

    What are best moveset of Metagross?

    Best moveset of Metagross are Zen Headbutt which does damage of 11 under Fast Attack and Earthquake of 140 under Charged Attack.

    Are Shadow Pokémon better?

    Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

    What is the best weather to raid Mega Metagross?

    Sunny weather to boosts Fire type counters. Windy weather to boosts Ghost and Dark type counters.

    What makes Mega Metagross strong?

    Its high attack, strong resistances, and Meteor Mash make it a top-tier raid and PvP option.
