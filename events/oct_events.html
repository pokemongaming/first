Pokémon GO — October 2025 Events

Compact calendar & short summaries of each confirmed event for October 2025.

XP Celebration

Worldwide Community Day
Aug 25, 2025 - Oct 14, 2025 • 1:00–11:59 PM local time

Level cap being increased to Level 80. Trainers will connect massive amounts of XP before the journey beyond level 50.

  • 2x Catch XP from Nice, Great, Excellent Throws
  • +3,000 XP from winning Raids
  • +3,000 XP from winning Max Battles

Steel Skyline

Worldwide Community Day

Lists all the bonuses, research and collection challenges which we can encounter during the event.

  • Global Challenges for XP

  • More Steel type pokemon available

Mega Metagross Raid Day

Special Raid Day
Oct 4, 2025

Mega Metagross debuts in Raid Day rotations on Oct 4 — a focused raid day where players can battle Mega Metagross in large numbers. Expect boosted Mega Energy drops and a concentrated window for Mega raids.

  • Mega raids
  • Higher Mega Energy gains

Harvest Festival

Seasonal / themed event
Oct 10 – Oct 16, 2025 • 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM local time

Harvest Festival runs mid-October and brings themed spawns, research, and likely new or regional Pokémon appearances. Community reporting highlights the debut/appearance of form-variants such as Dipplin and Hydrapple during this window. Expect event Field Research tasks, special raids, and themed bonuses.

  • 2x Candy for catching pokemon using Pinab and Silver Pinab
  • More apples to appear at Mossy Lures
  • Shiny odds for Pumbkaboo and Smoliv
  • Dipplin and Hydrapple debut
