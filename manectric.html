What is Manectric?

Manectric is a Electric type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Manectric, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Manectric be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, shiny Mega Manectric is available.

What are best moveset of Manectric?

Best movset of Manectric are Snarl which does damage of 11 under Fast Attack and Overheat of 160 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

How do I get Mega Energy for Manectric?

You can earn Mega Energy by defeating Mega Manectric in raids or walking with Manectric if already Mega Evolved once

What is the best weather to raid Mega Manectric?

Sunny weather boosts Ground type attacks.
