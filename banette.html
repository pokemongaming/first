What is Banette?

Banette is a Ghost type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Banette, its competitive strengths, top counters.

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.
