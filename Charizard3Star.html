Can You Solo 3-Star Charizard?

Yes. Charizard is one of the easier 3-star raids due to its double weakness to Rock. Trainers Level 30+ with strong Rock attackers can complete this raid solo. Lower-level trainers may need one additional teammate for safety.

Battle Tips

Avoid using Grass or Bug types, as they are heavily resisted. Focus on pure Rock-type damage output for the fastest clear time. If Charizard has Blast Burn, dodging charged attacks can help preserve your top attackers.

If available, Mega Aerodactyl can boost Rock-type damage for the entire team, making the raid even easier.
