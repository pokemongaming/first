Best Raid Attackers in Pokémon GO (2026 Complete Guide)

Raid battles in Pokémon GO require strong attackers to defeat bosses quickly and efficiently. Choosing the right Pokémon can make the difference between victory and failure, especially in high-tier raids.

This complete guide covers the best raid attackers, including legendary Pokémon, Mega evolutions, and budget-friendly options. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced trainer, this guide will help you build the strongest raid team possible.

🔥 Top Raid Attackers (Highest DPS)

The following Pokémon are considered top-tier attackers due to their extremely high damage per second (DPS), strong typing, and effectiveness across multiple raid scenarios.

Pokémon Type Strength
Mewtwo Psychic Best general attacker
Rayquaza Dragon/Flying Top dragon damage
Rampardos Rock Highest raw DPS
Lucario Fighting/Steel Versatile and powerful
Garchomp Dragon/Ground Strong dual-type attacker

These Pokémon perform exceptionally well in most raid battles. Mewtwo stands out as the most versatile attacker, while Rampardos delivers unmatched raw damage at the cost of lower survivability.

⚡ Best Mega Pokémon for Raids

Mega Evolutions play a critical role in raids by boosting the damage output of all players in the battle. Choosing the correct Mega Pokémon can significantly reduce the time needed to defeat a boss.

  • Mega Rayquaza – Best overall Mega attacker with incredible damage boost
  • Mega Blaziken – Strong Fire and Fighting damage
  • Mega Gengar – Top Ghost-type attacker
  • Mega Swampert – Powerful Water and Ground moves

Using a Mega Pokémon that matches your team’s typing provides additional bonuses, including increased damage and extra candy rewards after the raid.

💰 Best Budget Raid Attackers

Not every player has access to legendary Pokémon. Fortunately, there are several budget-friendly attackers that can still perform extremely well in raids.

  • Machamp – Reliable Fighting-type attacker
  • Gyarados – Strong Water-type with wide availability
  • Excadrill – Excellent Ground-type damage
  • Weavile – Fast Ice/Dark attacker

These Pokémon are easier to obtain and power up, making them ideal for beginners and casual players.

📊 How to Choose the Best Raid Attacker

Selecting the best attacker depends on the raid boss you are facing. Always prioritize Pokémon with type advantages, as they deal significantly more damage.

You should also consider:

  • ✔ Weather boosts that enhance certain types
  • ✔ Mega Evolution bonuses
  • ✔ Move effectiveness
  • ✔ Team composition

Combining these factors ensures maximum efficiency in raids.

⚔️ Strategy Tips for Raid Battles

Winning raids is not just about having strong Pokémon. Strategy plays a major role in success.

  • ✔ Join raids with multiple trainers for higher-tier bosses
  • ✔ Use dodging to increase survivability
  • ✔ Coordinate Mega Evolutions with teammates
  • ✔ Revive and rejoin quickly if your team faints

Proper coordination and preparation can make even difficult raids manageable.
