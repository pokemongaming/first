XP Celebration 2025

Aug 25, 2025, 1:00 PM local → Oct 14, 2025, 11:59 PM local

The **XP Celebration** event is designed to help Trainers prepare for Pokémon GO’s upcoming level cap raise (from level 50 to level 80). During this period, players can benefit from boosted XP bonuses, a special Timed Research with generous XP rewards (~7 million total), and an adjustment to a Level-Up Research task.

Event Bonuses (stacking)
  • 2× Catch XP from Nice / Great / Excellent throws
  • +3,000 XP for each Raid win
  • +3,000 XP for each Max Battle win
Level-Up Research Adjustment
  • The Level-Up Research task for level 47 (which normally required exploring 25 km in a week eight times) is temporarily eased: now only two instances of the 25 km exploration requirement will be necessary.
