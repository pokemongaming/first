Weather Boost Guide

Overview

In Pokémon GO, different weather conditions increase the power of certain types of Pokémon. Weather boost helps you catch stronger Pokémon, deal more raid damage, and encounter high-level wild Pokémon.

Weather Types & Their Boosted Pokémon

  • Sunny/Clear: Fire, Grass, Ground
  • Rain: Water, Electric, Bug
  • Partly Cloudy: Normal, Rock
  • Cloudy: Fairy, Fighting, Poison
  • Windy: Dragon, Flying, Psychic
  • Snow: Ice, Steel
  • Fog: Ghost, Dark

Benefits of Weather Boost

  • Higher CP Pokémon in the wild.
  • Better IV chances (up to level 35 wild Pokémon).
  • Raid bosses hit harder but also take extra damage.
  • Moves are boosted for types matching the weather.

Why Weather Boost Matters

Weather boost helps trainers build stronger teams faster. It also makes raids easier when your attackers get boosted damage from weather.

Examples

  • Rain boosts Electric, making Zekrom and Raikou stronger.
  • Windy boosts Psychic, helping Mewtwo and Espeon in raids.
  • Sunny boosts Grass and Fire, making Venusaur and Charizard stronger.

Tips for Trainers

  • Use weather-boosted attackers in raids for higher DPS.
  • Hunt wild Pokémon during their boosted weather for high IV encounters.
  • Plan raid counters based on the current forecast.
