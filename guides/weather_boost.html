Pokémon GO Weather Boost Guide (2026 Complete Guide)

Weather in Pokémon GO plays a major role in raid battles by boosting certain Pokémon types and moves. Understanding how weather works can significantly improve your chances of winning raids and catching stronger Pokémon.

In this guide, we explain all weather types, their effects, and how to use them strategically.

🌦️ What is Weather Boost?

Weather boost increases the power of specific Pokémon types based on in-game weather conditions. It also increases the level of Pokémon encountered in raids, making them stronger but harder to defeat.

For example, during Sunny weather, Fire-type and Grass-type moves deal more damage.

📊 Weather Boost Chart

Weather Boosted Types
Sunny Fire, Grass, Ground
Rainy Water, Electric, Bug
Partly Cloudy Normal, Rock
Cloudy Fairy, Fighting, Poison
Windy Dragon, Flying, Psychic
Snowy Ice, Steel
Foggy Ghost, Dark

Knowing this chart allows you to choose the best counters based on current weather conditions.

⚔️ How Weather Affects Raids

Weather boosts not only increase your Pokémon’s damage but can also strengthen the raid boss. This means battles can become easier or harder depending on the situation.

For example, a Dragon-type raid boss becomes significantly stronger during Windy weather.

💡 Best Strategies Using Weather

  • ✔ Use boosted types for higher damage output
  • ✔ Avoid raids where the boss is weather boosted
  • ✔ Combine weather boost with Mega Pokémon
  • ✔ Coordinate with teammates for maximum effect

Using weather strategically can reduce raid difficulty and increase rewards.

🎯 Weather Boost and Catch CP

Weather boost increases the level of Pokémon caught after raids, resulting in higher CP values.

This means you have a better chance of catching stronger Pokémon during boosted conditions.
