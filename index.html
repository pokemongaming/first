8. TYPES & COUNTERS OVERVIEW

Understanding Pokémon types is essential for winning raids. Each type has strengths and weaknesses that directly affect battle performance. For example, Water-types are weak against Electric and Grass moves, while Dragon-types are vulnerable to Ice and Fairy attacks. By selecting counters with the right type advantage, you can deal more damage and finish raids faster.

9. TIPS & STRATEGY SECTION

Winning raids consistently requires smart gameplay, not just strong Pokémon. One key strategy is to build a balanced team with high DPS attackers rather than relying on a single powerful Pokémon. Dodging charged moves at the right time can help your team survive longer, especially in solo or duo raids. Weather conditions also play an important role, as they can boost both your attacks and the raid boss’s moves. Planning ahead and understanding the boss mechanics can make even difficult raids manageable.
