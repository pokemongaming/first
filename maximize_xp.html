XP Celebration Guide

Overview

XP events in Pokémon GO give players opportunities to level up faster. During XP celebrations, catching, evolving, raiding, or battling can provide 2×, 3×, or even 4× XP rewards.

Common XP Bonuses

  • 2× Catch XP
  • 2× Evolution XP
  • 2× Raid XP
  • 2× Excellent Throw XP
  • Lucky Egg stacking bonuses for even more XP

How to Maximize XP Gains

1. Use Lucky Eggs

Double your XP gains for 30 minutes. During 3× XP events, Lucky Eggs make it effectively 6× XP.

2. Aim for Excellent Throws

Excellent throws during boosted events give huge XP. Practice throw timing for consistent gains.

3. Focus on Fast Evolutions

Save easy-to-evolve Pokémon like Pidgey, Weedle, and Caterpie for evolution sprees during XP events.

4. Raid Frequently

Raids during XP celebrations offer significantly higher XP, especially during special events or weekends.

Recommended XP Strategy

  • Use a Lucky Egg before raids or mass evolutions.
  • Play during weather that boosts spawns for more catches.
  • Prioritize Excellent throws for huge XP gains.

Why XP Events Matter

Higher trainer levels unlock better Pokémon power-ups, improved max CP, and additional gameplay features. XP events are the fastest path to reaching Level 50.
