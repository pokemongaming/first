What is Cresselia?

Cresselia is a Psychic type in 5 Star Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Cresselia, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Cresselia be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, shiny Cresselia is available.

What are best moveset of Cresselia?

Best movset of Cresselia are Confusion which does damage of 19 under Fast Attack and Moonblast of 130 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Cresselia?

Foggy weather to boosts Dark and Ghost type counters, Cloudy weather to boosts Bug types counters.
