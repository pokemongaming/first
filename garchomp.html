What is Garchomp?

Garchomp is a Dragon/Ground type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Garchomp, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Garchomp be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Garchomp has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Garchomp?

Best moveset of Garchomp are Dragon Tail which does damage of 14 under Fast Attack and Earthquake, Fire Blast of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Garchomp?

Snow weather boosts Ice type counters, Cloudy weather boosts Fairy type counters.
