What is Swampert?

Swampert is a Water/Ground type in 5 Star Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Swampert, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Swampert be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Shiny Swampert has been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Swampert?

Best moveset of Swampert are Water Gun which does damage of 5 under Fast Attack and Earthquake of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Swampert?

Partly Cloudy weather to boosts Rock type counters, Cloudy weather to boosts Fairy type counters, Snow weather to boosts Ice types counters.
