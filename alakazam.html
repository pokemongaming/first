What is Alakazam?

Alakazam is a Psychic type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Alakazam, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Alakazam be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Alakazam has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Alakazam?

Best moveset of Alakazam are Confusion which does damage of 19 under Fast Attack and Focus Blast of 140 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Alakazam?

Fog weather to boost Ghost and Dark type counters, Cloudy weather to boost Bug type counters.
