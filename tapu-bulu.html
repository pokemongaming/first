What is Tapu Bulu?

Tapu Bulu is a Grass/Fairy type in 5‑star raids known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Tapu Bulu, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Tapu Bulu be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Yes, Tapu Bulu's shiny version is available during select events and raid rotations.

What are best moveset of Tapu Bulu?

Best movset of Tapu Bulu are Rock Smash which does damage of 17 under Fast Attack and Solar Beam of 180 under Charged Attack.

Is Tapu Bulu useful in PvP?

Tapu Bulu can perform well in Master League and certain limited PvP metas, especially with its Grass/Fairy typing to handle Dragons and Fighters.

What is the best weather to raid Tapu Bulu?

Sunny weather to boosts Fire counters, Cloudy weather to boosts Poison-type counters, Windy weather to boosts Flying-type counters, Snow weather to boosts Ice and Steel counters

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.
