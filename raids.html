Pokémon GO Raid Guides — Legendary, Mega & Gigantamax

Raids are one of the most exciting and rewarding features in Pokémon GO. They allow trainers to battle powerful Pokémon, including Legendary, Mega, and special forms like Gigantamax. However, raids can also be difficult if you don’t know the boss’s weaknesses or which Pokémon to use. This section of PokemonGaming.in is dedicated to helping you win more raids with clear, easy-to-follow raid guides.

Each raid guide on this website is written to give you all the important information in one place, including the boss’s type, weaknesses, best counters, recommended teams, and simple battle strategies. Whether you are a casual player or a hardcore raider, these guides will help you prepare better and use your raid passes wisely.

Types of Raids in Pokémon GO

Legendary Raids

Legendary raids feature some of the strongest Pokémon in the game, such as Dialga, Giratina, Reshiram, and many others. These raids usually require multiple trainers working together. Our Legendary raid guides explain how many players you need, which Pokémon perform best, and which moves you should watch out for.

Mega Raids

Mega raids allow you to battle Mega-Evolved Pokémon like Mega Gengar, Mega Blaziken, Mega Metagross, and more. These raids are important because they reward Mega Energy, which you can use to Mega Evolve your own Pokémon. Our Mega raid guides show you the best counters and team setups to defeat them quickly and efficiently.

Gigantamax & Special Raids

Some special raids, such as Gigantamax or other unique forms, have special rules and mechanics. These battles often require specific strategies and team choices. Our special raid guides focus on explaining these mechanics in simple terms and helping you choose the right Pokémon for the job.

What You’ll Find in Each Raid Guide

  • Boss type, weaknesses, and resistances
  • Best Pokémon counters with recommended moves
  • Suggested team compositions for different group sizes
  • Moves you should dodge or be careful about
  • Catch CP ranges and shiny availability (when applicable)
  • Easy-to-understand battle tips and strategies
