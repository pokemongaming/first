Common moveset & G-Max notes

Community sim and database results list top moves for Gigantamax Venusaur as Vine Whip (fast) + G-Max Vine Lash (charged) for maximum DPS/TDO in many rotations. It can roll other Grass/Poison charged moves (e.g., Frenzy Plant in theoretical sets), but G-Max Vine Lash is the signature effect to watch for. Sunny weather boosts Grass moves; be careful with weather that amplifies its output.

Moves & Threats

Cobalion’s common fast moves are Metal Claw and Double Kick. Charged moves include Iron Head, Close Combat, and Stone Edge.

  • Iron Head hits Fairy/Rock hard but is resisted by Fire and Steel.
  • Close Combat punishes Rock/Dark counters—favour Fire/Ghost here.
  • Stone Edge threatens Fire/Flying—avoid Charizard if it’s present.

CP & IV (Catch)

  • Perfect (L20): 1727 CP
  • Weather Boost (L25): 2159 CP
  • Boosted by: Snow & Wind

Use Pinap on poor IVs; switch to Golden Razz on 96%+ or Shiny.

Weather Effects

  • Rain: Buffs Water—watch for big Hydro Pump; also boosts Electric damage.
  • Cloudy: Buffs your Fighting attackers.
  • Wind: Boosts the caught Gyarados (Water/Flying) and Dragon moves.

    • The Pokémon you catch is regular Gyarados; weather boost depends on its Water/Flying typing.

    Practical battle tips

    Battle Tips

    • Prioritize Electric teams; swap to Grass when Rain powers up both sides.
    • Run a matching Mega for teamwide damage bonuses and extra candies.
    • Charm/Fairy options are safe when Crunch is common and Outrage is absent.
    • Dodge one nuke (Hydro Pump/Outrage) to keep shadows alive and sustain DPS.

    FAQ

    Can Zapdos be shiny in Pokémon GO?

    Yes! Shiny Zapdos is available in Pokémon GO. It has more orange-toned beak and legs.

    What are best moveset of Zapdos?

    Best moveset of Zapdos are Charge Beam which does damage of 7 under Fast Attack and Zap Cannon of 140 under Charged Attack.

    Are Shadow Pokémon better?

    Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

    What is the best weather to raid Zapdos?

    Snow weather to boosts Ice type moves, Partly Cloudy weather to boosts Rock type moves
