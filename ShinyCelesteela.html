Why Celesteela Is a Long and Defensive Raid

Celesteela is not especially threatening in terms of raw damage, but its incredible bulk makes the fight much longer than most Ultra Beast raids. The biggest challenge is maintaining consistent DPS over time rather than surviving burst damage.
