Why Guzzlord Looks Scarier Than It Really Is

Guzzlord has enormous HP, but its Defense is extremely low for a Tier 5 raid boss. This means that although the HP bar looks intimidating, it actually goes down very quickly with proper Fairy-type counters.

Weather Effects

  • Cloudy: Boosts Fairy-type moves (BEST weather)
  • Windy: Boosts Dragon-type moves
  • Fog: Boosts Dark-type moves

In Cloudy weather, Fairy attackers become extremely strong and Guzzlord will faint much faster. In Fog or Windy weather, Guzzlord becomes more dangerous but still very manageable.
