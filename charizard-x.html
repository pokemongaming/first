What is Charizard X?

Charizard X is a Fire/Dragon type in 5 Star Mega Raid Egg known for high CP and strong resistances. In this guide, we explore how to get Mega Charizard X, its competitive strengths, top counters.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can Mega Charizard X be shiny in Pokémon GO?

Not currently. Shiny Diancie has not yet been released in raids or events.

What are best moveset of Charizard X?

Best movset of Charizard X are Fire Spin which does damage of 13 under Fast Attack and Overheat of 160 under Charged Attack.

Are Shadow Pokémon better?

Yes—Shadow forms have +20% attack, making them excellent choices if available.

What is the best weather to raid Mega Charizard X?

Partly Cloudy weather to boosts Rock type counters, Windy weather to boosts Dragon type counters, Clear weather to boosts Ground type counters.
